# FIVE TYPES OF FASTS

Absolute Fast - No food or water.

Radical Fast -- No food, only water.

Partial or Daniel Fast -- You may consume fruits, vegetables, whole grains, nuts and seeds. Drink only water.

Intimate Fast - This type of fast may be entered

into by couples who decide to set aside the pleasures of the flesh to seek the Lord for a specific time period.

Media Fast - This type of fast refrains from any type of media exposure for a certain time period.
