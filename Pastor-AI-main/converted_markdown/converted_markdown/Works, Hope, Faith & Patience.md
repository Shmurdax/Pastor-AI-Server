## II. HOPE!

Hebrews 6:11 "And we desire that every one of you do shew the same diligence to the full assurance of hope unto the end: - (18)That by two immutable things, in which it was impossible for God to lie, we might have a strong consolation, who have fled for refuge to lay hold upon the hope set before us: - (19)Which hope we have as an anchor of the soul, both sure and stedfast, and which entereth into that within the veil;"

As I preach over the next couple of weeks I will use the words "HOPE" and "CONFIDENCE" interchangeably, their meanings are exactly the same.

The word "HOPE" comes from the Greek word Elpis, and means "CONFIDENCE". There are four areas of "HOPE" I want to talk about:

- First, "CONFIDENCE" that the Blood of Jesus is enough to cover all my sin's, past, present and future.

 Second, "HOPE" that is capable of anchoring my soul (Mind, will, and emotion.) in the midst of the storms of life!

 Third, "CONFIDENCE" in His ability and willingness to meet all my needs!

 Fourth, "HOPE" in His eminent return!

A. "CONFIDENCE" that the Blood of Jesus is enough to cover all my sin's, past, present and future. Mankind has been faced with the problem of sin since Adam and the garden. The thing that makes this sin problem so serious is that it not only breaks communion between God and man, but also breaks relationship! To be separated from God, is to be eternally lost! To be lost, is to be without "HOPE"! To be without "HOPE", is to become miserable and self destructive! 1 Cor. 15:19 "If in this life only we have hope in Christ, we are of all men most miserable."

A lack of "HOPE" is the reason for most of this worlds problems. When a person loses "HOPE" they lose their ability to reason or to care! Sin will drive you to a point of "HOPELESSNESS", thus causing you to do things you never thought you would do. As this begins to happen the old account is steadily growing; the more you sin, the less you care, the more "HOPELESS" you feel! This is what I would call a vicious cycle that drives you lower and lower until you despair of life itself.

What can raise you from this pit of despair and cause you to begin to "HOPE" again? The old song say's "What can wash away my sin? Nothing but the Blood of Jesus! What can make me whole again? Nothing but the Blood of Jesus!"... Zech. 13:1 "In that day there shall be a fountain opened to the house of David and to the inhabitants of Jerusalem for sin and for uncleanness." Romans 5:8-11 "But God demonstrates his own love for us in this: While we were still sinners, Christ died for us. Since we have now been justified by his blood, how much more shall we be saved from God\'s wrath through him! For if, when we were God\'s enemies, we were reconciled to him through the death of his Son, how much more, having been reconciled, shall we be saved through his life! Not only is this so, but we also rejoice in God through our Lord Jesus Christ, through whom we have now received reconciliation." 1 Peter 1:18-19 "For you know that it was not with perishable things such as silver or gold that you were redeemed from the empty way of life handed down to you from your forefathers, but with the precious blood of Christ, a lamb without blemish or defect."

One of the greatest deterrents to the restoration of our "HOPE" through the Blood of Jesus is our inability to forgive ourselves! Romans 8:1-2 "There is therefore now no condemnation to them which are in Christ Jesus, who walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit. For the law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus hath made me free from the law of sin and death." The law of sin and death is a law of "HOPELESSNESS"!

There are two reasons the enemy puts so much effort into tearing down our "CONFIDENCE" with regard to the forgiveness we have received through the Blood of Jesus:

- He knows God has forgiven us, and no accusation he brings before God with regard to our past life will work! Ephes. 2:12-16 "Remember that at that time you were separate from Christ, excluded from citizenship in Israel and foreigners to the covenants of the promise, without hope and without God in the world. But now in Christ Jesus you who once were far away have been brought near through the blood of Christ.For he himself is our peace, who has made the two one and has destroyed the barrier, the dividing wall of hostility, by abolishing in his flesh the law with its commandments and regulations. His purpose was to create in himself one new man out of the two, thus making peace, and in this one body to reconcile both of them to God through the cross, by which he put to death their hostility."

 He knows that if he can destroy "HOPE" and "CONFIDENCE" in our own standing and relationship with God, we will be no threat to him! Hebrews 10:35 "So do not throw away your confidence; it will be richly rewarded." I grew up under the assumption that if you committed one sin you were a backslider and on your way to hell! I have since discovered that this theology was a trick of the enemy to badger and destroy our "CONFIDENCE", thus rendering us ineffective in the work of the Lord! 1 John 2:1 "My little children, these things write I unto you, that ye sin not. And if any man sin, we have an advocate with the Father, Jesus Christ the righteous:" 1 John 1:7 "But if we walk in the light, as he is in the light, we have fellowship one with another, and the blood of Jesus Christ his Son cleanseth us from all sin."

.

Here is a life changing truth: I am as clean and righteous as the Blood of Jesus can make me, and am entitled to all the benefits and blessings of a child of God!!!

B. "HOPE" that is capable of anchoring my soul in the midst of the STORMS of life! Today there are two things I want us to look at; 1) Our Souls 2) Storms and Anchors.

1) OUR SOULS: Just as God is a triune being, Father, Son and Holy Spirit, man is made of three parts as well, body, SOUL and spirit. Your body is the tent you live in. It is the temporary part of man.

Does it seem strange to you that we spend more time, effort and resources on the one part of us that is temporary?

Your spirit is the part of you that is capable of knowing God. This is the part of you that got saved at conversion. The spirit is eternal and will live on in eternity somewhere! You have the right to choose, smoking or non smoking!

Your SOUL is your mind, will and emotions - Think about where the governmental seat of those three entities is located - It's all in your head! I turned 42 this past week, I received several cards one of which said, "Another year older? Remember, age is mostly in your head... That's where you'll find the gray hair, the loss of memory, the false teeth, the loss of memory, the hearing aid, the loss of memory, the failing eyesight, the loss of memory, HAPPY BIRTHDAY! PS... Did I mention the loss of memory?"

When we face trials and temptations it will almost always be in our soulish nature. The battleground is the mind or SOUL! This battle is the flesh against the spirit!

2) STORMS: Webster has the following to say about a STORM:

A disturbance in the atmosphere in which there is a strong wind usually accompanied by rain, thunder and lightning. To blow violently! Before you were saved you were living on the devil's turf, doing all the things he could possibly convince you to do. Once you got saved the thing that changed was the fact that you intend to live differently, and God has promised to always be with you!

God never promised to change your culture or environment, only that he would always be there. He never promised a life without STORMS! I've got a friend who is a concrete contractor and he makes a threefold guarantee; the concrete will dry, when it does it will get hard, and it will crack! My guarantee to you this morning, whether you are saved or lost is this, you WILL have STORMS!

Right now, the enemy is drawing up a plan for a STORM, or series of STORMS, tailor made to bring about your destruction! You are on his turf - he is the god of this world! It may very well appear that he is going to destroy you with his STORM! So what is one to do? I ANCHOR my SOUL with HOPE! Where do I get my HOPE even in difficult times? The Word Of God! 2 Cor. 10:3-5 "For though we live in the world, we do not wage war as the world does. The weapons we fight with are not the weapons of the world. On the contrary, they have divine power to demolish strongholds. We demolish arguments and every pretension that sets itself up against the knowledge of God, and we take captive every thought to make it obedient to Christ." KJV "The weapons of our warfare are not carnal..."

A strong emotional outburst. To rage! The most effective weapon the enemy has, is his ability to talk to us! If you will allow him to do so he will verbally abuse you!!! He is an accuser of the brethren! (Story of the three pigs!)

The enemy may instigate, agitate, aggravate, humiliate, huff, puff, harass and threaten, but the one thing he can never do without your cooperation is destroy you! He will tell you that you are not good enough - He will tell you that you are never going to amount to a hill of beans - He will tell you that your children are going to be alcoholics and drug addicts - He will tell you that your marriage is going to end in divorce - He will tell you that you are going to go bankrupt - So what are you to do? ANCHOR your SOUL with HOPE! 1 Cor. 10:13 "No temptation has seized you except what is common to man. And God is faithful; he will not let you be tempted beyond what you can bear. But when you are tempted, he will also provide a way out so that you can stand up under it."
