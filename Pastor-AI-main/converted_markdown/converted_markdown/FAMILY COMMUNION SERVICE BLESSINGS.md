# FAMILY COMMUNION SERVICE

BLESSINGS

(Place on screen one at a time)

INCREASE

I speak this blessing over you -- "Be fruitful and increase in the earth." As God's promise to Abraham was for His seed to increase in the earth, I speak the blessing of increase over you. May everything you set your hand to do be blessed with supernatural increase.

COMPOSURE

In a world of chaos, I speak over you a calm demeanor, undisturbed composure, and obedient behavior. We will follow the Lord, and great will be our peace. The Word of the Lord "Will be a lamp unto our feet and a light unto our path." I declare that "You will perfect everything that concerns this family."

PROSPERITY

I declare a spirit of prosperity over your life. I declare this over:

- Your spiritual walk...

- Your health...

- Your relationships...

- Your career...

- Your finances...

I speak the blessing of Abraham over you, that "Those who bless you will be blessed, and those who curse you will be cursed."

I declare that you will have good success in all aspects of your life. You will be the Joseph in your culture -- Things will improve in all aspects, wherever you go, because you will walk in a spirit of prosperity all the days of your life.

LONG LIFE

I declare that you will live long upon the earth and that your days will be filled with Kingdom purpose. "No plague shall come near where you live, and no evil shall befall you." As the Children of Israel survived the wilderness, and there was not one sick among them when they entered the Promised Land, so I declare not only long life over you, but that all of your day's will be lived out in divine health.

FEARLESSNESS

In a world paralyzed by fear I declare courage, faith, and fearlessness over you. The word say's "You have not been given a spirit of bondage whereby you live in fear. Therefore, you are to live in the power of God, the love of God, and have soundness of mind." You are to live your life in faith rather than fear. I declare that as you do this, you will accomplish everything the Father has planned for you from before the foundation of the world.

ENDURANCE

In a world of instant everything, I declare that you will have the endurance that causes you to run this race with patience. I declare that you will not:

- Quit...

- Give up...

- Throw in the towel...

- Allow your enemies to win...

You have been sent into this earth on purpose, for a purpose. I declare that you will finish the purpose that is your life assignment. The Word says "Unto those who endure until the end, the same shall be saved." I declare that everything that you start, you will finish in Jesus' name!
