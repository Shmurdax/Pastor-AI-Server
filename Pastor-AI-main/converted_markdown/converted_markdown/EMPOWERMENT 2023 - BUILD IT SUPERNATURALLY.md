A SUPERNATURAL ORDER OF MINISTRY INVOLVES MIRACLES, SIGNS, AND WONDERS

The proof that we are who we say we are, and the proof that our message is true is miracles, signs, and wonders. "These signs SHALL follow those who believe..."

These three words, "Miracles, Signs and Wonders" are frequently found together in the New Testament in one order or another.

1) The Greek word translated as "miracles" is (δύναμις) dunamis, and it means "a deed manifesting great power with the implication of some supernatural force." Oftentimes we find it translated in the New Testament as "power, mighty works, and strength." The word dunamis has been described as "Explosions of Almightines."

2) The Greek word translated as "sign" is (σημεῖον) semeion, and it means "a sign, a mark; by which a person or thing is distinguished from others and is known; an event which is regarded as having some special meaning; not simply a miraculous event but something which points to a reality of even greater significance." (HAVE SOMEONE TO PUT UP "I DID THIS -- GOD" YARD SIGNS AROUND THE STAGE)

3) The Greek word translated as "wonder" is (τέρας) teras, and it means "a marvel." It speaks to the wonder or awe that is produced in those who witness the miracle, or the dunamis.

Therefore:

- A miracle is the supernatural power of God being manifest in the natural realm...

- A sign points up the fact that no one but God could have done such a thing... (HAVE EVERYONE TO STAND THAT HAS HAD AN UNMISTAKEABLE MIRACLE -- DISTRIBUTE SIGNS)

- A wonder is the awe that is produced in the hearts of the people as they witness the miracle...

All three together, miracles, signs, and wonders give a complete revelation of the Supernatural power of God and are utilized in turning our hearts, and the hearts of others to God. (Over one million Muslims a month are turning to God -- Most of them come to God as a result of a Miracle)
