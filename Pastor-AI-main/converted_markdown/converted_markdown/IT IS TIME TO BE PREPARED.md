# IT IS TIME TO BE PREPARED

INTRO: (verywellhealth.com)

What is a pandemic? When a contagious disease is present over a large portion of the earth -- At least two or more continents. This constitutes a pandemic. What happens during a pandemic?

- Supply chains slow down... (Toilet paper -- The roll is being called up yonder)

- Economics weaken... (Stock market plunges -- Layoffs -- Business closings)

- Schools and businesses close... (Parents become teachers and school administrators -- -- What I never liked about school was the principle of the thing)

- Travel restrictions become necessary... (Our world gets exponentially smaller)

- Misinformation creartes confusion and panic... (In times like these be VERY careful who you listen to -- Walk Through The Word)

With the onset of a pandemic people understandably become anxious and afraid. Deuteronomy 3:18 "He will never leave you or forsake you. Do not be afraid; do not be discouraged." Romans 8:28 "And we know that in all things God works for the good of those who love Him, who have been called according to His purpose." Isaiah 43:1 "Don't fear, for I have redeemed you; I have called you by name; you are mine."

The phrase "Fear not" is used at least 80 times in the Bible, most likely because the Lord knows the enemy uses fear to:

- Decrease hope...

- Limit our faith...

- Diminish our expectation for victory...

What do we do to prepare for the onslaught of fear? 1 John 4:18 "Perfect love casts our ALL fear."

- God is love, perfect love...

- The closer we come to Him the less power fear has to operate in our lives...

- Abiding in His love shelters us from fear... Psalm 91 "He who dwells in the secret place of the Most High shall abide under the shadow of the Almighty."

Psalm 18:2 "The Lord is my rock, my fortress and deliverer."

Even though we battle anxiety there are actions that we must take to be prepared. Just like one would prepare for a hurricane or ice storm, we can prepare for a pandemic as well.

I want to bring a few things to our attention that will prepare us for what we are facing now, and could face in the future:

- Fight...
