# IT IS TIME TO DECLARE - NO MORE LACK

INTRO: Job 22:28 "You will also decide and decree a thing, and it will be established for you; And the light \of God's favor\ will shine upon your ways." The word "Established" here means, "To arise and come onto the stage of action."

The words of Kingdom people change things in the atmosphere. Our words move things from the heavenly realm to the earthly realm. We need to stop seeing our words as conversation and start viewing them as legislation!

If the words we speak line up with the Word and will of God, they become legislation for the good of the Kingdom and for devastation to the kingdom of darkness.

Psalm 34:10 say's, (AMP) "But they who seek (inquire of and require) the Lord \by right of their need and on the authority of His Word\, none of them shall lack any beneficial thing."

Kingdom people:

- Inquire of the Lord... We are to search His Word for Kingdom benefits -- Many people have benefits they never access -- As a result the benefit is not a benefit -- is it? What are His benefits toward us? Psalm 103:1-5 (NKJV) "Bless the Lord, O my soul; And all that is within me, bless His holy name! ^2 ^Bless the Lord, O my soul, And forget not all His benefits: ^3 ^Who forgives all your iniquities, Who heals all your diseases, ^4 ^Who redeems your life from destruction, Who crowns you with lovingkindness and tender mercies, ^5 ^Who satisfies your mouth with good things, So that your youth is renewed like the eagle's." According to this Scripture we can declare: Forgiveness for all our sins -- Healing for all our diseases -- Deliverance for anything that would attempt to destroy anything that belongs to us -- More than enough lovingkindness and tender mercies "Surely goodness and mercy shall follow us all the day's of our lives." Good things are guaranteed in every direction we turn -- Youthful energy will become our trademark

- Require of the Lord... Stand on the promises -- Standing on His promises requires us to declare and decree Kingdom principles over our lives, families, careers, and ministries -- Someone needs to curse lack and declare a spirit of plenty -- He is El Shaddi not El Getby!

- Declare that we will not lack any beneficial thing... (John 10:10 "... have life and that MORE ABUNDANTLY."

Kingdom people have the authority to call forth riches hidden from secret places, to fulfill the purposes of God in the earth. Isaiah 45:3 (NKJV) "I will give you hidden treasures, riches stored in secret places, so that you may know that I am the LORD, the God of Israel, who summons you by name." If we are in fact Kingdom people, we need to start calling hidden riches forth from secret places!

Desmond Tutu said, "People often speak of God being even-handed. God is not even-handed. God is biased in favor of His people."

Everything I know about God and His Word points me in the direction of believing He wants us to live beyond the power of Lack.

Four principles, that if followed, will most certainly break the back of the power of lack!

ATTITUDE

The ability to transition from poverty to prosperity is at least in part driven by our attitude toward money and things.

George Brazell in his book, "This is Stewardship" makes the following statement: "Stewardship is the sum total of man\'s attitude and reaction toward the Divine Creator and His creation. God is the owner of all creation, and man is the responsible servant entrusted with, and commissioned to, rightly handle for God His program on earth." Our attitudes toward money and things, regulate whether we are going to live our lives in poverty or prosperity.

If we are going to DECLARE NO MORE LACK, and make it stick, we must change our attitude toward things! Everything I have belongs to God, therefore He can have it when He wants it, and I don't have to worry about it, because there is more where that came from!

OBEDIENCE

DECLARING NO MORE LACK while refusing to do what is Biblical with our finances is nothing short of a fruitless exercise. Leviticus 27:30-34 (NLT) "A tenth of the produce of the land, whether grain or fruit, belongs to the Lord and must be set apart to him as holy. 31 If you want to redeem the Lord's tenth of the fruit or grain, you must pay its value, plus 20 percent. 32 The Lord also owns every tenth animal counted off from your herds and flocks. They are set apart to him as holy. 33 The tenth animal must not be selected on the basis of whether it is good or bad, and no substitutions will be allowed. If any exchange is in fact made, then both the original animal and the substituted one will be considered holy and cannot be redeemed." 34 These are the commands that the Lord gave to the Israelites through Moses on Mount Sinai." Malachi 3:10 (KJV) "Bring all the tithes into the storehouse..."

Tithing is not a suggestion for a Christian; it is a commandment to honor God with our finances. This commandment is not because He needs our money, it is because we need to give! There is something about giving and generosity that opens up the heavens.

We are either obedient or disobedient! Isaiah 1:19-20 (NKJV) "If you are willing and obedient, You shall eat the good of the land; 20 But if you refuse and rebel, You shall be devoured by the sword"; For the mouth of the Lord has spoken."

TRUST

Tithing does not require trust and therefore does not necessarily bring with it the blessing of multiplication. First-fruit requires trust and enacts the blessing of multiplication upon our resources. If I give the first of what I have believing for at least nine more - That requires trust and trust is a big deal to God. Proverbs 3:5-6 (NKJV) "Trust in the Lord with all your heart, And lean not on your own understanding;^6 ^In all your ways acknowledge Him, And He shall direct your paths." Let me paraphrase this -- "Be bold, confident and careless when it comes to the things of the Lord by refusing to lean on your own understanding. Acknowledge God first with the way you live your life and He will make the traveling path of your troop or caravan, pleasant, prosperous, and good." (ILLUSTRATION WITH 2 \$100 BILLS -- HAVE EVERYONE TO HOLD UP A \$5 BILL)

We must be willing to give up what we have in our hand to get what He has in His!

GENEROSITY

If we are going to DECLARE NO MORE LACK, we must be ready to enact the principle of generosity.

A lot of people want to be compliant, but if we want to truly walk in - NO MORE LACK - We must become one with generosity, in every area of our lives.

Generosity is the defining test of all would-be-stewards and is essential in breaking the power of lack. Matthew 25:29 (NLT) "To those who use well what they are given, even more will be given, and they will have an abundance. But from those who are unfaithful, even what little they have will be taken away." (BRING 3 PEOPLE UP TO DO TRIP ILLUSTRATION)

If we cannot pass the generosity test with a good attitude, we can forget being in possession of the many other blessings of God. Proverbs 11:24 (NKJV) "There is one who scatters, yet increases more; And there is one who withholds more than is right, But it leads to poverty." My personal paraphrase: "The path of the generous leads to prosperity; The path of the stingy leads to poverty."

Generosity is the first step toward multiplication. The Bible is full of instances of the miracle of multiplication:

- The strength of Israelite soldiers was multiplied on many occasions when they were hopelessly outnumbered...

- The meal and oil of the widow and her son was multiplied...

- The multiplication of a little boy's lunch...

Multiplication is the ground that grows the ability to DECLARE NO MORE LACK!
