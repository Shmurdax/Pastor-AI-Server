DOMINION COMES FROM SUBMISSION 
TO SPIRITUAL AUTHORITY 

All of us were born with a natural desire to 
conquer and rule over things, property, and even 
people. 
Think of Adam… 
God placed Adam under His authority and gave 
him one commandment: "Do not eat of the tree 
in the midst of the Garden." Then, God gave 
Adam spiritual authority to rule over all created 
things. Three things happened here: 
• God delegated authority to mankind… 
• Mankind received authority from God… 
• The connection between the Kingdom of 
God and the Kingdom of man is a thing 
called obedience… 
As long as Adam remained under spiritual 
authority, he had governmental authority 
over everything else. 
Adam surrendered his right to have dominion 
(Rule), when he stepped out from under spiritual 
authority and disobeyed the Lord. At this point 

he lost his governmental authority over created 
things. 
God will only allow our dominion to go as far 
as our submission will take us. 
When Adam fell, the desire to rule did not leave 
him, but the ability to rule was gone. In fact, 
from that very time his entire life was an 
attempt to regain his original dominion, which 
couldn’t be completely retrieved. The Kingdom 
life is one of attempting to reach the level of 
submission that allows us to have dominion. 
“He who is greatest in the Kingdom must be 
servant to all.” 
Even Jesus submitted Himself to the authority 
of man. At twelve years old Jesus had a conflict 
with His parents over remaining in the Temple 
when He was expected to return home with 
them. Luke 2:51-52 (NKJV) “Then He went 
down with them and came to Nazareth, and 
was subject to them, but His mother kept all 
these things in her heart.” He obeyed His 

parents and returned to Nazareth with them at 
their direction. Listen to the results… (Vs. 52) 
“And Jesus increased in wisdom and stature, 
and in favor with God and man.”

POWER GRABBING IS A PARTNERSHIP 
WITH SATAN 
Since the fall of Adam, the acquisition of 
authority is an attempt to take power, rather 
than waiting for it to be given. When one takes 
power rather than it being given, the alliance of 
such an activity is from Satan, rather than from 
God. 
A person who awaits the moment that authority 
is conferred upon them operates in the spirit of 

Christ, a person who attempts to seize control is 
in alliance with the devil. 
The TAKING of authority is a principle that 
was enacted by Satan himself, and is directly 
opposed to how we are to acquire and assume 
authority in the Kingdom of God. 
Please remember, if we stand against authority, 
God will stand against us. If we want to walk 
in authority, influence and power we must 
learn to stand under authority rather than 
standing against authority. 
Nadab and Abihu are great examples of 
taking authority rather that awaiting 
authority to be conferred: (PLACE ON 
SCREEN ONE AT A TIME) 
• Nadab and Abihu were the sons of 
Aaron… (Aaron was the High Priest) 
• They offered an unauthorized sacrifice 
upon the altar… (They had watched their 
father offer such offerings hundreds of times 
– They were familiar) 

• They believed they were as capable of 
offering sacrifices as was their father… 
(But they were not authorized to do so) 
• Only Aaron had been authorized by God 
to offer such a sacrifice… (The authority to 
do so could not come from Aaron or his 
sons – Such authorization was the sole 
responsibility of the Lord) 
Leviticus 10:1-2 (NASB) “Now Nadab and 
Abihu, the sons of Aaron, took their respective
