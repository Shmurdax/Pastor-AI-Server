# I WILL

TEXT: PSALM 91:15-15 (NKJV) ""Because he has set his love upon Me, therefore I will deliver him; I will set him on high, because he has known My name.

15 He shall call upon Me, and I will answer him; I will be with him in trouble;

I will deliver him and honor him."

"Because he (The believer) hath set his love upon me (God), therefore (For that reason) I WILL..." (Psalm 91:14--15)

The key to what I am going to speak on tonight is for us to SET our love on God:

- Set a thermostat...

- Set the cruise control...

- Set the alarm on your house...

- Set the time on the microwave...

1. I WILL deliver him... (He hath delivered -- He doth deliver -- He will yet deliver)

2. I WILL set him on high... (Make me the head & not the tail -- Above & not beneath -- The lender & not the borrower)

3. Call on me I WILL Answer him... (Never silent -- Never inactive -- Never disconnected)

4. I WILL be with him in trouble... (Trouble means God is there -- Tore up from the floor up -- Messed up from the neck up -- Beat up from the feet up - Trouble usually indicates haters -- Table before me in the presence of my enemies -- Shake off the haters -- I refuse to allow the dissenters to be the deciders)

5. I WILL honor him... (Honor = favor -- Favor ain't fair -- A curse is divine forces working against you -- Favor is more than finding a great parking place - Favor is divine forces working for you -- You can't lose with the stuff I use -- Devil bring it)

CONCLUSION:

Do you need God to do any of these things for you? According to His Word he WILL do them, the condition is that you must set your love upon Him.
