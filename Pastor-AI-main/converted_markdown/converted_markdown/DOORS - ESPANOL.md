# DOORS

TEXT: Revelation 3:20 "Behold I stand at the door and knock. If any man will hear my voice and open the door, I will come in and sup with him, and he with me."

INTRO: 247 Bible verses mention doors ! Worldwide, doors are important. No matter where you go in the world, all buildings have doors, and no one must explain what they are.

Doors:

- Allow access...

- Allow for privacy...

- Deny access...

- Allow for safety...

- Protect from the elements...

Literally, everywhere we go, we deal with doors:

- Some are open...

- Some are closed...

- Some are locked...

- Some are unlocked...

- Some take effort to open...

- Some open automatically...

- Some are single doors...

- Some are double doors...

(My pet peeve about doors -- "Please use other door!")

I felt led to announce the opening of four specific doors over this house...

THE DOOR OF SALVATION

Revelation 3:20 "Behold I stand at the door and knock..." This is the greatest altar call ever! I am the altar call guy!

I am believing for the salvation of your family and friends... Anyone want to confess it?

- Mothers & Fathers...

- Sons and daughters...

- Brothers and sisters...

- Family and friends...

Family circle unbroken... Satan will not drag one of my offspring off to Hell!

THE DOOR OF RECOVERED RELATIONSHIPS

Nothing is worth a broken relationship...

Some of you have struggled with a broken relationship with:

- A spouse...

- A parent...

- A child...

- A sibling...

- An authority figure...

- Relationships left behind in your former country...

I open a door of recovered relationships over everyone under the sound of my voice this morning.

THE DOOR OF HEALING

Jesus didn't take stripes on His back so we could keep on being sick... He took stripes on His back so we could be healed...

"By whose stripes we were healed..." "By whose stripes we are healed..."

Why doesn't everyone get healed? Not God's fault...

Maybe if we approached healing the way we do salvation we would get healed! (Never doubted that if we came to Him, He would save us)

I am believing that someone in this room is about to come face to face with the goodness of God and experience healing and restoration!

THE DOOR OF FINANCIAL BLESSING

Some folks don't believe God wants them blessed...

Anyone in here hoping their children or grandchildren will live a life of poverty, and never know what it is to have more than enough?

Absolutely not!

We want to get them off our payroll -- Right?

"If you being evil know how to give good gifts to your children... HOW MUCH MORE..."

(How I respond to people who want me to pray for a financial need)

If you are not a tither, the promise of the goodness of His financial resources don't apply to you!

You have yourself under a curse! I can't pray this curse off you - You cannot pray your way out you have to obey your way out!

We serve a "How much more God!"

The Scripture says, "With God, NOTHING is impossible..." When we are in need -- It is IMPOSSIBLE for God to do nothing! (My experience praying for the devourer to be rebuked...)

CONCLUSION:

I have come to:

- Open the door of Salvation over this church and over your family...

- Open the door of the recovering of broken relationships over this church, and over each of you...

- Open the door of the healing of sickness and disease over this church, and over each of you...

- Open the door of financial blessing over this this church and over each of you...
