# HIS UNFORGETTABLE DEEDS

TEXT: Psalm 9:1-11 (NLT) "I will thank you, LORD, with all my heart; I will tell of all the marvelous things you have done. 2 I will be filled with joy because of you. I will sing praises to your name, O Most High. 3 My enemies turn away in retreat; they are overthrown and destroyed before you. 4 For you have judged in my favor; from your throne, you have judged with fairness. 5 You have rebuked the nations and destroyed the wicked; you have wiped out their names forever. 6 My enemies have met their doom; their cities are perpetual ruins. Even the memory of their uprooted cities is lost. 7 But the LORD reigns forever, executing judgment from his throne. 8 He will judge the world with justice and rule the nations with fairness. 9 The LORD is a shelter for the oppressed, a refuge in times of trouble. 10 Those who know your name trust in you, for you, O LORD, have never abandoned anyone who searches for you. 11 Sing praises to the LORD who reigns in Jerusalem. Tell the world about his unforgettable deeds."

INTRO: The narrative in Psalm 9 is David writing a note to his Music Director enumerating his passion for God and his knowledge about the character of God.

His passion for God:

- I will thank the Lord with my whole heart...

- I will tell of the marvelous things you have done ...

- I am filled with joy because of you...

- I will sing praise to your name O Most High...

- Those who dwell in Zion sing praise to the Lord...

The Character of God:

- Enemies are overthrown by you, they have retreated...

- You judge in fairness...

- Your judgment is in our favor...

- You judge the nations...

- You destroy the wicked...

- When you judged cities, their memories perished...

- You reign forever, executing judgment from your throne...

- You judge the world with justice and rule the nations with fairness...

- You are a shelter for the oppressed...

- You are a refuge in the time of trouble...

- You can be trusted because you never abandon anyone...

Following these declarations the Psalmist say's "Sing praises to the LORD who reigns in Jerusalem. Tell the world about his unforgettable deeds."

I would like to take the next few minutes to tell you about His unforgettable deeds:

- God made the world and everything that it encompasses in six day's...

- God saved the world from extinction with a preacher of righteousness and a boat full of animals...

- God delivered Abraham from a life of pagan worship and used his family line to bring salvation to the human race...

- God used a fugitive from justice to delivered the Children of Israel from Egyptian bondage...

- With nothing more than a shout of praise, God flattened the fortified walls of a major city...

- God provided food and water to an entire nation for more than forty years... (God provided for them for 40 years or 14,600 days! This required - 58,400,000 tons of food at a cost of \$87,600,000,000.00 -160,600,000,000 gallons of water were supplied at a cost of \$876,000,000.00 - Total cost of food and water would be \$88,476,000,000.00 - Transportation for food and water would require a freight train 26,309,200 miles long and would reach around the world 106 times!)

- In response to the prayer of a beleaguered prophet God sent fire on Carmel's altar and gave rain to a parched land...

- God robbed the fire of its heat so He could prove His point to a wicked King and deliver three obedient souls...

- God locked the jaws of hungry lions to deliver one preacher from the venom of misguided men...

I stand today to declare His unforgettable deeds!
