# THE MAN IN THE RED BANDANA

DISCUSSION QUESTIONS

1\) DO YOU REMEMBER WHAT YOU WERE DOING WHEN THE WORLD TRADE CENTER WAS
DESTROYED? (Give some time for discussion but do not take too much time
here)

2\) WHAT WERE YOUR FIRST THOUGHTS? FIRST REACTIONS? (Give some time for
discussion but move along pretty quickly)

3\) WHAT WAS THE YOUNG MAN'S NAME WHO WAS WEARING THE RED BANDANA?
(Welles Crowther)

4\) WAS WELLES CROWTHER A FIRST RESPONDER OR VOLUNTEER? (A volunteer)

5\) HOW DOES OUR BEING WILLING TO VOLUNTEER INMPACT THE LIVES OF OTHERS?
(When we touch the lives of others with the love of Christ we leave His
fingerprints on them)

6\) WELLES CROWTHER WAS OBVIOUSLY DRIVEN BY A SENSE OF URGENCY, WHY
SHOULD WE FEEL THE SAME URGENCY FOR REACHING AND DISCIPLING PEOPLE?
(Because the heart of God is to make an impact upon them and we are
displaying His heart to them)

7\) WHAT IS THE HIGHEST ORDER AND PRIVILEDGE WE ARE GRANTED BY GOD?
(Rescuing, discipling, empowering and serving others)

8\) A LIFE OF ACTION PRODUCES WHAT? (A life of achievement -- Read Jude
1:22-23)

9\) WHO ARE YOU PERSONALLY INVOLVED WITH FOR THE PURPOSE OF RESCUE?
(Encourage each person to pick a target individual that they are going
to attempt to bring with them to life group)

10\) IF WELLES CROWTHER GAVE HIS LIFE TO RESCUE TOTAL STRANGERS AND
JESUS GAVE HIS LIFE TO SAVE THE WORLD, WHAT SHOULD BE OUR ATTITUDE BE
TOWARD THOSE WHO DO NOT KNOW CHRIST? (A sense of urgency)

PRAY FOR THOSE INDIVIDUALS BY NAME WHO WILL BE THE TARGET FOR THE GROUP.
