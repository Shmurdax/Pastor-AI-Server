# TAKE THE PLUNGE TEST

AND ANSWER KEY

Mark 1:9-11, Mark 16:15-16, Colossians 2:12, Romans 6:3-11

1) Who is qualified to be baptized in water? Anyone who has been born
 again

2) How soon after conversion should a person be baptized in water? As
 soon as possible

3) Why should water baptism be a high priority to the new believer?
 Because the believer wants to be obedient to the directives of Jesus

4) May baptism be done privately? No

5) Why? Because it is a public testimony that a person has decided to
 follow Christ

6) Must a person be baptized by a professional minister? No (Phillip
 baptized the eunuch in the desert)

7) Is it necessary to be baptized in a church? No, a person can be
 baptized anywhere

8) Must a person be a member of a church to be baptized in water? No
 (The only requirement is to be born again by the blood of Jesus)

9) Does water baptism in any way save an individual? No

10) Must a person be baptized to go to Heaven? No (The thief on the
 cross)

11) Can a person be an obedient Christ follower without being baptized
 in water? No, this is an act of disobedience

12) Water baptism signifies entrance into what church? The invisible
 church

13) What happens to the believer who resists being baptized in water?
 Their Christian growth is stunted through disobedience

14) Water baptism is a gateway into what? Into the Church Jesus died to
 build

15) What are three things Jesus did, that water baptism points to? The
 death, burial and resurrection of Jesus

16) When a believer goes into the baptismal pool it signifies what? A
 real-life change has been made

17) When a believer is put under the water of baptism it signifies what?
 Death to the old life

18) When a believer comes up out of the water of baptism it signifies
 what? Walking in the new life

19) Does baptism signify an outward testimony of an inward work? Yes
