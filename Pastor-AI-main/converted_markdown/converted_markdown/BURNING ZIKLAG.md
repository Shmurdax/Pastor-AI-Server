# "BURNING ZIKLAG"

TEXT: I SAMUEL 27:1-7; I SAMUEL 30:1-8

INTRO: David has been anointed by Samuel to be king over Israel.

Saul's jealousy causes him to hate David, thus he pursues David to kill him. "Saul hath killed his thousands, David his ten thousands."

David no doubt becomes weary with all the pressures of being pursued and he begins to take things into his own hands and makes plans to dwell in the Land of the Philistines. I Samuel 24:1-6, 26:2-9 - David has opportunity to kill Saul but refuses to.

David goes to Achish King of Gath - Achish allows him to dwell in the Royal City.

I personally don't believe that it was within the will of God for David to be in the land of the Philistines in the first place. Commitment in the bad times as well as the good times. Saul couldn't have killed David if he had wanted to.

David finally speaks to Achish and asks him to give him a city!

I SAMUEL 27:5-7

5\) And David said unto Achish, If I have now found grace in thine eyes, let them give me a place in some town in the country, that I may dwell there; for why should thy servant dwell in the royal city with thee?

6\) Then Achish gave him Ziklag that day: wherefore Ziklag pertaineth unto the kings of Judah unto this day.

7\) And the time that David dwelt in the country of the Philistines was a full year and four months.

Let's look at the significance of Ziklag in our lives today!

## I. ZIKLAG!!!

A. ZIKLAG represents a hiding place or refuge other than God! Most of the time it represents the path of least resistance.

Notice if you will, that David didn't stay in Ziklag all the time. He would go out from time to time, on Saul's turf and when the pressure was on he would return to Ziklag! DAVID WAS EVEN WILLING TO HELP ACHISH FIGHT ISRAEL! (Before Ziklag, David was unwilling to kill Saul.)

Let's look at some other people in God's word who had their own personal Ziklag!
