## I. STRANGE FIRE -- THE FIRE OF SIN!

The wrong kind of fire is destructive. If the wrong fire is present, it could bring the house down to ashes! The wrong fire manifests itself through the works of the flesh.

How could someone risk it all with a fling, or risk stoning in the Old Testament? I'll tell you why, because there is a strange fire in the fireplace of the heart and it is just our human nature to gravitate to the fire!

In the Bible, those who flirted with strange fire were made aware of its devastating consequences:

- Nadab and Ahibu attempted to furnish their own fire in ministering to the Lord. Because they were using strange fire the fire of God came down and devoured them.

- David and Bathsheba -- When David saw her, a fire began to burn. That was the beginning of the end!

Fire does not extinguish itself as long as it has oxygen and fuel! If we continue to feed the fire of sin and give it opportunity in our life it will burn until we are destroyed.

Some who are in this room today are have been standing too close to the enemies fire! We cannot flirt with the fire and hope not to get burned.

There are four things that are never satisfied, and one of them is fire! Proverbs 30:15-16 (CEV) "Greed has twins, each named "Give me!" There are four things that are never satisfied: 16 The world of the dead and a childless wife, the thirsty earth and a flaming fire."

The fire of the world can never get enough. When the thrill is over there is an immediate need for more stimulation and gratification. Sin will take us farther than we intended to go; keep us longer than we intended to stay; and make us pay far more than we intended to pay! (Samson) Mark 8:36-37 (NKJV) "For what will it profit a man if he gains the whole world, and loses his own soul?37 "Or what will a man give in exchange for his soul?"

Unless we stay on fire with Holy Ghost enthusiasm we will be on fire with the passions of the world!

- Religion flew jetliners into the world trade center, the pentagon and into the ground on 9/11/01!

- Religion causes men, women and young people to strap a bomb to their body and detonate it at just the right time to take as many lives as possible.

- Religion caused hundreds to drink poison at the direction of Jim Jones in Jonestown!

- Dozens of people go to a fiery grave at the insistence of David Koresh in Waco, Texas.

What could these people have been thinking of? What were they looking for? They were searching for something to be passionate about! People are so desperate for a passionate cause they will follow an Osama Bin Lauden, Saddam Hussein, Jim Jones, or David Koresh!

The world is looking for the fire of God to put into the fireplace of their heart! They may not even know what they are searching for but until they discover God's fire everything else will leave them feeling empty. Some of the most vicious people on the face of the earth are religious people! Be careful about turning your back on religious folks, they will rip you to ribbons! (Story of woman who said she could not lay her tongue on the altar)

When the world comes to Christian Temple will they find the fire of God or will they find religious fire? Religious fire is more dangerous than the fire of the word because religious fire causes the individual to believe they are right when they are wrong. At least the world knows what they are doing is not right. There will be as many religious people in hell as there will be outright sinners!

The church has been neutered by the world! We should ask ourselves:

- Could it be that in an effort to polish our programs and sermons we have lost our passion?

- Could it be that we have learned how to appeal to the consumer mind-set, and lost our commitment for the lost?

- Could it be that in our pursuit for prominence we have watered down the Gospel to the point that it has become ineffective?

- Could it be that we have become so civic minded that we no longer make Christ the center piece?

- Could it be that our focus has become so misplaced that we no longer consider eternity?

I believe in Methodology, but I also believe the fire of God must be in our preaching, teaching, singing, altars as well as the pews! 2 Corinthians 4:3-4 (NKJV) "But if our gospel be hid, it is hid to them that are lost: 4 In whom the god of this world hath blinded the minds of them which believe not, lest the light of the glorious gospel of Christ, who is the image of God, should shine unto them."1 Corinthians 1:21 (KJV) "...It pleased God by the foolishness of preaching to save them that believe." We should make no apologies for our focus on preaching the cross. The preaching of the cross is a matter of life and death. I am not trying to get you to be Assembly of God; I'm trying to convince you to receive Jesus and the life He has planned for you.

- Patrick Henry did not use a skit on Liberty and death; he lifted his voice full of passion and put his life where his mouth was!

- Winston Churchill did not use a talk show dialogue on courage under fire to rally the troops in World War II; he lifted his own steadfast courage before the people!

- Martin Luther King in his march on Washington lifted his voice and put flesh and blood in his dreams! He did not use a mime; he laid himself in harm's way!

I am certainly not against any of these things but I want you to know that no self-absorbed whining, cystic support group will ever raise the Church of the living God out of its self-inflicted, lethargic, comatose, half frozen condition! It will take the wind and fire of the Holy Ghost to resurrect the Church of the living God, and cause them to go forth and once again conquer!

My prayer is "Would you come, O God of the fire and wind, until we are consumed once again with a passion to have fellowship with God, and to reach a world on a toboggan sled to hell!"

## III. THE FIRE OF GOD!

The fire of God manifests itself in four ways:

- Fire renovates - The earth and works therein shall be burned up -- out of this shall come a new heaven and a new earth. 2 Peter 3:10 (NKJV) "But the day of the Lord will come as a thief in the night, in which the heavens will pass away with a great noise, and the elements will melt with fervent heat; both the earth and the works that are in it will be burned up." Sodom & Gomorrah were destroyed by fire.

- Fire exposes - When burning off a forest or new ground, it is amazing how many rats and snakes will come running out of the fire! Acts 28:3 (NKJV) "But when Paul had gathered a bundle of sticks and laid them on the fire, a viper came out because of the heat, and fastened on his hand." (He was not the last preacher to get snake-bit while building a fire!) There is no substitute for the fire of God. The fire of God will convict us of our sin; if we do not turn our back on it He will expose it!

<!-- -->

- Fire purifies -- The refiner's fire burns until the dross has floated to the top and has been removed. The fire has not completed its purpose until the refiner can see his reflection in the silver! With His purifying fire He will burn out every impurity! Zechariah 13:9 (NKJV) "I will bring the one-third through the fire, Will refine them as silver is refined, And test them as gold is tested. They will call on My name, and I will answer them. I will say, 'This is My people'; And each one will say, 'The Lord is my God.'" Malachi 3:2-3 (NKJV) "But who can endure the day of His coming? And who can stand when He appears? For He is like a refiner's fire And like launderer's soap. 3 He will sit as a refiner and a purifier of silver; He will purify the sons of Levi, And purge them as gold and silver, That they may offer to the Lord An offering in righteousness."

- Fire empowers -- Think of the old steam driven trains. The fire and water combined to develop the steam. This furnished steam to blow the whistle, but more importantly, it enabled the loaded train to pull the hills. (Story of little train) So you see, the shout is good but the shout is only indicative of the power. The whistle should mean the train is coming because the same power that blew the whistle will pull the train.

This power has produced victorious Christians, propelled missionaries to dark continents, purified hearts in believers; sought out sinners and turned them into saints, healed the sick and caused them to shout and shine; sent martyrs singing to their death. Acts 1:8 (KJV) "But ye shall receive power, after that the Holy Ghost is come upon you: and ye shall be witnesses unto me both in Jerusalem, and in all Judaea, and in Samaria, and unto the uttermost part of the earth."

One day on the back side of the desert God wrapped Himself in a shrub and transformed a fugitive by the name of Moses into a mighty deliverer of his people.

On another occasion He took hold of a discouraged prophet by the name of Jeremiah. This prophet had become the object of a lot of misunderstanding and maltreatment. God became live fire in his bones and gave him a new lease on life as well as a fresh start. Jeremiah had been ready to join the superannuated list and to speak no more in that name, but the consuming fire of God was like fire shut up in his bones!

In Isaiah six, with this same fire touching his lips, a timid prophet by the name of Isaiah was changed into a flaming messenger and he became one of the greatest prophets of all times. The consuming fire of God caused Isaiah to say "Here am I Lord, send me!"

Evangelist Vance Havner made the following statement: "We have a lot of dead heads, figure heads, and hot heads, but what we need are some hot hearts; we need some people who run a spiritual temperature for God." Lord, give us cool heads and warm hearts.

There are those who do not like the Holy Ghost fire. If you have no interest in the fire of God you should go to the church where Dr. Sounding Brass and Evangelist Tinkling Symbol give their lengthy discourse on mind power, and better methods of obtaining green power. That's the church where the thermometer never rises higher than the freezing point in the summer time. This is the same church where the sipping saints and puffing pastors condone sin and cuddle the carnal Christian!

You can attend that church if you want to but I will tell you that you will not find God in the place! He is going to dwell where men are seeking Him and doing their best to follow Him!
