## I. THE STATUE OF LIBERTY

A. The "STATUE OF LIBERTY", is a huge copper statue that stands on "LIBERTY" Island, in New York harbor.

It's proper name is "LIBERTY ENLIGHTENING THE WORLD". France gave the monument of the U.S. in 1884 as a symbol of friendship between the two nations.

Notice what the Statue of Liberty represents; 1) "THE STATUE OF LIBERTY" symbolizes the "LIBERTY" and brotherhood that citizens of a country enjoy under a free form of Government. Notice our text, John 8:31-36 "To the Jews who had believed him, Jesus said, "If you hold to my teaching, you are really my disciples. Then you will know the truth, and the truth will set you free." They answered him, "We are Abraham\'s descendants and have never been slaves of anyone. How can you say that we shall be set free?" Jesus replied, "I tell you the truth, everyone who sins is a slave to sin. Now a slave has no permanent place in the family, but a son belongs to it forever. So if the Son sets you free, you will be free indeed."

2\) "THE STATUE OF LIBERTY" is a proud woman dressed in a loose robe that falls in graceful folds to the base of the pedestal on which the statue stands.

Thank God we had someone to stand one day to not only represent freedom for us, but to actually, signglehandedly win our freedom, and "LIBERTY", his name is "JESUS".

3\) The right arm holds a great torch raised high in the air. At night, the torch in the right hand gleams with powerful fluorescent lights.

Thus it stands as a symbol of "LIBERTY" shedding light upon the world.

John Speaking of Jesus, said, John 1:4 "In him was life, and that life was the light of men."

John 1:5 "The light shines in the darkness, but the darkness has not understood it."

How sad, but it is happening all around us today.

We are to help light up the world for Jesus! Matthew 5:14-16 "You are the light of the world. A city on a hill cannot be hidden. Neither do people light a lamp and put it under a bowl. Instead they put it on its stand, and it gives light to everyone in the house. In the same way, let your light shine before men, that they may see your good deeds and praise your Father in heaven."

Let's let our light shine.

4\) "THE STATUE OF LIBERTY" also serves as a lighthouse for ships, and as a beacon light for airplanes.

Sinner friend in the midst of the storm of your LIFE. JESUS will be the guiding light to safety. John 3:16 "For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life."

Satan would like nothing better than to capsize your vessel in the midst of the storm, on the sea of life! I am here to tell you that if you will hold to God's unchanging hand he will guide you to safety!
