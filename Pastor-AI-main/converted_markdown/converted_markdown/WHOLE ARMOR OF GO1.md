# WHOLE ARMOR OF GOD

Dear Lord Jesus, I give myself to you completely today body, soul, and spirit. By an act of my own will, I put on the whole armor of God in preparation for doing spiritual warfare throughout this day.

I put on the helmet of salvation. Lord help me to think thoughts that you would be pleased with. Help me to see what you see, hear what you hear, and speak your words, for they are words of life.

I put on the breastplate of righteousness. I commit myself to doing the right thing with regard to every situation I encounter today. I take off my filthy robes of righteousness, and put on the righteousness of the Lord Jesus Christ.

I gird my loins about with truth and truthfulness. I commit myself to walk before you, and before those around me in full integrity this day. Lord I will be truthful no matter the personal cost.

I put on the sandals of the preparation of the gospel of peace, that I might walk across hostile, alien, enemy territory with relative ease. Lord your word has said that I would "Tread upon serpents, scorpions, and over all the power of the enemy," and that "nothing would by any means hurt me". Now I receive that promise in the Name of Jesus.

I take up the shield of Faith and anoint the shield, that I might quench all the fiery darts of the wicked. Lord train me in the use of that shield today. I commit myself to the proper use of the shield of faith, so that I might make advances against the enemy and his strongholds.

I take up the sword of the Spirit, which is the Word of God. Lord help me to be trained in the proper use of the Word of God today. I commit myself to using the Sword of the Spirit to plunder every stronghold of the enemy in my life today. I will mortally wound the enemy of my soul with the Sword of the Spirit.

I further commit myself to fighting back to back with people of like precious faith throughout this day. I set the Holy Spirit as my rear guard against any ambushment of the enemy. I take off the spirit of heaviness, and put on the garment of praise. I will walk before you and others in praise and thanksgiving throughout this day.

PRAYER OF PROTECTION AND PROVISION

I apply the Blood of Jesus to my family and I (call the name of all family members). I also apply your Blood to everything that belongs to us. I pray that the Blood will destroy everything unlike itself in our lives today. I pray that the Blood will protect us and provide for us throughout this day!
