## IV. THE COMPONENT OF LONG-SUFFERING...

Long-suffering: Bearing injuries, insults, and troubles
[patiently]{.underline} for a long time.

Two significant things need to be noted about a long-suffering attitude:

- To maintain a spirit which is long-suffering means the person who
 operates in this attitude never [strikes]{.underline} back.

- The ability to walk free of the need to strike back or get even is not
 [natural]{.underline}, it is [divine]{.underline}.

Long-suffering is mentioned in this list of components of the fruit of
the Spirit because each of these items are a [character]{.underline}
[trait]{.underline} of God and long-suffering is one of them. Romans
2:4, 1 Timothy 1:16

God withholds His judgment from the world because He is long-suffering,
waiting for more and more [people]{.underline} to be
[saved]{.underline}. 2 Peter 3:15, Isaiah 48:9

Barclay says, "If God had been a man, He would have long ago wiped man
off the face of the earth because of his terrible disobedience."

Man is Gods [crowning]{.underline} [creation]{.underline}; therefore,
God is long-suffering toward him.

Once we are saved we are to begin to [emulate]{.underline} the
characteristics of our Heavenly Father and one way to do this is to
become long-suffering. Colossians 1:10-12, 2 Timothy 4:2

CONCLUSION: If God was long-suffering toward us when we were
[sinners]{.underline}, and He was, then He will require us to be
long-suffering with [others]{.underline}! Matthew 18:23-35

It is in our best interest to allow the fruit of the Spirit to operate
