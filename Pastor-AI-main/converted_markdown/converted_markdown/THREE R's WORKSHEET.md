THREE R\'s OF RENEWAL

TEXT: 2 Samuel 5:17-25

INTRO: The worst thing that can happen to any organization is a lack of introspection and a failure to adjust to the times.

When renewal does not occur the following things happen:

- Decay

- Disrepair

- Decline

- Lack of motivation

- Loss of interest

- Reversal

If renewal does not occur pretty often, the leader will burnout and the organization will crash and burn!

The following are three R's of renewal.

# I. REINVENT...

Reinvent Programs...

Reinvent People...

Reinvent Ourselves...

## II. REAFFIRM...

Reaffirm Programs - If a ministry has grown obsolete or ineffective do one of three things:

- Adjust it!

- Repackage it!

- Kill it!

Reaffirm People. People need to be reaffirmed in the following ways:

- Cards...

- Calls...

- A pat on the back...

- Public recognition...

Reaffirm Yourself

Things we must do to reaffirm ourselves:

- Ongoing evaluation...

- Ongoing education ...

- Work on our strengths...

- Overcome our weaknesses...

## III. RELEASE...

When we release a thing, we must allow for the task to be accomplished with the personality of the leader being dominant.

We must:

- Release Programs...

- Release People...

- Release Ourselves...

David could lead his men into battle but he could not fight the battle alone! Three things that made them successful were;

- God...

- The Leader...

- Obedient Followers...
