# FORMULA FOR SPIRITUAL RENEWAL

 2 Chron 7:14 I f my people, who are called by my name, shall humble themselves, and pray, and seek my face, and turn from their wicked ways; then will I hear from heaven, and will forgive their sin, and will heal their land.

## I. THE PEOPLE OF RENEWAL: If my people, which are called by my name...

We are: 1) His people (2) The sheep of His pasture (3) The apple of His eye (4) A holy nation (5) A royal priesthood (6) A chosen generation (7) Heirs of God (8) Joint heirs with Christ

## II. THE PROCESS OF RENEWAL: shall humble themselves, and pray, and seek my face, and turn from their wicked ways;

Pride and arrogance rule the day in this world and sad to say it is not much different in the church. God "Hates a proud look." "Pride goes before destruction and a haughty spirit before a fall." "Those who have a broken and contrite spirit God will in no wise cast out."

You can be baptized in every water hole, that will never never save your soul. You can shake and shake the preachers hand, that will never lead you to the promised land. Change of heart, change of mind, that is what it takes to leave this world behind. If you want to go up you gotta go down brother and pray, pray, pray.

1. I will hear (give audience to) from heaven "The eyes of the Lord run to and fro..." "The ear of the Lord is atuned to the cry of the righteous." How can a mom distinguish her child's cry form all the other children? "You will call upon me and I will answer."

2. I will forgive their sin You were not redeemed with corruptible things..." "Come now and let us reason together say's the Lord, though your sins be as scarlet..." "There is therefore now no condemnation..."

3. I will heal their land. The word land as it is used here means anything that effects your life! John 10 "The thief comes to steal, kill and destroy. I am come that you might have life and have it more ABUNDNTLY." Exodus 3:21-22 "When you go, you shall not go empty..." The Scripture speaks of 4 fold and seven fold restitution... God gave Job double for his trouble... Isaiah 61:7 "For your shame you shall have double; and for the confusion you have endured you shall be able to rejoice in everything that will be restored to you. Therefore, in this life you shall possess double what you forfeited: Everlasting joy shall be unto you."
