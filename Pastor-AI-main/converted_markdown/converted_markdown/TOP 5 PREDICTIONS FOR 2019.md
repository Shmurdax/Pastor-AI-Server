# TOP 5 PREDICTIONS FOR 2019

TEXT: 1 Peter 1:22-25 (NKJV) "Since you have purified your souls in obeying the truth through the Spirit in sincere love of the brethren, love one another fervently with a pure heart, having been born again, not of corruptible seed but incorruptible, through the word of God which lives and abides forever, because "All flesh is as grass, And all the glory of man as the flower of the grass. The grass withers, And its flower falls away, But the word of the Lord endures forever." Hebrews 13:8 (NKJV) "Jesus Christ is the same yesterday, today, and forever."

INTRO: Today's world is filled with all kinds of uncertainty:

- Uncertainty about the future...

- Uncertainty about the economy...

- Uncertainty about governmental structures...

- Uncertainty about human relationships...

- Uncertainty about eternity...

The uncertainty of the world's situation results in the population of the planet living at a stress level which no other generation has ever before experienced. The greatest side effect of a world filled with uncertainty is the loss of hope. The Word of God tells us "Hope is the anchor of the soul." When the anchor of one's life has been disabled, the winds of uncertainty will most certainly have a devastating result.

Diuna Barnes said, "Certainty is a stop the mind makes between uncertainties."

Though the world is filled with uncertainty, I want to talk to you about ten predictions for 2019 which will restore hope in any situation where hope is deficient.

PREDICTION #1 - THE WORD OF GOD WILL CONTINUE TO BE THE MOST POWERFUL FORCE ON THE EARTH...

There are many powerful forces on the earth but the most powerful force on the earth is the Word of God! Hebrews 4:12 (NKJV) "For the word of God is living and powerful, and sharper than any two-edged sword, piercing even to the division of soul and spirit, and of joints and marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart."

The Word of God:

- Is a devouring flame... Jeremiah 5:14

- Is a crushing hammer... Jeremiah 23:29

- Is a life-giving force... Ezekiel 37:7

- Is a saving power... Romans 1:16

- Is a defensive weapon... Ephesians 6:17

- Is a probing instrument... Hebrews 4:12

No matter what else changes, the Word of God will remain the most powerful force on the earth.

Abraham Lincoln said, "I am busily engaged in the study of the Bible. I believe it is God's word because it finds me where I am."

Abraham Lincoln also said, "I believe the Bible is the best gift God has ever given to man. All the good of the Savior of the world is communicated to us through the Book."

I predict Word wielding Christians will continue to see the power of the Word at work in 2019!

PREDICTION \# 2 - PRAYER WILL CONTINUE TO BE THE MOST EFFECTIVE THING A CHRISTIAN CAN DO...

Prayer is one of the most taught and least practiced of all theological topics. The Christian is never as powerful as they are when they are praying! Praying Christians accomplish far more over a lifetime than do their non-praying counterparts!

A.J. Gordon said, "You can do more than pray after you have prayed; but you can never do more than pray until you have prayed."  

Prayer is:

- Communication with God...

- A deterrent to sinful behavior...

- Filled with endless opportunity...

- A sure path to personal humility...

- A voltage regulator on the power gift...

- The only path to the throne room of God...

Requirements for receiving answers to prayer:

- Faith... Mark 11:24

- Contrition... 2 Chronicles 7:14

- Obedience... 1 John 3:22

- Righteousness... James 5:16

- Whole-heartedness... Jeremiah 29:13

E. M. Bounds said, "God shapes the world by prayer. The more prayer there is in the world the better the world will be."

Sidlow Baxter said, "Men may spurn our appeals, reject our message, oppose our arguments, despise our persons, but they are helpless against our prayers."

The next time the enemy attempts to make us feel as if prayer is not enough we should remind ourselves that prayer is the most powerful thing a Christian can do!

I predict praying Christians will continue to be a force to be reckoned with in 2019!

PREDICTION \# 3 - THE WORLD WILL CONTINUE TO BE A SAFE PLACE FOR THE CHRISTIAN...

A large part of the world's population is concerned about their safety! Political campaigns have been predicated on asking "Do you feel safe?"

The inference is that if we elect the right candidate, they will keep us safe. No candidate can give that assurance in today's world! Chris Coleman said, "We can't feel safe because we are not safe."

We live in a dangerous world! 2 Timothy 3:1-5 (NKJV) "But know this, that in the last days perilous times will come: For men will be lovers of themselves, lovers of money, boasters, proud, blasphemers, disobedient to parents, unthankful, unholy, unloving, unforgiving, slanderers, without self-control, brutal, despisers of good, traitors, headstrong, haughty, lovers of pleasure rather than lovers of God, having a form of godliness but denying its power. And from such people turn away!" This sounds like a local news cast or the latest newspaper! Any way you slice it, we live in a dangerous world!

Though the world around us is dangerous the Children of God are as safe as we can be for our destiny is in the hands of the Lord!

Why should Christians feel secure in dangerous times?

- The presence of the Lord undergirds us... "Underneath are His everlasting arms..."

- The presence of the Lord overshadows us... "The shadow of His hand..."

- The presence of the Lord is beside us... "Like a shade on our right hand and left hand..."

- The presence of the Lord is behind us... "He is our rear guard..."

- The presence of the Lord goes before us... "The steps of good people are ordered by the Lord..."

- The presence of the Lord is all around us... "Like a hen protects her chickens..." "He's got the whole world in His hand..."

The Child of God has nothing to fear because our lives are in the hand of the Lord!

I predict the world will continue to be a safe place for the children of God in 2019!

PREDICTION \# 4 - THE GRACE OF GOD WILL CONTINUE TO BE EXTENDED TO HIS CHILDREN...

Mercy is not getting what I do deserve; punishment for sin committed! Grace is getting what I do not deserve; the blessings of God!

The word grace is mentioned one hundred seventy times in the King James Version of the Bible. This is a very, very important subject!

The greatest gift available to mankind is the grace of God! Dwight Moody said, "Grace isn\'t a little prayer you chant before receiving a meal. It\'s a way to live." The law tells me how crooked I am. Grace comes along and straightens me out."

Until Christ finished His work on the cross grace was only something which was a figment of man's wildest imagination! Once His work was finished on the cross, grace became a reality to all humankind!

Think of grace in this way:

- Grace covers me when I deserve it the least...

- Grace releases me from my fiercest bondages...

- Grace opens up doors for me which was permanently shut...

- Grace provides a way of escaping judgment...

- Grace intervenes when sin is present...

- Grace delivers when the enemy persists...

- Grace proves my faith and salvation...

- Grace allows me the chance at heaven...

I predict God will continue to extend His grace in 2019! The only question which remains is whether we will accept it or receive it?

PREDICTION \# 5 - THE COMING OF THE LORD WILL CONTINUE TO BE THE MOST ANTICIPATED EVENT OF THE CHURCH...

John 14:1-3 (KJV) "Let not your heart be troubled: ye believe in God, believe also in me. In my Father's house are many mansions: if it were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for you. And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come again, and receive you unto myself; that where I am, there ye may be also." Here in John chapter fourteen Jesus is conditioning His Disciples to the fact that He will be leaving them very soon. One of the things He says to them which were specifically designed to bring cheer to their hearts and smiles to their faces is the promise, "I will come again." More than twenty centuries have come and gone and when I read those words it develops hope in my heart and causes a smile to break out on my face!

This world is in big trouble but let me explain to you that everything is working according to Gods calendar! A lot of people want to know what is about to happen and I want to take a few minutes to tell you exactly what is about to transpire!

1 Thessalonians 4:13-18 (KJV) "But I would not have you to be ignorant, brethren, concerning them which are asleep, that ye sorrow not, even as others which have no hope. For if we believe that Jesus died and rose again, even so them also which sleep in Jesus will God bring with him. For this we say unto you by the word of the Lord, that we which are alive and remain unto the coming of the Lord shall not prevent them which are asleep. For the Lord himself shall descend from heaven with a shout, with the voice of the archangel, and with the trump of God: and the dead in Christ shall rise first: Then we which are alive and remain shall be caught up together with them in the clouds, to meet the Lord in the air: and so shall we ever be with the Lord. Wherefore comfort one another with these words."

1 Corinthians 15:51-58 (KJV) "Behold, I shew you a mystery; We shall not all sleep, but we shall all be changed, In a moment, in the twinkling of an eye, at the last trump: for the trumpet shall sound, and the dead shall be raised incorruptible, and we shall be changed. For this corruptible must put on incorruption, and this mortal must put on immortality. So when this corruptible shall have put on incorruption, and this mortal shall have put on immortality, then shall be brought to pass the saying that is written, Death is swallowed up in victory. O death, where is thy sting? O grave, where is thy victory? The sting of death is sin; and the strength of sin is the law. But thanks be to God, which giveth us the victory through our Lord Jesus Christ. Therefore, my beloved brethren, be ye stedfast, unmoveable, always abounding in the work of the Lord, forasmuch as ye know that your labour is not in vain in the Lord."

He did not leave us comfortless but He provides everything we need to make it through whatever we are called upon to endure!

There is no need for us to allow our circumstances or surroundings to discourage us! God has placed us where we are but He assured us He would "Come again!"

I predict that in 2019, the coming of the Lord will continue to be the most anticipated event on the calendar of Christians around the world!
