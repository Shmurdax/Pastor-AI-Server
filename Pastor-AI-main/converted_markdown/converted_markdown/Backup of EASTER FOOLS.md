# EASTER FOOLS

INTRO: Welcome to Easter Fools Day! Rarely does a thing like this
happen that Easter and April Fools day fall on the same day! In fact,
the last time this occurred was in 1956 and it will not recur until 2029
and then in 2040. 2040 will be the last time this will occur in this
century! Since this happens so rarely I could not resist the temptation
to marry the two ideas together. If you feel I am being sacrilegious you
need to lighten up so the stress of this world won't kill you before
your time!

April Fools Day is all about a joke and Easter is not funny, Easter it
is serious stuff!

That first Easter dawned bright and early in the Garden Tomb outside
Jerusalem:

Jesus had been murdered at the hands of wicked and cruel men and a
religious system run amuck...

His body had been laid in a borrowed tomb on the outskirts of Jerusalem,
not far from where He had been crucified...

The disciples were so grief stricken that they were invisible at the
cross...

The women were reluctantly making their way to the cemetery...

The women encounter two angels that let them know that Jesus was not
dead but alive...

Mary Magdalene supposed Jesus to be the gardener...

It is neither a stretch nor a slight to say that the resurrection was a
joke---and a very good one at that. What better way could Jesus have
mocked a world who had brutally murdered Him than to rise from the dead?

The resurrection was the perfect joke at the expense of those who wanted
Jesus dead!

When we say we are Easter people, we say we live in the light Jesus
brought to the darkness between what is and what should be.
