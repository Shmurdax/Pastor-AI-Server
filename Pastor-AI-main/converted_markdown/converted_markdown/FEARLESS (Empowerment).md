Paul refers to fear in this way, “the spirit of 
fear”. This is a very interesting statement 
indeed. Fear is a spirit? 
 
Two very important facts about fear: 
• Fear is an emotion… Fear as an emotion 
is when someone scares you, or you are 
frightened by a sudden circumstance. 
Fear as an emotion is a good thing. 
• Fear is a spirit… Fear as a spirit is when 
there is constant ongoing fear with 
regard to a thing. 

 
If fear is a spirit we know it is not a spirit 
from God, “God has not given us the spirit 
of fear”. If God does not give us the spirit of 
fear, where does this spirit originate? It 
comes from hell and is of the devil! 
 
A spirit of fear in the life of a minister or 
disciple of Christ is paralyzing. Some of you 
have rolled up in here at Empowerment 
2015 absolutely afraid of your own shadow 
and paralyzed by a spirit of fear. Tonight, 
we are going to shake it off and make the 
devil take it back.
