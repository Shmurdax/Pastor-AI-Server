 Introspection - Anticipation

3) When we take communion, we are supposed to remember what? What Jesus
 did for us on the cross

4) What two things are we supposed to look back on when we take
 communion? The cross and our own journey

 Docking station -- Launching pad

 introspection? 10%

 about what has been revealed

8) Complete this statement... God loves us too much to
 \\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\\. To leave us like we
 are

9) What are we to anticipate when we take communion? The coming of the
 Lord

 Recalibrating our focus toward His eminent return
