INTRO: Proverbs 29:18 is a very interesting verse indeed. This verse is sandwiched between two verses that deal with similar topics but have nothing to do with this verse. Verse 17 has to do with correcting a son and verse 19 deals with the correction of a servant.

Right in the middle of all that, the writer pens, "Where there is no vision, the people perish." Vision is important physically, spiritually, and professionally. Today I want to talk about vision as it relates to the spiritual and professional aspect of our lives. Today, we are going to look at five rules that if followed will help us stay focused on the vision for our lives and ministries. If we follow these rules and are successful in maintaining our focus despite success, failure and fatigue, it will be alarming what we will be able to accomplish over the long haul.
