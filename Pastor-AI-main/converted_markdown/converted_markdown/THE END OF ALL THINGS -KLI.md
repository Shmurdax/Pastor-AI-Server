INTRO: The question on everyone’s mind and 
lips is, “What is about to happen in our 
world?” This is a great question and a question 
which should and must be asked. If you are truly 

interested in such things, study Matthew 24 and 
Luke 21. 
 
For those of you who are interested in such 
things, today I am going to give you a Biblical, 
theological framework to hang your research on. 
If you use this framework for your learning 
experience related to end times, you will stay in 
the proper lane theologically. 
 
Tonight, we are going to look at the final 
disposition on the human race and the new 
heavens and the new earth. 
 
In order to do this, there are 10 things that must 
be considered: 
1) The present age… (PLACE SLIDE #1 
OF PANORAMA OF PROPHECY ON 
SCREEN) Where we are today is living in 
what is known as the present age or the 
dispensation of grace. The dispensation of 
grace began with the resurrection of Jesus 

and will end with the rapture of the 
church. 
2) The Rapture… (PLACE SLIDE #2 OF 
PANORAMA OF PROPHECY ON 
SCREEN) The word rapture is not 
mentioned in the Bible but the words 
“Catching away” are mentioned many 
times and that is where we get our word 
rapture. The rapture will only involve 
those who have died with their faith in 
Christ, as well as those who are alive and 
have placed their faith in Christ, having 
made themselves ready. 1 Thessalonians 
4:16-18 (NKJV) “For the Lord Himself 
will descend from heaven with a shout, 
with the voice of an archangel, and 
with the trumpet of God. And the dead in 
Christ will rise first. 17 Then we who are 
alive and remain shall be caught up 
together with them in the clouds to meet 
the Lord in the air. And thus we shall 
always be with the Lord. 18 Therefore 
comfort one another with these words.” 

3) The Judgement seat of Christ… (PLACE 
SLIDE #3 OF PANORAMA OF 
PROPHECY ON SCREEN) Those who 
are raptured will be in Heaven with Jesus 
for seven years. During this time, 
everyone present will experience the 
Bema, or judgment Seat of Christ. This is 
the judgment of the righteous. The 
judgment of the righteous? Yes! This 
judgment is not to determine our salvation, 
but a judgment of our motives and works. 
2 Corinthians 5:10 (NKJV) “For we must 
all appear before the judgment seat of 
Christ, that each one may receive the 
things done in the body, according to 
what he has done, whether good or bad.” 
Following the Judgment Seat of Christ, we 
will experience The Marriage Supper of 
the Lamb. This is a time of celebration for 
the redeemed, and saints of the ages. 
4) The Great Tribulation… (PLACE SLIDE 
#4 OF PANORAMA OF PROPHECY ON 
SCREEN) Once the rapture has occurred 

the world will experience chaos at levels 
that have never before been seen. We 
believe in a pre-tribulation rapture (My 
philosophy on this). This will be a seven-
year period that coincides with the 
Judgment Seat of Christ and the Marriage 
Supper of the Lamb. This period of time is 
also known as The Time Of The End and 
The Time Of Jacob’s Trouble. Matthew 
24:21 (NKJV) “For then there will be 
great tribulation, such as has not been 
since the beginning of the world until this 
time, no, nor ever shall be.” The 
Antichrist will come into power and things 
will not be too chaotic for the first three 
and a half years. At the three-and-a-half- 
year point, the Antichrist will make an 
about face on the Jewish people and all 
hell will break loose on the earth. Mark of 
the beast – 666 in forehead or hand – 
Can’t buy, sell or trade without this mark – 
The most hated people on earth will be 
those who resist the Antichrist and refuse 

his mark – Even if one chooses martyrdom 
over the mark, one must be saved by the 
blood of Jesus and have their heart right – 
What makes you think you can live for 
God then, if you can’t do it now with the 
help of the Holy Spirit, a church, a pastor 
and a Christian community? Some things 
that will occur: Humans will be covered 
with sores – The sea will be turned to 
blood – Water sources will be turned to 
blood - The sun will scorch men with heat 
- Complete darkness will cover the earth – 
Depression will be so great that people 
will gnaw their tongues to cause pain – 
The Euphrates river will be dried up – The 
earth will be shaken by earthquakes like 
never before in history.

6) The Battle of Armageddon… (PLACE 
SLIDE #6 OF PANORAMA OF 

PROPHECY ON SCREEN) Revelation 
chapters 14, 16, 17 and 19 describe

gathered, assembled and destroyed. At

destruction by the Antichrist. Nations will

valley of Megiddo (Jezreel valley). 
(PLACE A PICTURE OF THE JEZREEL 
VALLEY ON THE SCREEN) The 
Jezreel Valley is roughly 280 miles long 
and 85 miles wide. It is bordered on the 

north by the Nazareth mountains and 
Mount Tabor, on the east and south by 
Mount Gilboa and the mountains of 
Samaria respectively, and on the west 
by Mount Carmel. All of which are 
mentioned in Scripture. This battle will be 
so great that blood will flow as high as the 
horse’s bits. Think of the magnitude of 
that – A valley 280 miles long and 85 
miles wide and blood as deep as the horses 
bits.
