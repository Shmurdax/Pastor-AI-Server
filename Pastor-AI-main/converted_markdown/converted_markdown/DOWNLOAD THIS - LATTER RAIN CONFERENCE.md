Before we decide to write the Holy Spirit off as unimportant or insignificant we should consider the words of Jesus as it relates to the importance of the Holy Spirit. John 16:7 (KJV) "Nevertheless I tell you the truth; It is expedient for you that I go away: for if I go not away, the Comforter will not come unto you; but if I depart, I will send him unto you." In this Scripture, Jesus is stressing to His disciples the necessity of His departure. As long as He lived in a flesh and blood body He could only be in one place at a time. He is attempting to explain to them that the reason it was so necessary for Him to go to the Father was so that the Holy Spirit would come and live in each believer, thus empowering each believer to carry on the work of Christ in the world.

If Jesus thought it necessary to depart so that the Holy Spirit could indwell and empower believers, where do we get off promoting the Holy Spirit as optional equipment?
