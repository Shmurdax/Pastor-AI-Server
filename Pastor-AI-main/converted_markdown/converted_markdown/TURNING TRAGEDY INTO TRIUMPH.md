# TURNING TRAGEDY INTO TRIUMPH

TEXT: JOHN 10:10 (NKJV) "The thief does not come except to steal, and to kill, and to destroy. I have come that they may have life, and that they may have it more abundantly."

INTRO: When tragedy strikes people either run to God or from God. Some people tend to get mad at God when something bad happens. John 10:10 is very clear, anything that falls under the heading of stealing, killing or destroying is NOT from God, but the responsible party is the devil!

Let me state it emphatically, not only is God the one who sends blessing; what the devil meant for evil God will turn it around and use it for your temporal and eternal good. Turnaround in your life is what God is trying to accomplish!

If we will trust God and follow Him there are four things He will accomplish in us and through us, even though we are facing the biggest test of our lives.
