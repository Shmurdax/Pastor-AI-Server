# WEDDINGS

Chain breakers 2017

1) Make sure you are legal to marry a person in the state the wedding is to be conducted

2) Make sure the couple is enrolled in pre marital counseling (Get information from Roy)

3) Have the couple to secure date and make sure the date works on your calendar

4) If you are going to charge them to do the wedding, make sure you communicate how much up front

5) Make sure the couple has done everything they need to do to follow through on the requirements of their venue

6) Require the couple to secure a wedding coordinator who has experience (Get that persons contact information so you can communicate with them)

7) Make sure the rehearsal is at a time and place where you can attend

8) At the rehearsal, do at least two full run thru's with all seating, songs, ques etc...

9) Before dismissing the rehearsal make sure you clearly communicate what time everyone is to be present the next day

10) While preparing the wedding ceremony I like to always put some personal comments about the couple in here and there

11) I usually allow the couple to tweak the ceremony (DO NOT let them fail to take their vows)

12) Don't forget to sign the marriage license (I usually do this before the ceremony so that I do not forget it afterwards)

13) Remember, you are not obligated to conduct a wedding -- If you are not comfortable with doing the wedding for some reason, decline the invitation
