# "MY NEVER AGAIN LIST"

INTRO: A couple of months ago Elaine Looney came to me with a piece of notebook paper that was handwritten, both front and back. She very politely asked me as she and Hugh's pastor, to please pray these Scriptures over Hugh until Easter. When I first looked at the list I did not think too much about it, but as I began to pray that list over Hugh day in and day out, it began to get down in my spirit. So I asked Elaine for her permission to preach a series of sermons from that list. I have entitled this sermon, "MY NEVER AGAIN LIST".

There are some things in life that after we have done them, we realize they did not go over well, and we say to ourselves, "I WILL NEVER AGAIN allow myself to do that particular thing!" The reason we do this is, we do not want to be guilty of repeating the same old mistakes over and over again! (ILL: Sermons, things said to family or business associates, decisions concerning employment, etc..)

I think that it would serve us all very well as we go through life, to make ourselves a "NEVER AGAIN LIST"!

Israel would have been light years ahead if they would have maintained a "NEVER AGAIN LIST"! They just kept repeating the same old mistakes over and over again! They found themselves in a vicious cycle of failure! Hebrews 3:8-11 "Harden not your hearts, as in the provocation, in the day of temptation in the wilderness: When your fathers tempted me, proved me, and saw my works forty years. Wherefore I was grieved with that generation, and said, They do alway err in their heart; and they have not known my ways. So I sware in my wrath, They shall not enter into my rest."

Does that sound like anyone you know? Sad to say, but I can see myself in that mode, during many periods of my life!

Look at what the word of God say's about repeating the same mistakes, over and over again; Solomon said, Proverbs 21:16 "The man that wandereth out of the way of understanding shall remain in the congregation of the dead."

Proverbs 27:8 "As a bird that wandereth from her nest, so is a man that wandereth from his place."

The Apostle Peter spoke some of the most severe words in Scripture on the subject of making the same mistakes over and over again, when he said, 2 Peter 2:14-22 "Having eyes full of adultery, and that cannot cease from sin; beguiling unstable souls: an heart they have exercised with covetous practices; cursed children: Which have forsaken the right way, and are gone astray, following the way of Balaam the son of Bosor, who loved the wages of urighteousness; But was rebuked for his iniquity: the dumb ass speaking with man\'s voice forbad the madness of the prophet. For that reason it becomes imperative that I make myself a "NEVER AGAIN LIST"! Whether I write it out on notebook paper, put it on the screen of my computer, or make a mental note of it, I know that I must do it in order to maintain a victorious life!

Again, the Apostle Peter writes, 1 Peter 2:24-25 "Who his own self bare our sins in his own body on the tree, that we, being dead to sins, should live unto righteousness: by whose stripes ye were healed. ## I. NEVER AGAIN WILL I CONFESS CONDEMNATION FOR...

A. Romans 8:1-14 "There is therefore now no condemnation to them which are in Christ Jesus, who walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit. For the law of the Spirit of life in Christ Jesus hath made me free from the law of sin and death. For what the law could not do, in that it was weak through the flesh, God sending his own Son in the likeness of sinful flesh, and for sin, condemned sin in the flesh: That the righteousness of the law might be fulfilled in us, who walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit. For they that are after the flesh do mind the things of the flesh; but they that are after the Spirit the things of the Spirit. For to be carnally minded is death; but to be spiritually minded is life and peace. Because the carnal mind is enmity against God: for it is not subject to the law of God, neither indeed can be. So then they that are in the flesh cannot please God. But ye are not in the flesh, but in the Spirit, if so be that the Spirit of God dwell in you. Now if any man have not the Spirit of Christ, he is none of his. And if Christ be in you, the body is dead because of sin; but the Spirit is life because of righteousness. But if the Spirit of him that raised up Jesus from the dead dwell in you, he that raised up Christ from the dead shall also quicken your mortal bodies by his Spirit that dwelleth in you.Therefore, brethren, we are debtors, not to the flesh, to live after the flesh. For if ye live after the flesh, ye shall die: but if ye through the Spirit do mortify the deeds of the body, ye shall live. For as many as are led by the Spirit of God, they are the sons of God."

The Greek word for "CONDEMNATION" here is "KATAKRIMA" (Kat- ak- ree-ma), meaning, an adverse sentence.

To be "CONDEMNED", means that after all evidence has been considered, to be declared to be reprehensible, wrong, or evil. It means that we have been pronounced guilty!

B. Please take a moment to remember, before you and I found Jesus as Lord and Saviour, we had a "CONDEMNED" sign hanging around our necks!

Absolutely all the evidence pointed to the fact that we were reprehensible, wrong, evil, guilty, therefore we were "CONDEMNED" by the power of sin.

According to the writer of the book of Romans, because I am in Christ, I am now free from "CONDEMNATION"!

Can you imagine the Joy a person would experience if they had been "CONDEMNED" to death for a hideous crime, and in the process of being strapped into the electric chair, the Governor walked into the death chamber with proper documentation to prove a pardon from their "CONDEMNATION"? They would no doubt make a complete fool out of themselves, before the Governor, their families and friends, a national television audience, and anyone else who was inclined to watch!

Well, I have even better news than that! Every person in this place who knows Jesus in the full and free pardon of sin has been freed from the guilt, and the power of "CONDEMNATION"!

## C. Even though we are all aware that a Christian is not supposed to live in "CONDEMNATION", we are also aware that Satan is "AN ACCUSER OF THE BRETHREN"! Rev. 12:10-11 "And I heard a loud voice saying in heaven, Now is come salvation, and strength, and the kingdom of our God, and the power of his Christ: for the accuser of our brethren is cast down, which accused them before our God day and night. And they overcame him by the blood of the Lamb, and by the word of their testimony; and they loved not their lives unto the death."

First of all, the enemy "ACCUSES" you to God! This is not anything new, he has been doing this since the beginning of time!

Job is a good example of how the enemy of our souls "ACCUSES" us before God! Job 1:1, 6-11 "There was a man in the land of Uz, whose name was Job; and that man was perfect and upright, and one that feared God, and eschewed evil.6 Now there was a day when the sons of God came to present themselves before the Lord, and Satan came also among them. And the Lord said unto Satan, Whence comest thou? Then Satan answered the Lord, and said, From going to and fro in the earth, and from walking up and down in it. And the Lord said unto Satan, Hast thou considered my servant Job, that there is none like him in the earth, a perfect and an upright man, one that feareth God, and escheweth evil? Then Satan answered the Lord, and said, Doth Job fear God for nought? Hast not thou made an hedge about him, and about his house, and about all that he hath on every side? thou hast blessed the work of his hands, and his substance is increased in the land. But put forth thine hand now, and touch all that he hath, and he will curse thee to thy face."

Look at this, there is not one shred of evidence to support anything that the Devil is saying about Job!

Job has a proven track record, but that does not keep the enemy from "ACCUSING" Job to God! Can you imagine that? God knows Job to be a man of impeccable integrity, Satan is aware of Job's integrity as well, yet it does not deter the enemy from "ACCUSING" Job!

So you can see that the truth has nothing to do with the enemy attempting to "CONDEMN" Job, or anyone else!

(Story of Martin Luther)

This tactic of the enemy has never worked and will never work because when God looks at us he sees us through the shed blood of Jesus! Glory to God I am as RIGHTEOUS as the blood of Jesus can make me, and there is absolutely nothing the devil can do about it!

Secondly, the enemy "ACCUSES" us to ourselves! This is possibly one of his most effective weapons he has in his arsenal!

It is difficult for us to forgive ourselves! We can usually forgive someone else quicker than we can forgive ourselves!

You see, once God forgives you it is vitally important that you learn to forgive yourself!

If you cannot bring yourself to the position of accepting and appropriating forgiveness in your own life, you will always live under a cloud of self "CONDEMNATION"!

You see, the reason it is so vitally important to the strategy of the enemy for us, as the children of God to fail to forgive ourselves, is because as long as we live in self "CONDEMNATION" we cannot accept and appropriate the benefits of God in our lives!

Thirdly, an equally effective tool of the enemy is Satan's ability to manipulate well meaning "CHRISTIANS" to "CONDEMN" one another!

Jesus said that the world would know that "We have passed from death unto life, because we love our brother"!

If I love you, I can not bring myself to aid in the process of hanging the old "CONDEMNED" sign, that God so powerfully delivered you from, back around your neck again!

When I do that, I have become the devils helper!

The bottom line is, that I refuse to allow the devil, myself, or anyone else, to bring me under "CONDEMNATION" ever again because, "There is therefore now no condemnation to them which are in Christ Jesus, who walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit."

B. I want us to take a few minutes to carefully examine our text in it's overall setting.

Paul is writing to the Philippian church here. This Church could be considered the "ideal" church, in many ways. Scripture makes aware that this church was very appreciative and benevolent. Do you think they had a "NEVER AGAIN LIST"?

This church was the fruit of Paul's second missionary journey. The founding of this church certainly would not have caused the pastor to be invited to speak at a church growth seminar, because it's beginnings were meager and down right small.

The nucleus of the charter membership of First Assembly of God in Philippi were a few women at the river side. Now that doesn't sound like much of an up and coming church, does it?

The first convert of this church was also a woman, by the name of Lydia, and her occupation was a seller of purple.

Following Lydia's conversion the Philippian jailer and his family became a part of this struggling home missions work.

This church was founded amidst some very bitter persecution, in fact, the Philippian jailer was saved while he was tending the jail cell of the pastor and the associate pastor!

Paul and Silas were such upbeat people that at midnight they forgot they were in prison, and held a prayer and praise fest that caused the jail house to rock, and in the midst of a suicide attempt the Philippian jailer got saved!

Is it possible that Paul and Silas maintained a "NEVER AGAIN LIST"?

## III. NEVER AGAIN WILL I CONFESS LACK FOR...

A. Philip. 4:19 "But my God shall supply all your need according to his riches in glory by Christ Jesus."

Last week we talked about the meager beginnings of this Church at Philippi, which amounted to a few women at the rivers edge. We also spoke of the bitter persecution and most trying of times that seemed to stalk this "IDEAL" Church!

Now, the billing of this Church as an "IDEAL" Church is not my idea, but the Apostle Paul's. Paul sets this Church up as a "ROLE MODEL" for others to follow.

Some people say "I don't want anyone to follow me because I am human"! Paul said "Follow me as I follow Christ"!

I have found that "ROLE MODELS" whether people or institutions, normally surface through bitterly difficult circumstances!

Everyone wants to preach like Paul, sing like Silas, and have compassion like Jesus, but who among us is willing to endure what they did to become a "ROLE MODEL"?

Notice the train of thought Paul followed when writing to the "IDEAL" Church;

1) In chapter One he thanks God for this Church! And also for their fellowship with him and his Ministry! He goes to great length to explain to them that the persecution that he, and they were experiencing was for the furtherance of the Gospel! He wraps up chapter One by saying, "DON'T BE TERRIFIED BY YOUR ADVERSARIES"!

2) In chapter Two he admonishes this Church, that no matter what they faced to "WALK IN LOVE"! He tells them not to fall into the trap of covetousness, and to allow the mind of Christ to always be present in them in every situation. He tells them not to murmur or get involved in disputes between one another, but to keep their focus so that their light would shine in the midst of a crooked and perverse generation!

3) He begins chapter three by telling them to rejoice in the Lord! Imagine that! In the midst of bitter persecution they are being told to rejoice! (Paul told the men who were about to be drowned in a storm at sea to "be of good cheer"!)

He tells them to be on the lookout for the enemy by saying, Philip. 3:2 "Watch out for those dogs, those men who do evil, those mutilators of the flesh."

He also tells them to put no confidence in the flesh. He say's that those who put confidence in the flesh will wind up disappointed! Then notice what he say's, Philip. 3:8-14

"Yea doubtless, and I count all things but loss for the excellency of the knowledge of Christ Jesus my Lord: for whom I have suffered the loss of all things, and do count them but dung, that I may win Christ, And be found in him, not having mine own righteousness, which is of the law, but that which is through the faith of Christ, the righteousness which is of God by faith: That I may know him, and the power of his resurrection, and the fellowship of his sufferings, being made conformable unto his death; If by any means I might attain unto the resurrection of the dead. Not as though I had already attained, either were already perfect: but I follow after, if that I may apprehend that for which also I am apprehended of Christ Jesus. Brethren, I count not myself to have apprehended: but this one thing I do, forgetting those things which are behind, and reaching forth unto those things which are before, I press toward the mark for the prize of the high calling of God in Christ Jesus."

4) Paul begins chapter four by identifying the Philippian church as "TRUE YOKEFELLOWS" in the great cause of the Gospel.

Paul spends most of Chapter Four reminiscing about how the Philippian Church had ministered to his need throughout the entirety of his ministry.

In verse Eighteen Paul say's, "I have received full payment and even more; I am amply supplied, now that I have received from Epaphroditus the gifts you sent. They are a fragrant offering, an acceptable sacrifice, pleasing to God."

In spite of their difficult circumstances the Church at Philippi found a way to demonstrate the love of Christ to Paul through their benevolent spirit.

Paul was offering his commendation to them for their random acts of kindness toward him.

Realizing he did not have the wherewithal to return their generosity Paul say's, "But my God shall supply all your need according to his riches in glory by Christ Jesus."

Please understand Paul is saying, "THANK YOU FOR YOUR GENEROSITY TOWARD ME. I HAVE NO WAY TO REPAY YOU, BUT MY GOD SHALL SUPPLY ALL YOUR NEED ACCORDING TO HIS RICHES IN GLORY BY CHRIST JESUS"!

This guarantee from Paul to the Philippians is much better than a personal guarantee! He is saying "I GUARANTEE YOU THAT GOD WILL SUPPLY ALL YOUR NEED, HEAVEN HAS BEEN PUT UP AS COLLATERAL!"

Joshua 1:8 "Do not let this Book of the Law depart from your mouth; meditate on it day and night, so that you may be careful to do everything written in it. Psalm 1:1-3 "Blessed is the man who does not walk in the counsel of the wicked or stand in the way of sinners or sit in the seat of mockers. ## V. NEVER AGAIN WILL I CONFESS SICKNESS, FOR... "He was wounded for my transgressions, he was bruised for my iniquities: the chastisement of my peace was upon him; and with his stripes I am healed."

A. Many people who say they believe in "DIVINE HEALING" spend an exorbitant amount of their time confessing "SICKNESS" and "DISEASE" over their lives.

Many people will tell you right up front that they have been "TRYING" to catch a cold, or some other malady.

The message of "DIVINE HEALING" has been all but lost out of the Pentecostal church!

Some of the reason for this is that many preachers do not want anyone to think they are a part of the "NAME IT AND CLAIM IT" or "BLAB IT AND GRAB IT" crowd! As a result, they and their congregations have become a part of the "DOUBT IT AND DO WITHOUT IT" crowd!

This kind of philosophy will produce a church full of "SICK" people!

Now let me just tell you that if you are bent on being "SICK", don't let me stand in your way, help yourself! If it were not for "SICKNESS" some people could not even carry on a conversation!

I have actually prayed for people who I knew full well that they did not want to be "HEALED"! They were getting too much mileage out of their "SICKNESS" to let it go! (Story of the man who did not want to be prayed for because he was on full disability!)

However if you have better things to do than sit in the doctors office by the hour, I am going to attempt to tell you how to walk in your "HEALING"!

First of all you are going to have to make up your mind to quit talking yourself into being "SICK"!

It is easier to speak "HEALING", than to go to the trouble of staying "SICK"! Can I tell you, your Heavenly Father want's you to be well!

Under the Old Testament economy the promises regarding the "HEALTH" of the Children of God is very plainly described as being protected by God! Psalm 91:9-16 "Because thou hast made the Lord, which is my refuge, even the most High, thy habitation; There shall no evil befall thee, neither shall any plague come nigh thy dwelling. For he shall give his angels charge over thee, to keep thee in all thy ways. They shall bear thee up in their hands, lest thou dash thy foot against a stone. Thou shalt tread upon the lion and adder: the young lion and the dragon shalt thou trample under feet. Because he hath set his love upon me, therefore will I deliver him: I will set him on high, because he hath known my name. He shall call upon me, and I will answer him: I will be with him in trouble; I will deliver him, and honour him. With long life will I satisfy him, and shew him my salvation."

I'm not sure we really understand this! Under the Old Testament economy God makes promises to his people such as, "NO PLAGUE SHALL COME NIGH THY DWELLING", and "WITH LONG LIFE I WILL SATISFY YOU"!

Notice our text: Isaiah 53:5 "But he was wounded for our transgressions, he was bruised for our iniquities: the chastisement of our peace was upon him; and with his stripes we are healed."

The writer of the book of Hebrews said that under the dispensation of Grace, God has given us;

"BETTER" things!

"BETTER" hope!

" BETTER" testament!

"BETTER" covenant!

"BETTER" sacrifices!

"BETTER" substance!

"BETTER" country!

"BETTER" resurrection!

"BETTER" inheritance!

"BETTER" promises!

Now, if God promised those people under the Old Testament economy that "NO PLAGUE WOULD COME NIGH THEIR DWELLING", and that he would "SATISFY THEM WITH LONG LIFE", and we have "BETTER PROMISES" under the New Testament economy, then we should claim the "PROMISES" and walk in our "HEALING"! Notice this: 1 Peter 2:24 "Who his own self bare our sins in his own body on the tree, that we, being dead to sins, should live unto righteousness: by whose stripes ye were healed."

Isaiah 53:5 "But he was wounded for our transgressions, he was bruised for our iniquities: the chastisement of our peace was upon him; and with his stripes we are healed."

Exodus 15:26 "And said, If thou wilt diligently hearken to the voice of the Lord thy God, and wilt do that which is right in his sight, and wilt give ear to his commandments, and keep all his statutes, I will put none of these diseases upon thee, which I have brought upon the Egyptians: for I am the Lord that healeth thee."

Please notice what the Word of God is saying with regard to "HEALING". It say's we "WERE HEALED", we "ARE HEALED", and we are "BEING HEALED"! My friend that is past, present, and future tense!

Based on those Scriptures, I would say God has us covered on the subject of "DIVINE HEALING"!

I want us to sing the old song, "STANDING ON THE PROMISES"!

B. I want us to spend some time exploring what the Word of God has to say about what we are to do in order that we could receive our "HEALING"!

1) Confess your faults! James 5:16 "Confess your faults one to another, and pray one for another, that ye may be healed. The effectual fervent prayer of a righteous man availeth much."

We are being told here that if we want to be "HEALED" we must acknowledge our sinful condition!

Notice what he say's will happen to the person who will suppress pride and confess his sin: James 5:16 "Confess your faults one to another, and pray one for another, that ye may be healed. The effectual fervent prayer of a righteous man availeth much.

"HEALING" is "PROMISED" to those who acknowledge and forsake a sinful condition!

He then say's, "... The effectual fervent prayer of a righteous man availeth much." This simply means "The producing, valid, intensely earnest prayer of righteous people will get the job done!"

Some times we do not feel very "RIGHTEOUS", thus we have very little confidence in our own ability to pray, or receive from God!

Let me just tell you that if you have confessed your sin's to God, you are as "RIGHTEOUS" as the blood of Jesus can make you! You and I are certainly not worthy on our own merits, but we have been made worthy by the blood of Jesus!

If we are as worthy to receive our "HEALING" as the blood of Jesus can make us we might as well lay hold of the promise and receive our "HEALING" today!
