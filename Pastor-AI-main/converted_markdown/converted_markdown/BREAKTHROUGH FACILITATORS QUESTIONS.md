# BREAKTHROUGH

(LIFE GROUP FACILITATORS QUESTIONS)

(Questions in black)

(Answers in blue)

WEEK ONE:

Read 2 Samuel 5

1\) How many times was David anointed? Three -- One when he was
seventeen and Samuel anointed him to be King -- One when he was thirty
and became King over Judah (Discuss how it took thirteen years from the
time he was anointed until he actually became King -- God often has a
slow developing plan for us) -- One when he was thirty seven and became
king over all Israel

2\) What does it mean to be anointed? Anointing is special enablement
from the Lord to accomplish a specific task

3\) How does breakthrough differ from progress? Progress is incremental
advancement -- Breakthrough is exponential advancement

4\) What is breakthrough? Radical expansion -- Turning point --
Exponential advancement

5\) Ask if anyone needs a breakthrough? If so, have the group to pray
for them

WEEK TWO:

Read 2 Samuel 5

1\) What is the first step toward breakthrough? Prayer (David didn't
assume anything -- He prayed before each battle)

2\) Why is prayer so important? It is the way God has chosen to
communicate with man

3\) How long should one fast and pray before making a major decision?
Pastor Nordin say's three days (There is no scripture that say's this --
It is just what has worked best for him)

4\) What is the second step toward breakthrough? Obedience (The Bible
say's "Obedience is better than sacrifice)

5\) What can we expect when we are on the verge of a breakthrough?
Accelerated resistance and verbal attacks (For David the Jebusites said
"The lame and the blind could keep you out) (If the resistance against
you has recently accelerated this is a good sign you are on the verge of
a breakthrough)

WEEK THREE:

Read 2 Samuel 5

1\) What is the third step toward breakthrough? Faith (Read Hebrews
11:1-11)

2\) What proves we have enough faith for a breakthrough? Patience (Read
Hebrews 10:36)

3\) What does it take to please God? Faith (Read Hebrews 11:6)

4\) What is the forth step toward breakthrough? Wage spiritual war

5\) What is needed to achieve a major breakthrough? Implement a
ridiculous strategy (Let them know at some point they will need to quit
playing defense and implement an offensive strategy in the situation)

6\) What was David told to do in the second conflict? Sneak up behind
the enemy -- Position themselves in the mulberry trees -- Don't say a
word or make a sound until you hear horses running through the tops of
the trees -- When you hear the sound, wage war

7\) What was the fifth step toward breakthrough? Expect the miraculous
(The sound of horses running through the tops of trees is both
ridiculous and miraculous) (Give the people opportunity to share
testimonies of miracles in their lives)

8\) What did David's strategy make the Philistines believe? They
believed David had hired a coalition of King's against them -- They
thought David and his men were in front of them -- They assumed the
hired guns were behind them -- They fled because they believed they were
outnumbered -- In their flight they became vulnerable to David and his
men -- They were defeated (Allow the people to share how the most
ridiculous and simple things have led to breakthrough)

9\) Pray for the people to receive breakthrough
