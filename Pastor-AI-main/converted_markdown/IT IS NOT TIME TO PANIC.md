# IT IS NOT TIME TO PANIC

TEXT: Psalm 121:1-8 (NKJV) "I will lift up my eyes to the
hills--- From whence comes my help? ^2 ^My help comes from the Lord,
Who\
made heaven and earth. ^3 ^He will not allow your foot to be moved; He

Shall neither slumber nor sleep. The Lord is your keeper;
The Lord is your shade at your right hand. ^6 ^The sun shall not strike
you by day, Nor the moon by night. The Lord shall preserve you from
all evil; He shall preserve your soul. ^8 ^The Lord shall preserve your
going out and your coming in From this time forth, and even
forevermore."

INTRO: The November 2020 world we live in is panic stricken:

- The uncertainty of the election...

- The uptick in Covid 19 cases...

- The threat of shutdowns...

- The possibility of a civil war looms...

- The economy is in a fragile state...

- Saber rattling by nations around the world...

Fear and panic are on the faces of almost everyone we meet, whether on
the street, at work, or in the market place. Our world has never faced a
time like this, and one can sense we could be moments away from the
whole world having a panic attack! The fear of what that would look like
is off the chain.

In some modern homes these days, an added feature to the construction of
the home is that of a panic room or safe room. (PUT UP SOME PICTURES OF
SAFE ROOMS AS I SPEAK)

In a country of gated communities, panic rooms are designed to be the
ultimate in security. Isn't it interesting that the very politicians who
do not want secure borders live in gated communities, have fences around
their houses, and hire armed guards to protect them?

Panic rooms range from simple rooms with reinforced doors and walls, to
elaborate mini-fortresses that protect their occupants against
biological and nuclear attacks, hurricanes, tornadoes, and home
invasions.

Think of a panic room as a vault for people.

Panic rooms can be traced as far back as ancient Egypt. Secret rooms
were built within the ancient pyramids to protect an entombed pharaoh\'s
treasures from thieves.

Modern residential panic rooms began appearing on the West Coast about
25 years ago. Panic rooms are evolving into safe "cores," which
include a fortified section or complete floor in newly constructed
residences.

While some may laugh off panic rooms as money pits, that placate the
fears of the wealthy and the paranoid, modern panic rooms are increasing
in popularity among those desiring security in a world filled with
insecurity. (I'm working on a bid for a house in Arkansas -- the first
thing the new homeowners wanted to talk about was a safe room)

Panic rooms are the ultimate gated community. (END PICTURES OF PANIC
ROOMS)

If you are a Child of God, IT IS NOT TIME TO PANIC! If you do not know
Jesus, I am going to give you an opportunity to push the panic button
and run to Jesus!

It is a fact that our world is teetering on the brink of chaos and
collapse. In this scenario, what is the child of God to do?

Some situations in life will offer us just two options; Panic or Pray!
Our tendency is to panic.

We all have situations that cause us to hyperventilate with heart-racing
panic attacks! We are often:

- Pursued by our troubles...

- Oppressed by the enemy...

- Trapped in a difficulty...

- Entangled in our troubles...

- Cornered by what appears to be impossible situations...

These things make us feel that the enemy is so hot on our trail that he
is nipping at our heels. (My experience with a Doberman Pincher while
selling Electrolux vacuum cleaners)

I assure you that if you are in a tough situation right now, suffering
pain, worry, anguish, or illness, the devil is undoubtedly behind it, to
a greater or lesser degree.

But, oh, what a sweet comfort to know that the Lord is looking out for
us.

God\'s protection is like a strong house. He will shelter us no matter
what kind of situation we face.

Psalm 121 reminds us that the Lord watches over His people. So, there is
no need to panic. Please take another look at this passage with me, and
let's look at it from the perspective of our current situation... Psalm
121:1-8 (NKJV) "I will lift up my eyes to the hills--- From whence
comes my help? ^2 ^My help comes from the Lord, Who made heaven and
earth." Starring down our trouble is not about who we are, it is
about who our helper is! If our help comes from the Lord, IT IS NOT TIME
TO PANIC!

^ ^Vs. 3 "He will not allow your foot to be moved..." On the
day of our fiercest battle, HE will not allow our feet to slide! IT IS
NOT TIME TO PANIC!

Vs. 3-4 "He who keeps you will not slumber. ^4 ^Behold, He who keeps
Israel Shall neither slumber nor sleep." Keep in mind, while we wage
war, the Lord is wide awake! He is not only, not asleep; He won't even
take a power nap! God is staying up so we can go to bed! IT IS NOT TIME
TO PANIC!

Vs. 5-6 "The Lord is your keeper; The Lord is your shade at your
right hand. ^6 ^The sun shall not strike you by day, Nor the moon by
night." These verses make us aware, that in the peak of our battles,
the Lord is so close that His very shadow will shield us from the worst
that desires to befall us! (ILLUSTRATE THIS) IT IS NOT TIME TO PANIC!

Vs. 7 "The Lord shall preserve you from ALL evil; He shall preserve
your soul." Notice, the writer says "The Lord will PRESERVE
us..." The original word here is Shaw-mar and means to hedge about
as with thorns... Protect... Guard... Keep... Save... How much evil will
the Lord preserve us from? ALL EVIL! He will preserve our mind, will,
and emotions from evil! He has us scattered, smothered and covered! IT
IS NOT TIME TO PANIC!

Vs. 8^ ^"The Lord shall preserve your going out and your coming in
From this time forth, and even forevermore." He will be with us
wherever we go and will PRESERVE us! How long has He promised to
PRESERVE us? FOREVERMORE!!! The original word here is O-lawm and
means, always... Long-lasting... Perpetual... Eternity...!!! IT IS NOT
TIME TO PANIC!

CONCLUSION:

If it is not time to panic, what are we to do?

- Get in the Word...

- Pray a hole through the sky...

- Make good on every opportunity to be with other believers...

- Teach our children to hold to God's unchanging hand...

- Make everyone we know rapture ready...

- Lift up our heads for our redemption is drawing nigh...
