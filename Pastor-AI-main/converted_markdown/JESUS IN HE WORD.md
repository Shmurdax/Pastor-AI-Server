# JESUS IN THE WORD

John 1:1-5 "In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God,
and the Word was God. ^2 ^He was in the beginning with God. ^3 ^All
things were made through Him, and without Him nothing was made that was
made. ^4 ^In Him was life, and the life was the light of
men. ^5 ^And the light shines in the darkness, and the darkness did
not comprehend it."

The miracle of the incarnation of Christ is incapsulated in Galatians
4:4 (KJV) "But when the fullness of time was come, God sent forth
[his Son]{.underline}, [made of a woman]{.underline}, made under the
law."

- God\'s Son became Mary's Baby...

- A child was born, but a son was given....

- The Lion of the Tribe of Judah became a lamb slain from the foundation
 of the world... (From everlasting to everlasting -- Eternal Priesthood
 after the order -- Before Abraham was, I am)

- An equal to God became a lowly servant of all mankind...

- The Ancient of Days became an infant born in the stable of
 Bethlehem...

- He placed himself in the hands of man, that man might place himself in
 the hands of God... (We are commanded to cast ourselves upon the
 mercies of God -- His mercies are new every morning)

- He often experienced hunger even though He was and is the Bread of
 Life...

- He used candles as a light in the darkness, but He gave light to the
 world three days before there was a son, moon, or stars...

- He walked, rode a donkey or sailed in a ship everywhere He went, yet
 He rides on the wings of the wind...

- He created the oceans, rivers, lakes, as well as the fountains of the
 deep. Yet in the Word He asked a woman of ill repute for a drink of
 water...

- In His earthly sojourn, He could only be in one place at a time, but
 He was and is God omnipresent...

- In His earthly visit He had to rely on the facts that He could see,
 but we know He is God omniscient...

- He wept at the tomb of His friend Lazarus, but then called Lazarus
 back to life...

- While He lived here, He tolerated injustice and aggression, but we
 know full well that He is going to tread out the wine press of the
 wrath of God, and break mighty armies across His knees...

- He only lived thirty-three- and one-half years, yet He is the Father
 of Eternity...

- While He broke under the weight of a cross, we know He is God
 omnipotent...

- While He was led as a lamb to the slaughter, we know He was really the
 Lion of the Tribe of Judah...

- He died a cruel death on the cross, while simultaneously abolishing
 death all together...

- His mother called Him Jesus, but His father gave Him a name above all
 other names...

IN TIMES OF TROUBLE AND DISAPPOINTMENT HE HAS PROMISED TO UPHOLD US!

Matthew 11:28-30 (NKJV) "Come to Me, all you who labor and are heavy
laden, and I will give you rest. ^29 ^Take My yoke upon you and learn
from Me, for I am gentle and lowly in heart, and you will find rest for
your souls. ^30 ^For My yoke is easy and My burden is light."

Stand on the Word!

HE HAS PROMISED VICTORY TO THOSE WHO ARE ENGAGED IN BATTLE!

Isaiah 54:17 (NKJV) "No weapon formed against you shall prosper, and
every tongue which rises against you in judgment You shall condemn.
This is the heritage of the servants of the Lord, and their
righteousness is from Me," Says the Lord."

1 John 4:4 (KJV) "Ye are of God, little children, and have overcome
them: because greater is he that is in you, than he that is in the
world."

Stand on the Word!

Hebrews 13:5 (NKJV) "He Himself has said, "I will never leave you nor

Stand on the Word!
