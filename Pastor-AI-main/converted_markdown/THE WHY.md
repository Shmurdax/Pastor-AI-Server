Sermon #3

January 30, 2022

INTRO: It is absolutely imperative to be "In Him" to
understand and fulfill our "WHY".

At Athens Paul said that it is, "In Him we live and move and have our
being; For we are also His offspring." Paul's words on this subject
sound very similar to the words of the prophet Jeremiah several hundred
years prior. Jeremiah 1:4-5 "Then the word of the Lord came to me,
saying, 5 Before I formed you in the womb I knew \[and\] approved of you
\[as My chosen instrument\], and before you were born I separated and
set you apart, consecrating you; \[and\] I appointed you as a prophet to
the nations."

The writer of Acts weighs in on the subject stating in Acts 17:26
"From one man, Adam, he made every man and woman and every race of
humanity, and he spread us over all the earth. He sets the boundaries of
people and nations, determining their appointed times in history."

The Psalmist makes it even more personal in Psalm 139:13 "You did
form my inward parts; You did knit me together in my mother's womb."

The Bible is very clear on the subject of our "WHY". We come to this
earth and live until death takes us out. The last enemy to be destroyed
will be death. I hate death -- I detest funeral homes -- I have a hard
time visiting the graves of my departed loved ones! Death is my enemy,
and I am hoping to cheat death through the rapture!

I must tell you today, that there is a fate worse than death, and that
is living a life devoid of purpose! Living without knowing "WHY" we are
here is a terrible waste of time, energy, and resources! I lived 61
years without knowing my "WHY"! My "WHY" is, "I innovate so others can
have a greater generational impact!"

It is my opinion, that the deepest craving of the human spirit is to
find a sense of significance and relevance. Without purpose, victories
are short-lived and defeats feel permanent. Without purpose, life has no
meaning.

Where there is no purpose:

- There is no self-control...

- There is no moral conviction...

- There are no ethical boundaries...

- There is very little sense of responsibility...

THE "WHY" IS THE SOURCE OF FULFILLMENT

Everything in life has a purpose. Everyone on this planet was born with
and for a purpose. It is this purpose that is the only source of
meaning.

Without purpose, life is an experiment or a haphazard journey that
results in:

- Failure...

- Frustration...

- Disappointment...

Without purpose, life is subjective, or it is a trial-and-error game,
that is ruled by environmental influences, and circumstances of the
moment.

In the absence of purpose:

- Time has no meaning...

- Energy has no reason...

- Activity has no precision...

If we discover our "WHY" and filter everything through the "WHY" life
is:

- Full...

- Effective...

- Rewarding...

Discovering the "WHY" is essential because:

- It brings into view the original intent for the creation of a thing...

- It gets us focused on the end for which the means exists...

- It reveals the destination that prompts the journey...

- It defines the aspiration for the inspiration...

THE "WHY" GIVES CONTEXT TO THE JOURNEY

Every product is produced on purpose, for a purpose.

"We are His workmanship..." If we were created on purpose for a
purpose, then it stands to reason, we must strive to be who we were born
to be. What was the reason for which you were born? If you don't know,
don't panic! We are going to help everyone in this church who desires to
know the "WHY" of their lives, to discover just that this year!

If we discover, and become focused on the "WHY," concern for doing the
right thing, rather than just doing things right, must always guide us.
Proverbs 20:5 "The purposes of a person's heart are deep waters, but
he who has insight, draws them out."

The context of the "WHY" looks like this:

- Plans may change, but the "WHY" never changes...

- The journey has many twists and turns, but the "WHY" remains the
 same...

- The road of life has many intersections, but the "WHY" provides the
 GPS for direction...

- The path we travel includes multiple detours, but the "WHY" gently
 guides us back to the assigned destination...

THE JOURNEY WAS DESIGNED FOR OUR "WHY"

Most people believe the road ahead is coincidental -- that life just
happens, and we must figure out how to respond to it. I disagree with
that view of life. Our journey was designed for our "WHY".

Let me give us some life-changing truths:

- We are perfect for our purpose...

- The path we are on was designed for us...

- We were sent here for such a time as this...

- We are capable of fulfilling our purpose...

- We can hear him say, "Well done, good and faithful servant..."

We are the way we are, because of who we are!

Our births are evidence, that our purpose is necessary!

We are chosen! He (GOD) chose us! Ephesians 1:4 "He CHOSE us in Him
before the foundation of the world..." We were "Chosen BEFORE the
foundations of the world were laid..." The Greek word for Chosen is,
"Eklegomai". Ek = out LEGO = Logos (The whole written and spoken Word)
MAI = I use or, I am used.

This means:

- We are a word from God... Our lives are to be a word out of the Word.

- We were chosen out of eternity past... We were sent from eternity into
 time because God had a specific reason for our being here right now!

- We are here to speak about God, and for God... What are our lives
 saying?

CONCLUSION:

The word for "Chosen" is Eklegomai = To pick out or to choose. A
better rendering of this word is to say that "God chose us out of
Himself." Jesus proceeded from the Father, and so has every son and
daughter of God been pulled out of the Father, and sent into the earth
-- This is why the Word states that we were made "In the image" of

Think of this :

- God picked Jesus...

- God picked the Children of Israel...

- God picked, prophets, kings and priests...

INTRO: You may be wondering what in the world the Christmas story
has to do with your "WHY". There are three things in this passage that
will determine whether we discover and live out our "WHY":

- The Word for the Why...

- The Favor for the Why...

- The Obedience to live out the Why...

THE WORD FOR THE WHY

In modern church culture everyone wants a Word from the Lord. Some
people will travel half way around the world to get a Word from the
Lord.

Make no mistake about it, a Word from the Lord is essential to our
"WHY". There will be no "WHY" without a Word from the Lord.

I agree, that a Word from the Lord is important in various seasons of
our lives, but the truth is, we have His Word in writing! It is called
the Bible:

- 66 books...

- 1,189 chapters...

- 23,145 verses...

- 783,137 words...

- 1,050 commands...

- 8,810 promises... (7,487 are direct promises from God to humankind)

When we need a Word, we should go to the written Word first. All other
words we receive should do two things:

- Confirm what the Lord has already been saying to us...

- Agree with the Word He has already given to us in the Bible...

Charles Spurgeon said, "A bible that is falling apart usually belongs
to someone who is not."

When we have a word from the Lord, we should treat it as a sacred trust.
Psalm 119:11 "Thy word have I hid in my heart that I might not sin
against you." Many people know the Word but continue to sin. If one
can read and understand the Word, yet continue to sin, what is this
scripture speaking of? Let's break this scripture down a bit:

- "Word" = Hebrew -- Imrah -- The Greek word is Rhema. These words
 are different than the Hebrew word Dabar, which in the Greek is Logos.
 Imrah is a personal, particular, whispered word. It is a secret
 conversation from the Lord's lips, to our ears. These Hebrew and Greek
 words are not to be confused with the Hebrew word Dabar, which in
 Greek is Logos. Logos is a general word -- Its principles are for
 all - The Commandments are a Dabar, Logos word -- A Dabar, Logos word
 is a universal word -- It is a written word - It is a word that must
 be proclaimed... Make no mistake about it, the Dabar, Logos word is
 powerful and must be center stage in the life of one who wishes to
 live out their "WHY", but the Imrah, Rhema word has a powerful place
 as well, for those who hope to discover and live out their "WHY". Mary
 knew the Logos Word, but it was the Rhema Word that made this radical
 adjustment to her life, and caused her to live out her "WHY".

- "Hid" = In the Hebrew, this word is Taman, and in the Greek, it
 is Thesaurus - What is a thesaurus? It's a treasury of words. A
 thesaurus is all the words in a language - The antonyms - The synonyms
 -The nouns -- The adjectives -- The verbs -- The Adverbs --

> The writer is saying, "I take all of your Imrah, Rhema words and put
> them in the safety deposit box of my heart..." We don't place junk in
> safety deposit boxes, we place highly valuable documents in our safety
> deposit boxes -- Susan and I keep a written copy of all Rhema words --
> They create a sacred echo.

- The writer say's, I do this so that I will not "Sin" against Him
 -- "Sin" = The Hebrew word is Chatta, and in the Greek it is
 Hamartia -- This simply means "To miss the mark." - We could
 paraphrase this scripture to say, "The divine design You have spoken
 to me... I have treasured it in my innermost being... Because of this,
 I will hit the bullseye of your purpose and calling on my life."

This scripture is saying that "If we treat His Word as a sacred trust,
it will cause our lives to be directed into the purpose He has for us."

Mary treated the WORD from the angel as a sacred trust and it became the
guiding principle for her life for the next thirty-five years!

THE FAVOR FOR THE WHY

When Gabriel told Mary, she was "highly favored," he used the
Greek word Charitoo. Charitoo means:

- Grace...

- Delight...

- Pleasure...

- Goodwill...

- Lovingkindness...

The only other place this word is used is in Ephesians 1:6 "To the
praise of his glorious grace, with which he has [blessed]{.underline} us
in the Beloved." We could paraphrase this to say, "Through His
glorious grace, He has Charitooed us in the beloved." Look at your
neighbor and say, "Charitoo to you!"

Without offending our former or current Catholic friends, the same favor
that was on Mary to become the vehicle that brought God's purpose into
the earth, is the exact same favor upon our "WHY".

Have you ever thought of yourself as someone as special as Mary? If not,
why not? We are just like Mary! We have been chosen to carry the seed of
His purpose (Our WHY), and to birth it in the earth!

God has Charitooed us in Christ! Charitoo to you!

We have been chosen to carry our own Rhema word! That whispered,
specific word, that we have hidden in our hearts, so that we can give
birth to it in the earth!

We have been given the favor to carry out our "WHY", the question is,
will we?

THE OBEDIENCE TO LIVE OUT THE WHY

Notice that Mary "Pondered this" -- Where? "In her heart!" "Thy
word have I hid in my heart that I might not sin against thee."

Our hearts are where the battle for our "WHY" is won or lost! Once Mary
"Pondered -- Considered -- this in her heart" she said, "Be it
unto me according to thy (Rhema) Word...."

No rhema word will ever be given, without the Dunamis to carry it
out!!!!!!!! If we have a word and know our "WHY" He will furnish the
Dunamis to do it!

The reason Mary pondered this in her heart is because she was
"troubled" by the words the angel was speaking. This doesn't mean
she was upset by the words of the angel, it means "she was surprised to
hear such extravagant words said to her, and about her."

Mary was being asked to be obedient to an extravagant word being spoken
over her life. Right here, she was being introduced to her "WHY".

Think of this, Mary's "WHY" would be to take on the role of the
matriarch of the savior's lineage! To be the Mother to God, playing a
role as the savior of the world! No pressure!

Mary didn't have to:

- Pray concerning her decision...

- Council with the Rabbi...

<!-- -->

- Get the intercessors involved...

- Do any research to make her decision...

- Get back to the angel about it...

- Have a church business meeting to vote on it...

She simply became obedient to her "WHY", and a peasant girl from
Nazareth, who would have lived and died in obscurity, is made world
famous by her "WHY"!!! Now 2,000 years later, I am preaching about her
thousands of miles from Nazareth, in the world class city of Houston,
Texas.

Here is where we get involved... Everything we have talked about up
until this point, God did on His own... We can all agree on the wonder
of such an extravagant word being spoken over a peasant girl from
Nazareth... BUT our "WHY" carries the Rhema Word from the same God!

We are chosen by Almighty God to carry out our own extravagant, Rhema
Word, in the earth!

- Do we know what that word is in our lives?

- Do we want to know what that word is in our lives?

- Are we willing to obediently to carry out the "WHY" no matter how
 extravagant it seems to be?

God has:

- A Word for our "WHY"...

- The Favor for our "WHY"...

The discovery of, and obedience to live out our "WHY" is totally on us!

CONCLUSION:

Mary:

- Exhibited Faith...

- Received the Word from the angel...

- Endured suspicion and ridicule...

- Birthed the Word in due time...

- Walked out the process of raising Jesus...

- Never received a second Word from the angel...

Mary received one Word from the angel and followed it for 33 1/2 years!
She never went to another meeting looking for someone to "confirm" the
word of the Lord to her!

How many words are we going to need before we discover our "WHY" and
walk it out?

GOD IS WHO HE IS

I am going to make one of the simplest and most complex statements you
have ever heard: "God is God..." I could preach the rest of my life on
that one statement and never exhaust the knowledge of who He is, and
never cover the same ground twice. If you really think about it, it is
mind blowing.

God lives in eternity - We live in time. Eternity transcends time -Time
is something God created and He is not subject to it:

- In the beginning -- God was already there...

- Before there was a calendar, a watch, or a tic toc -- God was there...

- Before there was a Monday, Tuesday, or Wednesday -- God was there...

- Before there was an earth -- God was there...

- Before there was a sun, moon, or stars -- God was there...

- Before the sun rose or set -- God was there...

- Before the moon glistened against a starry sky -- God was there...

- Before there was an evening or morning -- God was there...

- Before there was an angel or devil -- God was there...

- Before there was a created being -- A man, woman, boy, or girl -- God
 was there...

Eternity supersedes time. God created everything. The supernatural
created the natural.

David said "He is from everlasting to everlasting."

Before there was a

- When...

- Where...

- Who...

- What...

- Or how... He was already God!

Before there was anyone there to tell Him He was God - He was God.

- As far back as I can see -- God is there...

- As far ahead as I can see -- God is there...

Whatever you are asking God to do in time, it's already settled in
eternity. If God created time, we shouldn't take life for granted. If
God created time, He is not going to work on our schedule. It may not be
according to our time line, but God will certainly help us do what He
sent us here to do -- our "WHY"

.

We get in a hurry because we are confined to time but God is eternal.
And since he operates in eternity instead of time, He does not generally
get in a hurry to get anything done. He is an on-time God!

- He never comes too late...

- He never shows up with too little...

- He is rarely early...

- BUT He is ALWAYS on time...
