# REVOLUTIONARIES

TEXT: Acts 19:23-27 "About that time there arose a great
disturbance about the Way. A silversmith named Demetrius, who made
silver shrines of Artemis, brought in a lot of business for the
craftsmen there. He called them together, along with the workers in
related trades, and said: "you know, my friends, that we receive a good
income from this business, and you see how this fellow Paul has
convinced and led astray large numbers of people here in Ephesus and in
practically the whole province of Asia. He says that gods made by human
hands are no gods at all. There is danger not only that our trade will
lose its good name, but also that the temple of the great goddess
Artemis will be discredited. Soon the whole city was in an uproar. The
assembly was in confusion."

INTRO: I have literally been in and around the church my whole life
(Explain this).

I had the privilege of spending my teenage years in a church that was in
the midst of a red hot, Holy Ghost revival. This experience impacted my
life in a positive direction, and gave me an ongoing insatiable desire
to see a real move of God in the earth, otherwise known in church
circles as revival.

I have had the opportunity of being a part of some of the great revivals
of the past (Brownsville -- Malvern). I could literally morph into a
revival junkie with little to no effort whatsoever. The revivals of the
past have been more or less soaking events. They featured:

- Protracted services... (Weeks or even months in duration)

- Passionate worship...

- Sermons filled with conviction...

- Strong altar calls...

- Extended altar services...

There is certainly nothing wrong with any of this, in fact, I could live
like this indefinitely.

One of the things I remember growing up is the prediction, if not
prophecy, that there would be an end time revival. Based on this, we
(The church) have waited with baited breath, for this tsunami of the
presence of God to hit the church, so we can all sit in circles, hold
hands, and soak in the presence of the Lord. But is this really what the
reality of an end time revival will look like? I don't think so.

The Biblical prediction is that there will be an end time revival and a
great falling away simultaneously. How can such a thing be? Here is what
I am seeing happen:

- The love of many has waxed cold... (We are seeing this now -- People
 can go everywhere but to church - Covid)

- Those who seek another soaking event have become discontented with
 organized religion, because they are not experiencing what they have
 witnessed in the past... (Many of these people have intentionally
 absented themselves not only from church services, but from the body
 of Christ altogether)

- The expectation of my generation has not been fulfilled... I think
 this is because we are looking for something that God never intended
 to send. (What if what the Lord is intending to send - and what we are
 expecting is two different things?)

- When our expectation and what the Lord is trying to do is different,
 is it possible for those who are hungry for the next great revival to
 miss it altogether? (I believe the answer to this is YES!)

I believe what God is going to do in this next season is going to be
marked by the following characteristics:

- There will be tremendous numbers of people return to the God of their
 fathers and mothers... (Backsliders will return in mass numbers)

- Scores of people will be swept into the Kingdom of God...

- Scores of religious people will miss it altogether because it doesn't
 look like what they were expecting...

- This move of God will be ushered in on the shoulders of a revolution
 involving the five-fold gifts of Ephesians four... (Apostles --
 Prophets -- Evangelists -- Pastors -- Teachers)

- The five-fold will be understood by these revolutionaries as intended
 for the organized church, but more importantly for the secular
 world... (Use the Gringos story as an example)

- Because many denominations have placed so much effort into
 discrediting the need for the five-fold, they will be relegated to
 watching this great shift happen from the sidelines...

- The manifestation of the sons and daughters of God as described in
 Romans eight will occur before our very eyes...

- The Ecclesia will begin to step up to the plate, and take its proper
 place on the leading edge of a cultural shift... (Believers will begin
 to take their rightful place in the seven spheres of cultural
 influence -- Family - Religion -- Business -- Education -- Arts and
 entertainment -- Government -- Media)

In our text the Apostle Paul was engaged in a spiritual revolution and
was on the leading edge of a group of revolutionaries, who would later
be known as having "Turned the world upside down."

REVOLUTIONARIES AFFECT THE ECONOMY

A businessman from Ephesus, by the name of Demetrius, had created an
economic boom by building God's of silver and gold and selling them
throughout Asia.

The message Paul preached did not allow for such idols. Paul's preaching
had such an impact that it got the attention of Demetrius, and he warned
the Ephesians that if Paul were allowed to continue what he was doing,
it would impact the economy of Ephesus. Think about that!

The gospel message affecting the economy! Business was down at the pagan
temples because of the message Paul preached and the transforming work
of Jesus Christ.

The preaching and teaching of revolutionaries should always have an
effect on the economy, and I believe this coming revolution will do just
that! I believe:

- The church will develop entrepreneurs...

- The church will launch people who have witty inventions...

- The church will produce a workforce that can be trusted...

- The church will move the needle of gross national product...

I am convinced that this is one of the ways the end time move of God
will change society. The gospel will affect the trades of our cities.

REVOLUTIONARIES FOCUS ON WHAT THEY ARE FOR, RATHER THAN WHAT THEY ARE
AGAINST

At Ephesus, Paul was on a Pro --Jesus campaign, more than an
anti-everything else campaign. (The church has become known for its name
calling and hateful rhetoric on cultural issues)

Sadly, the great moves of the past have become known more for what they
were against, rather than what they were for! (We don't smoke, we don't
chew, and we don't run with those who do)

I grew up in a church culture that was marked more by the do not's,
rather than what we were being empowered to do. When I was growing up
God didn't have much of a sense of humor, and wasn't much fun! (Explain
this)

The truth of the matter is that those saints had the power of God on
them, but sadly it didn't do much to change the world they lived in!
(Most of their children do not even serve Jesus today) This is not
because what they had was not real, it is because the leaders of that
day never taught them to take what they were experiencing at church, and
deploy it in the seven spheres of influence in culture!

What they had was real! I experienced it for myself and it has an effect
on me to this day! If they had understood why they were having the
experience -- To take it into the world to be salt and light -- We would
be living in a completely different world today!

I believe this next move of God will be marked by what we are for, not
what we are against! Those who understand and embrace this next move of
God:

- Will take their rightful place in culture...

- Will raise Godly families in an ungodly world...

- Will run for political office, and then serve with integrity...

- Will climb to the highest places of influence in the educational
 systems of our world... (Become classroom teachers - Serve on school
 boards -- Become school administrators -- Write curriculum -- etc...)

- Will infiltrate social media platforms, and launch rival platforms...

- Write movies scripts -- Become producers -- Become actors -- etc...

- Will lead churches and religious organizations that actually do what
 the church was designed to do, rather than create a bloated religious
 bureaucracy that supports a few fat cats at the top of the food
 chain...

- Will invent things -- Launch business led by Godly principles --
 Become the next generation of billionaires... (If God is going to
 "Take the wealth of the wicked and give it to the righteous" He
 needs some righteous people who can properly manage it once they have
 it!)

REVOLUTIONARIES DIMINISH THE NEED FOR IMMORAL PRACTICES IN A SOCIETY

When God moves among his people it affects the livelihood and trade of
any vice or immorality within that city or society. (This has happened
with CT Church -- Community Transformation)

went to, and they didn\'t do it with protest marches or boycotts; they
did it through the faithful proclamation of the Gospel (The good news of
Jesus)!

The gospel is the power of God. It does not merely release God's power
-- it is the power of God! When we speak the Gospel, power comes out of
our mouths. When the good news of what God in Christ has done, comes out
of our mouth, it is like beams of light driving away darkness:

- Revolutionaries find a way to empower believers!

- Revolutionaries light up the world with their example! (When we light
 up the world, we automatically dispel darkness)

- Revolutionaries change every environment they enter! (Joseph -- His
 father's house -- Potiphar's house -- The prison -- The palace)

- Revolutionaries raise the level of everyone they touch! (When a
 revolutionary walk into a relationship, everything changes -- A
 changing tide raises and lowers the level of all boats, from a \$1,000
 flat bottom boat to a \$ M yacht -- Everything changes)
