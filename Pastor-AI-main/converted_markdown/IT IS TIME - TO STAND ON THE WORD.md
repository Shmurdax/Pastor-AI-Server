# IT IS TIME TO STAND ON THE WORD

TEXT: Matthew 24:35 (NKJV) "Heaven and earth will pass away, but
My words will by no means pass away."

INTRO: Matthew 24 is what is referred to as the Olivet Discourse.
This is a sermon preached by Jesus from the Mount of Olives just before
his substitutionary death on the cross. Of the 51 verses in Matthew 24,
all but 78 words are the words of Jesus! In this chapter the disciples
of Jesus ask a question that is currently on the lips of believers and
non-believers worldwide. That question is, "What is about to happen in
the world? When will the end of the world be?"

Jesus answers their questions by telling them what the wrap-up of the
world will look like, and quite honestly, it sounds a lot like the news
on our televisions in real time.

"Many will come in my name and saying, I am the Christ, and will
deceive many."

"There will be wars and rumors of wars."

"Nation will rise against nation, and kingdom against kingdom."

"There will be famines and pestilences and earthquakes in many
places. All these are the beginnings of sorrows,"

"Christians will be killed for the cause of Christ."

"Christians will be hated around the world."

"A spirit of offense will be in the land."

"Individuals will betray and hate one another."

"False prophets will arise and deceive many."

"A spirit of lawlessness will cover the world."

"The love of Christians for Christ will grow cold."

The gospel of the Kingdom will be preached in all the world for a
witness, and then shall the end come."

"Christ will come back in the first part of the second coming."

"The great tribulation will be initiated and the antichrist will be
revealed."

"The abomination of desolation will occur and antichrist will
double-cross the Jewish people."

"False Christs will arise with power to do miracles and will deceive
many."

"The sun will be darkened and the moon will refuse to shine."

"The powers of the heavens will be shaken."

"Jesus will come back with His Saints in the second part of the
second coming."

Verse 35 -- "Heaven and earth will pass away, but MY WORDS will not
pass away."

In these times of disenchantment and uncertainty I challenge us to stand
on the Word of the Lord!

IN TIMES OF TROUBLE AND DISAPPOINTMENT -- THE WORD OF THE LORD HAS
PROMISED TO UPHOLD US!

Matthew 11:28-30 (NKJV) "Come to Me, all you who labor and are heavy
laden, and I will give you rest. ^29 ^Take My yoke upon you and learn
from Me, for I am gentle and lowly in heart, and you will find rest for
your souls. ^30 ^For My yoke is easy and My burden is light."

It is time to stand on the Word!

WHEN WE ARE FULLY ENGAGED IN BATTLE -- THE WORD OF THE LORD HAS
PROMISED GREAT VICTORY!

Isaiah 54:17 (NKJV) "No weapon formed against you shall prosper, and
every tongue which rises against you in judgment You shall condemn.
This is the heritage of the servants of the Lord, and their
righteousness is from Me," Says the Lord."

It is time to stand on the Word!

WHEN IT LOOKS LIKE WE ARE OUTNUMBERED -- THE WORD OF THE LORD HAS
PROMISED A LOPSIDED VICTORY IN OUR FAVOR!

1 John 4:4 (KJV) "Ye are of God, little children, and have overcome
them: because greater is he that is in you, than he that is in the
world."

It is time to stand on the Word!

WHEN IT FEELS LIKE WE ARE ALL ALONE -- THE WORD OF THE LORD HAS A
NEVER-ALONE POLICY!

Hebrews 13:5 (NKJV) "He Himself has said, "I will never leave you nor
forsake you." ^6 ^So we may boldly say: "The Lord is my helper; I will
not fear. What can man do to me?"

It is time to stand on the Word!

WHEN IT APPEARS THAT EVERY THING WE HAVE WORKED FOR WILL BE DESTROYED
-- THE WORD OF THE LORD HAS A DOUBLE FOR YOUR TROUBLE PROMISE!

Haggai 2:9 (NKJV) "'The glory of this latter temple shall
be greater than the former,' says the Lord of hosts. 'And in this place
I will give peace,' says the Lord of hosts."

(Job got double for his trouble)

It is time to stand on the Word!

THOUGH THE WORLD IS IN THE WRAP UP STAGE -- THE WORD OF THE LORD HAS
PROMISED THAT HE WILL COME AGAIN!

John 14:1-6 (NKJV) "Let not your heart be troubled; you believe in
God, believe also in Me. ^2 ^In My Father's house are many mansions;
if it were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for
you. ^3 ^And if I go and prepare a place for you, I will come again and
receive you to Myself; that where I am, there you may be also. ^4 ^And
where I go you know, and the way you know." ^5 ^Thomas said to Him,
"Lord, we do not know where You are going, and how can we know the way?"
^6 ^Jesus said to him, "I am the way, the truth, and the life. No one
comes to the Father except through Me."

It is time to stand on the Word!

CONCLUSION:

We are living in dangerous times -- And that is a fact, Jack! Even
though we are living in dangerous times we should make a conscious
effort every day to stand on the Word! The Word is the only thing that
is going to stand!

- Governments will collapse -- The Word of the Lord will stand forever!

- Politicians will fail to have answers -- The Word of the Lord will
 stand forever!

- Financial systems will fail -- The Word of the Lord will stand
 forever!

- Health solutions will become inadequate -- The Word of the Lord will
 stand forever!

- Ecosystems will falter and fail -- The Word of the Lord will stand
 forever!

When the angel appeared to Mary and told her of the plan of God to bring
Jesus into the world, Mary's response was, "Be it unto me according
to Thy Word!"

This should be our prayer in these last days -- "Be it unto me
according to Thy Word!" It is time to stand on the Word!
