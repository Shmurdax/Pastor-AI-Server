# THE G.O.A.T.

The Greatest of All Time

TEXT: Isaiah 9:6-8 (NKJV) "For unto us a Child is born, Unto us a
Son is given; And the government will be upon His shoulder. And His name
will be called [Wonderful Counselor]{.underline}, [Mighty
God]{.underline}, [Everlasting Father]{.underline}, [Prince of
Peace]{.underline}. Of the increase of His government and peace
There will be no end, Upon the throne of David and over His kingdom, To
order it and establish it with judgment and justice From that time
forward, even forever. The zeal of the Lord of hosts will perform
this."

INTRO: The prophet Isaiah, writing seven hundred years before Jesus
was born, prophesied that Messiah would have at least four names:
"[Wonderful Counselor]{.underline}, [Mighty God]{.underline},
[Everlasting Father]{.underline}, [Prince of Peace]{.underline}."

THE GREATEST OF ALL TIME IS A WONDERFUL COUNSELOR

Wonderful Counselor literally means "a wonder of a counselor."

The word, Wonderful, means "astonishing" or "extraordinary." A
literal translation would be that He is an "Astonishing advisor."

While human counselors are limited by knowledge, this Wonderful
Counselor:

- Has never seen a problem He could not resolve...

- Has never seen a hurt He could not heal...

- Has never seen a battle He could not win...

- Has never seen a question He could not answer...

- Has never seen an obligation He could not meet...

- Has never seen a failure He could not fix...

- Has never seen a loss He could not restore...

No wonder the Prophet Isaiah called Him a "Wonderful Counselor." I
declare this "Wonderful Counselor" to be The Greatest Of All Time!

THE GREATEST OF ALL TIME IS THE MIGHTY GOD

The word translated "Mighty" is the word "gibor" meaning
"strong one" or "powerful valiant warrior." Therefore, Jesus as
Mighty God becomes the God who fights for his people!

- He fights for the righteous...

- He fights for righteousness...

- He fights to save lives...

- He fights to save the lost...

- He fights to save marriages...

- He fights to save churches...

- He fights to save you and me...

If He is the Mighty God:

- We are on the winning team...

- He will grant to us victory for every battle...

- There is nothing impossible for Him...

- There is nothing impossible for us...

- The Kingdom is waiting for the victorious...

No wonder the prophet Isaiah said "He shall be called Mighty God."
I declare this "Mighty God" to be The Greatest Of All Time!

THE GREATEST OF ALL TIME IS THE EVERLASTING FATHER

In the Hebrew, the phrase "Everlasting Father," literally means,
"the Father of Eternity."

In a very literal sense, He is the possessor of eternity, eternity
belongs to Him! As a result, He gives us eternal life because it is His
to give! (Larissa telling a Board Member "My Daddy owns this whole
place!")

Because He is our Everlasting Father:

- When we fail, He forgives us!

- When we fall, He lifts us up!

- When we are weak, He is strong!

- When we are lost, He is the Way!

- When we are afraid, He is our courage!

- When we stumble, He steadies us!

- When we are broken, He mends us!

- When we are blind, He gives us sight!

- When we are hungry, He feeds us!

- When we face trials, He is with us!

- When we face persecution, He shields us!

- When we face problems, He comforts us!

- When we encounter loss, He provides for us!

The ultimate goal of the everlasting father is a relationship with us!
Someone said, "A truly rich man is one whose children run into his arms
when his hands are empty."

I would follow this Everlasting Father if He had nothing to give! No
wonder the Prophet Isaiah called Him the "Everlasting Father." I
declare this "Everlasting Father" to be The Greatest Of All Time!

THE GREATEST OF ALL TIME IS THE PRINCE OF PEACE

Because we live in a democracy it is difficult for us to properly
understand the gravity of what the role of a prince entails. Under a
monarchy a prince:

- Is the son of a King...

- Is heir to the throne and the kingdom...

- Possesses authority over military forces...

- Determines how much authority his subjects will have...

- Is to be feared and loved simultaneously...

- Is justified in using whatever force is necessary to retain political
 control...

What does this look like in the Kingdom of God?

- Jesus is the only begotten Son of God... "For God so loved the
 world that He gave His only begotten son..."

- Jesus is heir to the throne and the Kingdom... "Heirs of God and
 joint heirs with Jesus Christ..."

- Jesus has been given charge of everything... "All power has been
 given to me in Heaven and in earth."

- Jesus determines how much authority believers will be able to
 exercise... "I give unto you power to tread upon serpents and
 scorpions, and over all the power of the enemy..."

- Jesus is to be feared and loved simultaneously... "The fear of the
 Lord is the beginning of wisdom." "You shall love the Lord your God
 with all your heart, mind, soul and strength."

- Jesus is justified in using whatever force is necessary to maintain
 control of Gods plan for the ages... "He will rule until He has put
 all things under His feet." "Every knee shall bow..."

The literal meaning of the phrase "Prince of Peace" = "The
prince who's coming brings peace."

He is the Prince whose coming brings peace!

- In the past, his coming made peace with God! His death on the cross
 satisfied the offence of sin, and set man in right relationship with
 God!

- In the present, those who come to him find peace in their heart and
 live at peace with others!

- In the future, his second coming will usher in a dispensation of
 peace! William Gladstone said, "We look forward to the time when the
 Power of Love will replace the Love of Power.  Then will our world
 know the blessings of peace."

If we are truly in relationship with the Prince whose coming brings
peace, we cannot allow war to stay in our hearts!

In their book, "Let there be peace on earth" Seymour Miller and Jill
Jackson said, "Let there be peace on earth and let it begin with me."

No wonder Isaiah declared Him to be "The Prince whose coming brings
peace!" I declare Him to be The Greatest Of All Time!

CONCLUSION:

The Greatest Of All Time is:

- The Wonderful Counselor...

- The Mighty God...

- The Everlasting Father...

- The Prince of Peace...
