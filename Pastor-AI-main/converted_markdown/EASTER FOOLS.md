# EASTER FOOLS

INTRO: Welcome to Easter Fools Day! Rarely does a thing like this
happen that Easter and April Fools day fall on the same day! In fact,
the last time this occurred was in 1956 and it will not recur again
until 2029 and then in 2040. 2040 will be the last time this will occur
in this century! Since this happens so rarely I could not resist the
temptation to marry the two ideas together.

If you feel I am being sacrilegious you need to lighten up so the stress
of this world won't kill you before your time!

April Fools Day is all about a joke!

- On April 1, 2016 Cottonelle Corporation Tweeted they were about to
 introduce left-handed toilet paper...

- On April 1, 1996 Taco Bell ran an ad purporting to have purchased the
 Liberty Bell for \$50,000 in an effort to help relieve the national
 debt...

- On April 1, 1962 the Swedish National Network ran a TV spot featuring
 an expert who reported that it's black and white broadcasts could be
 seen in color by viewing the TV set through nylon stockings...

(My greatest April Fools joke ever was played on staff!)

Easter is not funny, right? Easter is serious stuff so how can we marry
the two thoughts together? Just hang with me as I guide you through
this...

That first Easter dawned bright and early in the Garden Tomb outside
Jerusalem:

- Jesus had been murdered at the hands of wicked and cruel men and a
 religious system run amuck...

- His body had been laid in a borrowed tomb on the outskirts of
 Jerusalem, not far from where He had been crucified...

- The disciples were so grief stricken that they were invisible at the
 cross...

- The women were reluctantly making their way to the cemetery...

- The women encountered two angels that let them know that Jesus was not
 dead but alive...

- Mary Magdalene was so distraught that she didn't even recognize Him,
 supposing Him to be the gardener...

It is neither a stretch nor a slight to say that the resurrection was a
joke---and a very good one at that!!! What better way could Jesus have
mocked a world who had brutally murdered Him than to rise from the dead?

The resurrection was the perfect joke at the expense of those who wanted
Jesus dead!

Fool = Someone who is unwise, lacks good sense and judgment.

## I. THE FOOL HAS SAID IN HIS HEART THERE IS NO GOD...

Psalm 53:1 (NKJV) "The fool has said in his heart, "There is no God."
They are corrupt, and have done abominable iniquity; There is none who
does good." Notice the Scripture does not state that the fool said
these words out loud but has said, "in his heart, there is no
God."

There are only 450 -- 500 million atheists in the world today! There are
more than seven billion people on the earth so less than 7% of the
world's population identify themselves as atheists. (We live in a world
full of Gods)

Probably no one in this room would place themselves in the atheist
category this morning but look at what the Bible say's: "The fool has
said [in his heart]{.underline}, there is no God..." People who live
as if there is on God: (Psalm 53:1)

- Are corrupt... (Think nothing of participating in acts of corruption
 -- Sorry they got caught, not for what they did)

- Do abominable things... (Idolatry -- Condemning the just -- Adultery -
 Homosexuality -- Sacrifices of the wicked -- A proud look -- A lying
 tongue -- An unjust balance or scale -- The Bible lists 57 things that
 are an abomination to the Lord)

- Commit iniquity... Iniquity means to bend, make crooked or pervert.
 This is making wrong right and right wrong. This is the spirit of the
 age.

You do not have to verbalize the words that there is no God in order to
be a fool. If you embrace the aforementioned lifestyle you are "A
fool who has said [in his heart]{.underline}, there is no God." It is
possible for one to say they believe in God or that they are a follower
of Christ, yet, their lifestyle say's something completely different.

Are you a "Fool who has [said in his heart]{.underline}, there is no
God?"

## II. THE DEVIL AND HIS CRONIES WERE FOOLS FOR HAVING JESUS
CRUCIFIED...

I Corinthians 2:14 (NKJV) "The natural man does not receive the
things of the Spirit of God, for they are foolishness to him; nor can he
know them, because they are spiritually discerned."

1 Corinthians 2:6-8 (NKJV) "We speak wisdom among those who are
mature, yet not the wisdom of this age, nor of the rulers of this age,
who are coming to nothing. 7 But we speak the wisdom of God in a
mystery, the hidden wisdom which God ordained before the ages for our
glory, 8 which none of the rulers of this age knew; for had they known,
they would not have crucified the Lord of glory."

Trust me, the devil knew exactly who he was crucifying! No one knows who
Jesus is better than the devil!

According to the Apostle Paul the wisest men of Jesus day did not know
who they were crucifying! Why? Because they were fools!

The devil proved himself to be a fool by inciting the crowd to crucify
Jesus in spite of the fact he knew exactly who Jesus was, and knew every
prophecy of his resurrection!

Why would he do such a thing? Because his foolishness convinced him that
he could prevent the resurrection from happening! What a fool! This was
no doubt the greatest miscalculation of all time!

The smartest men and religious leaders of Jesus day proved themselves to
be mere fools by not recognizing who Jesus was despite all of the
prophecies He fulfilled through:

- His virgin birth...

- Sinless life...

- Substitutionary death...

- Unprecedented resurrection...

- Very public ascension...

What fools! Yet there are people sitting in this very room today that
are taking equally foolish actions by not accepting Jesus as the savior
of their lives and following His Bible as the roadmap for their lives!

sold out to the idea of following Christ! No matter what!
