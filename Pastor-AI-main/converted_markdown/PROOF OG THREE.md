The thing that all these scriptures have in common of course are the use
of the word [ONE]{.underline}. Each instance of the usage of the word
[ONE]{.underline} in all of these scriptures comes from the same root
Greek word- HEIS- (pronounced --HISE). The meaning of HEIS is quite
literally to be one. This has everything to do with purpose, unity, and
harmony but has absolutely nothing to do with physical merge of two or
more personalities occupying the same body. When you get into two or
more personalities occupying the same body you then have what is known
as a split personality which borders on demon possession.
