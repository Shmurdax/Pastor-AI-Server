Elijah, by digging that trench around that altar, separated the altar

My friend I believe that two things are represented here that are of
utmost importance in our lives today!

1. We need time alone with God, completely separated from things!

Psalms 91:1

Isaiah 40:31

Moses spent time alone with God! We have record of 80 days and nights he
fasted and prayed. God made known his way unto mores his acts unto the
children of Israel.

Jesus spent time alone with God, separated from everything around Him!
And when He left that solitary place he was able to overcome the Flesh
and resist the enemy! The Devil leaveth Him alone for a season.

If Jesus, being the Son of God, needed time alone with his father, don't
you think that it is absolutely necessary for us to find time to spend
with God?

Time at church, fellowship with other believers in corporate prayer,
doing religious activities will NEVER take the place of time alone in
the presence of God!
