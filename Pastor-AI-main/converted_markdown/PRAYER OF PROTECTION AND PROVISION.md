# PRAYER OF PROTECTION AND PROVISION

[]{.mark}

I apply the Blood of Jesus to my family: (call the name of all family
members). I also apply Your Blood to everything that belongs to us. I
pray the Blood will destroy everything unlike itself in our lives today.

I pray that you would bless us indeed, enlarge our territory, and our
lives will bring no pain to you or others. Protect us and provide for
us, make a way where there seems to be no way in our lives. I ask that
You help us to walk in our identity and live out our purpose. May not
one thing You have assigned to us, go undone.

I pray that my family circle will be unbroken in heaven, not one of my
descendants will perish -- ever! In Jesus name, Amen
