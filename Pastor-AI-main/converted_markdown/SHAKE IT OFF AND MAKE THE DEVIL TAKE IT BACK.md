# SHAKE IT OFF

AND MAKE THE DEVIL TAKE IT BACK

TEXT: Acts 28:1-5 (NKJV) "Now when they had escaped, they then
found out that the island was called Malta. And the natives showed us
unusual kindness; for they kindled a fire and made us all welcome,
because of the rain that was falling and because of the cold. But
when Paul had gathered a bundle of sticks and laid them on the fire, a
viper came out because of the heat, and fastened on his hand. So when
the natives saw the creature hanging from his hand, they said to one
another, "No doubt this man is a murderer, whom, though he has escaped
the sea, yet justice does not allow to live." But he shook off the
creature into the fire and suffered no harm."

James 4:7-10 (NKJV) "Submit to God. Resist the devil and he will flee
from you. Draw near to God and He will draw near to you. Cleanse your
hands, you sinners; and purify your hearts, you double-minded. Lament
and mourn and weep! Let your laughter be turned to mourning and your joy
to gloom. Humble yourselves in the sight of the Lord, and He will
lift you up."

INTRO: For far too long the people of God have walked around upset,
discouraged, disgusted, depressed, restless, battle fatigued, and
running in fear. The reason this has happened is that we have allowed
the enemy to harass, agitate, aggravate, humiliate, rob, and lie to us.

I for one, think it is high time you and I as people of God, know our
position in God, and realize the power and resources available to us
when the enemy tries to over-power us! As believers we are under no
obligation to accept all the junk the devil tries to place on our
proverbial wagon! We can, shake it off and make the devil take it back!

Notice our text; the Apostle Paul is approaching the end of his earthly
journey, however the race is not yet completely over, there are still a
few battles to fight before the lights go out!

Paul was most certainly not afraid to die! 2 Corinthians 5:6-8 (NKJV)
"So we are always confident, knowing that [while we are at home in
the body we are absent from the Lord]{.underline}. For we walk by
faith, not by sight. We are confident, yes, [well pleased rather to

Lord]{.underline}." 2 Timothy 4:6-8 (NKJV) "I am already being
poured out as a drink offering, and the time of my departure is at hand.

to [live is Christ]{.underline}, and to die is gain." When the snake
bite on the Island of Malta did not kill him, he prayed for the sick.
Many were healed and the entire island came to the saving knowledge of
Christ!

Have you ever had the devil to tell you he was going to kill you? I
have! That is not a very good feeling, until you begin to realize he
can't do anymore than your father will allow! Why? Because we belong to
God; we are the children of God; since we belong to God, the Lord will
direct our steps and will have the final say, on what the enemy or
anyone else will or will not be permitted to do! We should not allow the
devil threaten us with Heaven! We need to learn to shake it off and make
the devil take it back!

## III. THE PATH TO VICTORY IS NOT THAT DIFFICULT FOR THE CHILD OF GOD

How can we walk in the victory Jesus meant for us? (PLACE ON THE
SCREEN ONE AT A TIME)

- Commit our lives to Christ...

- Follow the Lord in water Baptism...

- Establish a daily routine of prayer...

- Read the Word of God on a daily basis...

- Be filled with the Spirit...

- Become a faithful attendee of a full Gospel church...

- Develop wholesome relationships with other Christians...

- Stand on the Word... Luke 4:1-12 (NKJV) "Then Jesus, being [filled
 with the Holy Spirit]{.underline}, returned from the Jordan and was
 led by the Spirit into the wilderness, being tempted for forty days
 by the devil. And in those days He ate nothing, and afterward, when
 they had ended, He was hungry. And the devil said to Him, "If You
 are the Son of God, command this stone to become bread." But Jesus
 answered him, saying, "[It is written, 'Man shall not live by bread
 alone, but by every word of God]{.underline}.' " Then the devil,
 taking Him up on a high mountain, showed Him all the kingdoms of the
 world in a moment of time. And the devil said to Him, "All this
 authority I will give You, and their glory; for this has been
 delivered to me, and I give it to whomever I wish. "Therefore, if
 You will worship before me, all will be Yours." And Jesus answered
 and said to him, "Get behind Me, Satan! [For it is written, 'You shall
 worship the [Lord]{.smallcaps} your God, and Him only you shall
 serve.]{.underline}' " Then he brought Him to Jerusalem, set Him on
 the pinnacle of the temple, and said to Him, "If You are the Son of
 God, throw Yourself down from here. "For [it is
 written]{.underline}: 'He shall give His angels charge over you, To
 keep you,' "and, 'In their hands they shall bear you up, Lest you
 dash your foot against a stone.' " And Jesus answered and said to
 him, "It has been said, '[You shall not tempt the [Lord]{.smallcaps}
 your God]{.underline}.' " Now when the devil had ended every
 temptation, he departed from Him until an opportune time."

The devil will huff and puff and blow against your house, but remember
he's a blow-hard! Someone said, "Fear knocked on the door, faith
answered and no one was there!" Shake it off and make the devil take it
back!
