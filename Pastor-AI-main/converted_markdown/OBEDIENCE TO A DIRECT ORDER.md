# OBEDIENCE TO A DIRECT ORDER

(April 24, 2022)

TEXT: Mark 1:9-11 (NKJV) "It came to pass in those days that
Jesus came from Nazareth of Galilee, and was baptized by John in the
Jordan. And immediately, coming up from the water, He saw the heavens
parting and the Spirit descending upon Him like a dove. Then a voice
came from heaven, "You are My beloved Son, in whom I am well
pleased."

Mark 16:15-16 (NKJV) "He said to them, "Go into all the world and
preach the gospel to every creature. 16"He who believes and is baptized
will be saved; but he who does not believe will be condemned."

Colossians 2:12 (NKJV) "Buried with Him in baptism, in which you also
were raised with Him through faith in the working of God, who raised Him
from the dead."

INTRO: Today we are giving many of you the opportunity to be
baptized in water. This is an act that some believe is essential to
salvation, and at the same time others see it as a silly little ritual
of the church.

So where is the truth in the matter? Let me attempt to answer that
question with a few simple statements:

- Water cannot save us... (Only the blood cleanses from sin)

- Jesus set the example for us to follow... (Jesus said to John the
 Baptist "I need to be baptized by you..."

- Jesus directly commanded us to be water baptized... (If He told us to
 do so, this must be a pretty important action on our part)

Let's take a few minutes and look at why we have been commanded to be
baptized and what it accomplishes in our lives.

## I. WATER BAPTISM IS AN ACT RESERVED FOR THOSE WHO HAVE ACCEPTED CHRIST
AS THEIR SAVIOR Water baptism is meaningless unless it is the act of
one who has accepted the Lord Jesus Christ as their personal Savior.
Once a person has decided to follow Christ the next step is water
baptism.

Water baptism should occur as soon after conversion as possible.
(Phillip and the Ethiopian Eunuch) Acts 8:36-38 (NKJV) "As they went
down the road, they came to some water. And the eunuch said, "See, here
is water. What hinders me from being baptized?" Then Philip said, "If
you believe with all your heart, you may." And he answered and said, "I
believe that Jesus Christ is the Son of God." So he commanded the
chariot to stand still. And both Philip and the eunuch went down into
the water, and he baptized him."

Water baptism should receive high priority!

Look at the instance of Paul and Silas converting the Philippian Jailer.
Acts 16:30-33 (NKJV) "He (The jailer) brought them out and said,
"Sirs, what must I do to be saved?" So they said, "Believe on the Lord
Jesus Christ, and you will be saved, you and your household." Then
they spoke the word of the Lord to him and to all who were in his house.
 And he took them the same hour of the night and washed their stripes.
And immediately he and all his family were baptized."

I strongly encourage you; if you have not been baptized in water to do
so today. In so doing you will be following a direct command of the Lord
Jesus Christ!

## II. WATER BAPTISM IS AN ACT OF OBEDIENCE, NOT AN ACT OF
REGENERATION\
Mark 16:15-16 (NKJV) "He said to them, "Go into all the world and
preach the gospel to every creature. 16"He who believes and is baptized
will be saved; but he who does not believe will be condemned."

Some passages sound as though baptism itself saves, but we know from the
example of the thief on the cross that one may be saved even though he
has not had opportunity in life to be baptized. (The thief on the cross)

Once one has come to the understanding that they should be baptized they
should do so as soon as possible, so that they can fulfill a direct
order of the Lord!

## III. WATER BAPTISM IS NOT OPTIONAL FOR OBEDIENT DISCIPLES

Water baptism signifies entrance into the visible Church, just as
conversion secured entrance into the invisible Church.

Too many people view water baptism as insignificant or unimportant.
Those who do so, place a very low priority on this very important step
in the Christian life.

Even though water baptism does not save us, one cannot get much further
down the road as a disciple of Jesus until they submit to this order.
The reason the growth process of a Christian who has not been water
baptized is stunted, is because they are walking in disobedience.

Because water baptism is a gateway into identification with the church
Jesus died to build, obedience to this order is not optional!

CONCLUSION:

The message being proclaimed in water baptism is:

- The old life has left us in a filthy condition...

- We no longer choose to be identified with the old life...

- By an act of our own will, we are choosing for the old life to be
 buried in Jesus...

- We are going to be raised from the baptismal water, a new person,
 living a new lifestyle...

- It is an outward testimony of an inward work...

I strongly encourage you, if you have not obeyed the command of Jesus to
be water baptized, to do so today!
