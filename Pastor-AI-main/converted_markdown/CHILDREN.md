# RAISING GODLY CHILDREN IN AN UNGODLY WORLD

TEXT: Psalm 127:3-5 "Lo, [children are an heritage of the
Lord]{.underline}: and the fruit of the womb is his reward. As
arrows are in the hand of a mighty man; so are [children]{.underline} of
the youth. Happy is the man that hath his quiver full of them: they
shall not be ashamed, but they shall speak with the enemies in the
gate."

INTRO: David said, "Lo, [children are an heritage of the
Lord:]{.underline} and the fruit of the womb is His reward."

Please notice, "CHILDREN" are God's "HERITAGE", not mine, or
Satan's! My "CHILDREN" were not born to be defeated and destroyed by
the devil. As God's "HERITAGE", they are heirs to everything that
their heavenly Father bestows in blessings.! They were born with a
silver spoon in their mouths, so to speak!!!

Also notice, "CHILDREN" are God's reward from us, not to us!

I know the "little darlings" sometimes don't seem much like it but
they have been sent to us from God! Thus, in dedication today you have
in essence said to God that you will do your part to fulfill your
responsibility to raise your "CHILDREN" in the fear and admonition
of the Lord.

Malachi, the Prophet, says, "And did not He make one? Yet had He the
residue of the Spirit. And wherefore one? [That He might seek a Godly
seed]{.underline}. Therefore take heed to your spirit, and let none deal
treacherously against the wife of his youth."

We must understand that God is seeking a Godly family, who will dare to
produce Godly "CHILDREN"!

That seems easy enough, until you realize that you are going to be
called upon to raise them in an "UNGODLY" world. In a world where
sin is so prevalent, how can we "RAISE GODLY CHILDREN IN AN UNGODLY
WORLD?"

## I. PROVIDE YOUR CHILDREN WITH GODLY PARENTS!

A. If you are going to "PROVIDE YOUR CHILDREN WITH GODLY
PARENTS" there must be a faithful father in the home.

Abraham was a father of faith that we would do well to follow! I want to
attempt to paraphrase Romans 4:12-13 for you, "Walk in the steps of
faith that Abraham walked in. For the promise that he should become the
heir of the world was not just to him and his seed after him, through
the law. But to all of you as well, through the righteousness of
faith!"

God said of Abraham in Genesis 18:19, "For I know him, that he will
[command his children and his household after him,]{.underline} and
they shall keep the way of the Lord, to do justice and judgment;
[that the Lord may bring upon Abraham that which He hath spoken of
him]{.underline}"

You see, it is true that Abraham was a friend of God, but their is
strong scriptural evidence that their friendship caused Abraham to be a
Godly example to his "CHILDREN"!

The worst mistake any Father in this house can make is being a poor
example before your "CHILDREN"! You can probably outlive bad career
choices, poor investment choices, poor fitness choices, as well as many
other poor choices, but you will not live long enough to outlive the
negative things your "CHILDREN" witness every day!

As a faithful father, I must love righteousness, justice, and judgment,
and be a true friend of God!

The sobering reality is that my "CHILDREN" will probably grow up to
be just like me!

B. There must be a faithful mother in the home. Hannah prayed for a
son and made this vow in 1 Samuel 1:11, "I will give him unto the Lord
all the days of his life."

Godly mothers make those kinds of vows, and then follow through on them!

Lois and Eunice were faithful and passed that faith down the line to a
son and grandson!

2 Tim. 1:5 "I have been reminded of your sincere faith, which
[first lived in your grandmother Lois and in your mother
Eunice]{.underline} and, I am persuaded, [now lives in you
also]{.underline}."

Mothers have one of the greatest responsibilities given to a human being
because they probably have more influence in the life of their
"CHILDREN" than any other person or groups of people will have!

## C. Not only is it imperative that parents make a vow to give their
"CHILDREN" to God and to pass their faith along to them, but to make
a commitment to doing the right thing, as an example, even in the tough
times!

Hebrews 11:23 "By faith Moses, when he was born, was hid three
months of his parents, because they saw he was a proper child; and [they
were not afraid of the king\'s commandment]{.underline}."

Notice the result of having parents who are committed to doing the right
thing, no matter the consequences! Hebrews 11:24-31 "By faith
Moses, when he was come to years, [refused to be called the son of
Pharaoh\'s daughter; Choosing rather to suffer affliction with the
people of God, than to enjoy the pleasures of sin for a]{.underline}
[season; Esteeming the reproach of Christ greater riches than the
treasures in Egypt: for he had respect unto the recompence of the
reward]{.underline}. By faith he forsook Egypt, not fearing the
wrath of the king: for he endured, as seeing him who is invisible.
Through faith he kept the passover, and the sprinkling of blood,
lest he that destroyed the firstborn should touch them. By faith
they passed through the Red sea as by dry land: which the Egyptians
assaying to do were drowned. By faith the walls of Jericho fell
down, after they were compassed about seven days. By faith the
harlot Rahab perished not with them that believed not, when she had
received the spies with peace."

Every "CHILD" deserves to have a father and mother who are Godly!
The best security a "CHILD" can have in this world is to have a
father who loves the mother and expresses it, and a mother who loves the
father and expresses it, and both of them to be in love with God and
don't care who knows it!!!

## II. PROVIDE YOUR CHILDREN WITH GODLY PRINCIPLES!

A. Teach your "CHILDREN" to love and respect God with everything
they have! Deut. 6:1-9 "Now these are the commandments, the
statutes, and the judgments, which the Lord your God commanded to teach
you, that ye might do them in the land whither ye go to possess it:
That thou mightest fear the Lord thy God, to keep all his statutes
and his commandments, which I command thee, thou, [and thy son, and thy
son\'s son]{.underline}, all the days of thy life; and that thy days may
be prolonged. Hear therefore, O Israel, and observe to do it; that it
may be well with thee, and that ye may increase mightily, as the Lord
God of thy fathers hath promised thee, in the land that floweth with
milk and honey. Hear, O Israel: The Lord our God is one Lord: And
thou shalt love the Lord thy God with all thine heart, and with all thy
soul, and with all thy might. And these words, which I command thee
this day, shall be in thine heart: And thou shalt [teach them
diligently unto thy children]{.underline}, and shalt talk of them when
thou sittest in thine house, and when thou walkest by the way, and when
thou liest down, and when thou risest up. And thou shalt bind them
for a sign upon thine hand, and they shall be as frontlets between thine
eyes. And thou shalt write them upon the posts [of thy
house]{.underline}, and on thy gates."

B. Notice this, we are to be diligently teaching these principles to
our "CHILDREN" and "GRANDCHILDREN" at all times! This is to be
done while we are sitting, walking, lying down, or rising up!

We teach by word and deed! Our example outweighs everything else!

Our "CHILDREN" learn more from what they see from us than from what
we say!

We should never need to say, "do as I say, not as I do!"

## C. "CHILDREN" come with an instruction manual! Just raise you
children by the Book! God's Book!

## III. PROVIDE YOUR CHILDREN WITH GODLY PROMISES!

A. I have made promises to my "CHILDREN" that I have forgotten!
I have also made promises to my "CHILDREN" that in the end I could
not fulfill!

I can promise you this, my "CHILDREN" have never forgot a promise
that I made. "But Daddy, you promised!" has always caught my
immediate attention!

B. God is not a man that he should lie, or fail to have the ability
to follow through!

There is a great promise of God for you and your "CHILDREN" found in
Deut. 7:9

"Know therefore that the Lord thy God, he is God, [the faithful God,
which keepeth covenant and mercy with them that love him and keep his
commandments to a thousand generations]{.underline};"

1 Kings 8:56 "Blessed be the Lord, that hath given rest unto his
people Israel, [according to all that he promised]{.underline}: [there
hath not failed one word of all his good promise, which he promised by
the hand of Moses his servant]{.underline}."

Psalm 89:1 "I will sing of the mercies of the Lord for ever: with
my mouth will I make known [thy faithfulness to all
generations]{.underline}."

B. Think of this, that the faithfulness of God has come down through
many generations to us, and now we can be channels of that marvelous
faithfulness to generations to come.

To do that, I must be a recipient of God's faithfulness and steadfast
love, and in turn love him and be faithful to keep his commandments!

## C. Another great promise is that the Holy Spirit is for us and our
"CHILDREN". Acts 2:38-39 "Then Peter said unto them, Repent,
and be baptized every one of you in the name of Jesus Christ for the
remission of sins, and [ye shall receive the gift of the Holy
Ghost]{.underline}. For the promise is unto you, [and to your
children]{.underline}, and to all that are afar off, even as many as the
Lord our God shall call."

In this ungodly world, we must have the divine guidance of the Holy
Spirit, who will help us raise our "CHILDREN" to become Godly
parents themselves! It is possible to "RAISE GODLY CHILDREN IN AN
UNGODLY WORLD"!
