WHAT DOES CT BELIEVE ABOUT END TIME PROPHECY?

THE PRESENT AGE... (PLACE SLIDE #1 OF PANORAMA OF PROPHECY ON
SCREEN) Where we are today is living in what is known as the present age
or the dispensation of grace. The dispensation of grace began with the
resurrection of Jesus and will end with the rapture of the church.

THE RAPTURE... (PLACE SLIDE #2 OF PANORAMA OF PROPHECY ON THE
SCREEN) Some folks think the rapture has already occurred. If you look
at what the scripture has to say will happen between the rapture and the
final disposition of all things, you quickly realize that there has been
nothing in history that corresponds to many of these happenings.
Revelation 8 -- An Astoroid called Wormwood will hit the Earth -- Four
trumpets are blown in Revelation 8 - Atmosphere overheats - Burns up the
grass and trees -- A great mountain falls into the sea -- 1/3 of sea
life dies and 1/3 of the ships of the earth will be destroyed - Sky
grows dark -- More than one half of the Earth's population dies within a
seven-year period -- This HAS NOT HAPPENED IN HISTORY -- The rapture is
yet to come!

Some folks believe we will go through all or part of the tribulation.
The Scripture say's repeatedly that "We will be saved from wrath to
come." "We have not been appointed to wrath." Etc... Do you think for
a moment I would intentionally allow my wife, children, or grandchildren
to go through something terrible that I knew was coming, and had the
power to prevent? "If we we being evil know how to give good
gifts..."

Some folks believe there will be no rapture, that we are going to make
this earth so Heavenly that Jesus will just come and live with us. How
is that working out for you?

I believe in a pre-tribulation Rapture, and for good reason...
Revelation 4:1 "After these things I looked, and behold, a door
standing open in heaven. And the first voice which I heard was like
a trumpet speaking with me, saying, "Come up here, and I will show you
things which must take place after this." The church is never seen
again on the earth apart from the physical presence of Jesus.

The rapture is the first part of the second coming of Christ. The
rapture will only involve those who have died with their faith in
Christ, and those who are alive and have placed their faith in Christ.

I believe this is where we are living right now!

THE JUDGMENT SEAT OF CHRIST... (PLACE SLIDE #3 OF PANORAMA OF
PROPHECY ON SCREEN) Those who are raptured will be in Heaven with Jesus
for seven years. During this time, everyone present will experience the
Bema, or Judgment Seat of Christ. This is the judgment of the righteous.
2 Corinthians 5:10 (NKJV) "For we must all appear before the judgment
seat of Christ, that each one may receive the things done in the body,
according to what he has done, whether good or bad." Following the
Judgment Seat of Christ, we will experience The Marriage Supper of the
Lamb. This is a time of celebration for the redeemed, and saints of the
ages. (Jewish weddings featured a 7-day feast)

THE GREAT TRIBULATION... (PLACE SLIDE #4 OF PANORAMA OF PROPHECY ON
SCREEN) Once the rapture has occurred the world will experience chaos at
levels that have never before been seen. This will be a seven-year
period that coincides with the Judgment Seat of Christ and the Marriage
Supper of the Lamb. This period of time is also known as "The Time Of
The End" and "The Time Of Jacob's Trouble" (Matthew 24:21). The
Antichrist will come into power and things will not be too chaotic for
the first three and a half years. At the three-and-a-half- year point,
the Antichrist will make an about face on the Jewish people and all hell
will break loose on the earth. Some things that will occur: Humans will
be covered with sores -- The sea will be turned to blood -- Water
sources will be turned to blood - The sun will scorch men with heat -
Complete darkness will cover the earth -- Depression will be so great
that people will gnaw their tongues to cause pain -- The Euphrates river
will be dried up -- The earth will be shaken by earthquakes like never
before in history.
