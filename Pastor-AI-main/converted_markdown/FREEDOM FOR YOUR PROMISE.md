## I. LET GO AND LET GOD...\
\
Here we find Abram and Sara having a little trouble with God\'s promise.
They\
are trying to figure out how to make it happen on their own.

This is where we get into trouble; we try to make something happen
rather than waiting on God! If our desires are to be fulfilled by God,
our pursuit of those desires\
must be kept in line with God\'s plan.\
\
Like Sara, we begin to say, I know what God said, but maybe this is what
he\
meant.\
\
Sara believed God would fulfill His promise but she was not convinced He
would do it through her.

A lot of people believe God can heal. They are just not sure he will
heal\
them. They believe He is able but they are not convinced He is willing.
He is at least as willing as He is able to fulfill your promise!\
\
A promise is a promise!

- If God gave you a promise, Satan is going to make sure that you are
 offered\
 a substitute.

- Something almost like it, but not lasting, not fulfilling.

- If God gave you a promise then it will come to past.

Don't trade an Isaac for an Ishmael...\
\
Don\'t trade a promise for a substitute...\
\
Abraham and Sara\'s plan brought forth Ishmael.  God\'s plan brought
forth Isaac! Remember, your plan will always be inferior to God's plan!\
\
Their plan was so close to the promise that for 13 years Abram thought\
Ishmael was the promise.\
\
Sometimes it\'s easier to keep the substitute!\
\
The promise will not come easily!\
\
Galatians 4:22-23 Abraham had two sons, one from his slave-wife and\
one from his freeborn wife. The son of the slave-wife [was born in a
human\
attempt to bring about the fulfillment of God\'s promise]{.underline}.
But the son of the\

.\
God overrode the laws of nature to bring Abram\'s promise to life. God
is no\
respecter of persons. He can override natural laws to bring your promise
to\
pass! Don't be guilty of settling for an Ishmael; Ishmael is always a
second rate substitute!\
\
A lot of people in this day have settled for an Ishmael where salvation
is\
concerned. They have settled for:

- Shaking the preacher's hand!

- Church membership!

- Religion!

- The live the way you want to path!

- The path of least resistance!

Nothing will take the place of repentance and forgiveness! Jesus told
Nicodemus "You MUST be BORN AGAIN!"\
\
Isaac was a type of Jesus. Jesus was the son of promise and only through
Him\
is our covenant established.\
\
Acts 2:38 tells us:

- There are no short cuts...

- There is no back door to God...

- There is no other way...

The only way to the promise is through the blood of\
Jesus!\
\
Satan would like for you to give birth to an Ishmael where your
salvation is\
concerned, to trust something besides the promise.  God has given us a\
promise that creates new life in him!\
\
John 3:16 tells us that God has done everything to forgive us, now it is
up\
to us to receive our salvation and then walk in it!\
\
God wants each of us to have freedom in every area of our life:

- In our praise...

- In our family...

- In our finances...

- In our mind...

- In our everyday walk with him... 

If we can wait on him and not settle on a substitute, for the promise he
has given us, then we can experience the freedom the Lord has for us.
Hebrews 10 :36 For we have need of patience, that, [after we have done
the will of God, we might receive the promise]{.underline}.\
\
I don\'t know what the promise is in your life; it may have to do with;

- your kids or spouse getting saved

- God bringing your family back together

- God setting you free from your past

- freedom from addiction

- generational curses being broken

- Whatever your situation may be, there is freedom here for you; it's up
