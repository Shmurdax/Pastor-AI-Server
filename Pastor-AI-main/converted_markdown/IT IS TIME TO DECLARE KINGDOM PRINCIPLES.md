INTRO: Job 22:28 “You will also decide and 
decree a thing, and it will be established for 
you; And the light [of God’s favor] will shine 

upon your ways.” The word “Established” here 
means, “To arise and come onto the stage of 
action.” 
 
The words of Kingdom people change things in 
the atmosphere. Our words move things from 
the heavenly realm to the earthly realm. We 
need to stop seeing our words as conversation 
and start viewing them as legislation! What are 
we making law, by the words we speak? 
 
If the words we speak line up with the Word and 
will of God, they become legislation for the 
good of the Kingdom and devastation to the 
kingdom of darkness. 
 
So, I am saying that It Is Time for all of us to 
Declare Kingdom Principles over our lives and 
ministries. What does it mean to declare 
Kingdom? 

AS A CITIZEN OF THE KINGDOM, I 
DECLARE THE FAVOR OF GOD IS ON

Psalm 5:12 (NKJV) “For You, O Lord, will 
bless the righteous; With favor You will 
surround him as with a shield.” 
 
What does it mean to have favor? According to 
dictionary.com, “Something done or granted 
out of goodwill, rather than from justice or for 
remuneration; a kind act: Excessive kindness or 
unfair partiality; preferential treatment.” 
• Favor is granted rather than earned… (“For 
by grace are you saved through faith…” 
Kingdom people need to STOP thinking we 
only get what we earn – Mercy = Not 
getting what I do deserve – Grace = Getting 
what I do not deserve – When we receive 
favor, God is not paying us back, however I 
do believe what enacts the favor of God is 
the heart and motives of the individual) 
• Favor has nothing to do with the worthiness 
of the recipient and everything to do with 

the goodwill of the grantor… (Abraham was 
a liar but God decided to make him the 
father of nations – Jacob was a supplanter 
but wrestled with Jesus and received the 
blessing - Moses was a stuttering hothead 
but became the greatest pastor ever – Jonah, 
the world’s worst preacher conducted the 
world’s greatest revival - David was a cheat 
but became a man after Gods own heart – 
John Baptist was indecisive but was the 
forerunner of Christ - Peter was a cursing, 
short tempered, disloyal traitor but became a 
prolific Apostle in the New Testament 
Church - Paul was a murdering maniac but 
wrote much of the New Testament) 
• Favor is an overt act of excessive 
kindness… (Mephibosheth, the son of 
Jonathan and the grandson of Saul was 
shown the kindness of God for Jonathan’s 
sake – We receive excessive kindness for 
Jesus sake) 
• Unfair partiality… (We are Children of the 
King – Heirs of God - Joint Heirs with Jesus 

– A holy nation – A royal priesthood – A 
chosen generation – I believe this will be the 
generation that sees the Kingdom come – In 
Houston as it is in Heaven) 
• Preferential treatment… (Favor ain’t fair – 
Favor is not achieved, it is received) 
 
Not only are we favored by the Lord, as citizens 
of the Kingdom, we are “Surrounded as with a 
shield”, with that very favor! In other words, if 
we declare Kingdom favor over our lives, 
businesses, and ministries, we will experience 
“unfair partiality and preferential treatment” in 
literally everything we do!!! I want to declare a 
FOG (Favor of God) alert over CT and all of its 
members and adherents beginning now and 
continuing throughout this year! 
 
Max Lucado said, “When grace happens, we 
receive not a nice compliment from God, but a 
new heart. Give your heart to God and He will 
return the favor.” 

As citizens of the Kingdom we can declare 
unfair partiality and preferential treatment: 
• In our marriages… 
• In raising our children… 
• In the furtherance of our careers… 
• In all our business dealings and 
transactions… 
• With our health… 
• With our wealth… 
 
We need to be declaring Kingdom favor over 
these things each day of our lives. If we declare 
it, they will arise and come onto the stage of 
action! 
 
Joyce Meyer said, “God will give favor to 
anyone who will believe in Him. Every day we 
should confess that we have favor wherever we 
go. When we do, God will begin to open doors 
that we will not believe.” 
 
Deuteronomy 28:8 (NKJV) “The Lord will 
command the blessing on you in your 

storehouses and in all to which you set your 
hand, and He will bless you in the land which 
the Lord your God is giving you.”

messenger… “With the blessing” = (Ber-aw-
kaw) The present… “In your storehouses” = 
(Aw-sawm) Your barns = (Aw-sawm) Storage 
for overflow… “He will bless” = (Baw-rak) To 
congratulate abundantly, praise, or to salute… 
“In the land” = (Eh-rets) The world, the 
wilderness, the field, the country or the world! 
 
The Don Nordin paraphrase: “The Lord will 
send a messenger with a present to fill up your 
barns and storage overflow. He will reward 
everything you set your hand to do with 
success. He will congratulate, praise and 
salute you in the wilderness, the field, the
