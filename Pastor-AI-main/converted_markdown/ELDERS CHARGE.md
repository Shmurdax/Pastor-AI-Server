# ELDERS CHARGE

It is my happy privilege to give the elders charge to this 2012
ordination class of the South Texas District of the Assemblies of God.
"I charge you in the presence of God, and of Christ Jesus, who will
judge the living and the dead to PREACH THE WORD!

At times the task at hand will not be easy. I challenge you to keep your
sense of urgency:

- Whether the opportunity seems to be favorable, or unfavorable, PREACH
 THE WORD...

- Whether the task is convenient or inconvenient, PREACH THE WORD...

- Whether the message is welcome or unwelcome, PREACH THE WORD...

I remind you, it is not your job to do the spiritual work, it is your
job to convince the people, and if you can do that, God will do for them
what only He can do!

In you're preaching:

- Make sure you are Biblically sound...

- Be an encouragement to road weary pilgrims...

- Teach line upon line and truth upon truth...

- Warn the people of the impending judgment soon to come upon this
 earth...

- Rebuke and correct when necessary, but do so in meekness and
 humility...

- Urge the hearers to follow you as you follow Christ...

- Be unflagging and inexhaustible in patience...

As ministers we have been warned, "The time is coming when people
will not tolerate sound and wholesome instruction, but having ears
itching for something pleasant and gratifying, they will gather to
themselves one teacher after another attempting to justify the errors
and myths they hold." When this occurs, do not change your message to
please the crowd, remain calm, cool and steady. Be sure to maintain a
good attitude while accepting and suffering courageously every hardship
dealt to you in the ministry.

Tonight, I admonish you with the words of Paul to those he was in the
process of mentoring:

- Be studious... "Study to show thyself approved unto God, tested by
 trial, a workman who has no cause to be ashamed, correctly analyzing
 and accurately dividing the word of truth. But avoid all empty (vain,
 useless, idle) talk for it will lead people into more and more
 ungodliness."

- Present the Word rather than amend the Word... "Every scripture is
 God-breathed and profitable for instruction, for reproof, conviction
 of sin, and for correction of error. So that the man of God may be
 complete and proficient, well-fitted and thoroughly equipped for every
 good work."

- Endure difficulty gracefully... "You therefore must endure
 hardship as a good soldier of Jesus Christ. No one engaged in warfare
 entangles himself with the affairs of this life; that he may please
 him who has enlisted him to be a soldier."

- Use the Word in conjunction with the power of the Holy Spirit...
 "Our sufficiency is of God; who also has made us able ministers of
 the New Testament; not of the letter, but of the spirit; for the
 letter/law kills, but the spirit gives life."

- Do not lose heart... "Therefore, since we have this ministry, as we
 have received mercy, we do not lose heart. But have renounced the
 hidden things of shame, not walking in craftiness, not handling the
 word of God deceitfully, but by manifestation of the truth commending
 ourselves to every one's conscience in the sight of God."

- Present yourself as a living sacrifice... "I beseech you therefore,
 by the mercies of God, that you present your bodies a living
 sacrifice, holy, acceptable unto God which is your reasonable (or
 spiritual) service. And be not conformed to this world: But be ye
 transformed by the renewing of your mind, that ye may prove what is
 the good and acceptable, and perfect will of God. For I say, through
 the grace given unto me, to everyone that is among you, not to think
 of himself more highly than he ought to think; but to think soberly,
 according as God hath dealt to everyone the measure of faith."

- Operate within your giftedness... "Having then gifts differing
 according to the grace that is given to us, let us use them: if
 prophecy, let us prophesy in proportion to our faith; or ministry, let
 us be patient; or one that teacheth, on teaching; or one that
 exhorteth, on exhortation; one that giveth, do it with simplicity; one
 who rules, do it with diligence; one that sheweth mercy, with
 cheerfulness."

- Do not soil your name or the ministry... "Giving no offence in
 anything, that the ministry be not blamed: but in all things approving
 ourselves as the ministers of God."

- Be content where God chooses to place you... "For Godliness with
 contentment is great gain. For we brought nothing into this world, and
 it is certain we can carry nothing out. And having food and clothing,
 let us be content."

- Never forget, your primary job is to feed the flock of God... "Feed
 the flock of God; care for His Flock willingly, not grudgingly; not
 for what you will get out of it, but because you are eager to serve
 the Lord. Don't be tyrants, but lead them by your good example."

- Your retirement here may not be as much as some other professions but
 your reward will be over the top, and off the chain... "And when
 the chief Shepherd shall appear, then you shall receive a crown that
 will not fade away." I Peter 5:2-4 "For he has promised, "I come
 quickly: and my reward is with me to give to everyone according as
 their work shall be." Revelation 22:12 "Be thou faithful unto
 death," said the risen Christ, "and I will give thee a crown of
 life."

In closing I say once again, PREACH THE WORD, it is an honorable
profession that will change the eternal destinies of men, women, and
children. For this Word has declared; "Through the foolishness of
preaching souls will be converted."
