# TRUST IN THE LORD

TEXT: Proverbs 3:5-6 "Trust in the Lord with all thine heart; and
lean not unto thine own understanding. In all thy ways acknowledge
him, and he shall direct thy paths."

## I. TRUST IN THE LORD!

TRUST - Heb. BATACH - (BAW-TAKH) - Having confidence in something, as in
a refuge. (Like a storm cellar.)

Trust in the Lord, with all thine heart. HEART - Heb. - LEB - (LABE) -
The feelings, the will, the intellect. (The soul)

## II. LEAN NOT UNTO THINE OWN UNDERSTANDING!

LEAN - Heb. - SHAAN - (SHAW-AN) - To rely - Totally disregard or to have
no confidence in the flesh!

UNDERSTANDING - Heb. - BIYNAH - (BEE- NAW) - Wisdom or knowledge. The
things of God rarely make sense. He is an unreasonable God!

## III. IN ALL YOUR WAYS ACKNOWLEDGE HIM!

WAYS - Heb. - DEREK - (DEH-REK) - Course of life, or mode of action.

ACKNOWLEDGE - Heb. - YADA - (YAW-DAH) - To know. To ascertain by seeing
through observation, instruction, or designation.

## IV. AND HE SHALL DIRECT THY PATHS!

DIRECT - Heb. - YASHAR - (YAW-SHAR) - To be straight or even. In the
fortieth chapter of the book of Isaiah Judah's captivity is being
prophesied, and amidst the prophesy of problems, comes words of hope and
comfort. Isaiah 40:3-5 "The voice of him that crieth in the
wilderness, Prepare ye the way of the Lord, make straight in the desert
a highway for our God. [Every valley shall be exalted, and every
mountain and hill shall be made low: and the crooked shall be made
straight, and the rough places plain]{.underline}: And the glory of
the Lord shall be revealed, and all flesh shall see it together: for the
mouth of the Lord hath spoken it."

To make right pleasant or prosperous. Jeremiah 29:11 "For I know
the plans I have for you," declares the Lord, "[plans to
prosper]{.underline} you and not to harm you, [plans to give you hope
and a future]{.underline}."

PATHS - Heb. - ORACH - (O-RAKH) - A well traveled road, as if traveled
by a caravan. You may feel as if you are all alone, but remember, Jesus
has been there ahead of you! Hebrews 4:14-16 "Therefore, since we
have a great high priest who has gone through the heavens, Jesus the Son
of God, let us hold firmly to the faith we profess. For we do not
have a high priest who is unable to sympathize with our weaknesses, but
we have one [who has been tempted in every way, just as we are\--yet was
without sin]{.underline}. Let us then approach the throne of grace
with confidence, so that we may receive mercy and find grace to help us
in our time of need."

Others have traveled this road! 1 Peter 5:8-11 "Be
self-controlled and alert. Your enemy the devil prowls around like a
roaring lion looking for someone to devour. Resist him, standing firm
in the faith, because [you know that your brothers throughout the world
are undergoing the same kind of sufferings]{.underline}.And the God
of all grace, who called you to his eternal glory in Christ, after you
have suffered a little while, will himself restore you and make you
strong, firm and steadfast. To him be the power for ever and ever.
Amen."
