"HAPPY PERSON," or a "SAD PERSON?" A pessimist or an optimist?

## III. THESE BLESSINGS ARE VIEWED AS FLOWING FROM THE FAVOR OF GOD!

 A. Vs. 15 "Happy is the people that is in such a case; happy is
 the people whose God is Jehovah." It was common among the
 ancient Hebrews to regard [temporal]{.mark} prosperity as an
 evidence of [Divine]{.mark} favor.

Prosperity and peace, such as are referred to in the previous verses,
are, and must be, the result of following Jehovah with a whole heart!

Peace, order, abundance, a Nation's security and prosperity is directly
tied to the worship of Jehovah God.

That which is most certain to make a nation happy and blessed, is to
acknowledge God and to keep His laws.

> Proverbs 14:34 Righteousness exalts a nation, But sin is a reproach
> to any people.

B. The Christian's view of the evidence of Divine blessing is truer,
 deeper, and nobler than that of the ancient [Hebrew]{.mark}.

God has granted unto us a fuller and clearer revelation of Divine truth.
We look for the evidences of His favor in our souls rather than in our
circumstances.

We do not trust in well-stored granaries, but in the abounding "fruit
of the Spirit," which is in all goodness and righteousness and truth.

We depend on inward joy rather than in outward "HAPPINESS."

## C. In conclusion, "HAPPY PEOPLE" are those folks who know, and have
