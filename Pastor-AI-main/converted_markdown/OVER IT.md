# OVER IT

INTRO: Property ownership is a bedrock right in America. If we own
property, we have the right to say what will and will not be done on
that property. Eminent domain, permitting, zoning, and governmental
interference, have all but eroded this right. The reason this has
occurred is because we as Americans have not stood our ground and
demanded our rights. Most of this has been allowed at the ballot box.

No matter how much property one owns, if it is not posted against
trespassers, it is fair game for anyone to enter and exit at their will.
Once a property owner has posted the property, the landowner has the
right to take legal action against all trespassers.

We can say we are "Over It" as many times as we want to, but until we
have taken action to post the property, we have very little legal
authority to resist trespassers. (Go to Lowes, get a ladder, and get
over it)

Most of us think, when we came to Christ, it was a be all to end all
decision. The truth is, the choice to follow Jesus is not the end, it is
a new beginning.

However, it doesn't take long to see that many Christians who are
sincere and have an intense desire to serve the Lord often find
themselves defeated and cannot understand why.

This morning, I am going to point out three steps that each of us must
take to get "Over It!"

## I. THE POSTING OF THE PROPERTY

Please understand that I am very much aware that at conversion we are
sealed with the Holy Spirit of promise.

Even though we have been sealed with the Holy Spirit of promise, there
is a progressive work of sanctification that must be worked out. (Ask
everyone to raise their hand that has not sinned since conversion)

There are two questions that I believe must be asked here: What areas of
our lives have been left vulnerable? And how have they been left
vulnerable? This happens in three ways:

- Intrusion... Many people discover that doors that have been left open
 to the enemy through the activities of others! (Those who have been
 abused, parents who engrain certain practices into their children,
 etc.)

- Legal ground... Often doors are left open through years of knowingly
 practicing certain sinful habits! (Alcohol, drugs, tobacco,
 horoscopes, etc.)

- Learned behaviors... When a person has been raised in an environment
 where certain doors have been opened, those individuals are often more
 vulnerable to certain habits and vices. (Even the medical community is
 aware of this -- When you go to a new doctor, they ask you to fill out
 a family history form)

When Holy Spirit is active in the life of the believer, He convicts us
of sin and sends us back to the cross to be sanctified day by day.

As a result, we must post and reposted our lives, day by day, if we in
fact want to be "Over It!"

What are we to post our lives with? The blood of Jesus! 1 Peter 1:18-19
(NKJV) "You were not redeemed with corruptible things, like silver or
gold, from your aimless conduct received by tradition from your fathers,
^19 ^but with [the precious blood of Christ]{.underline}, as of a lamb
without blemish and without spot." (Distribute prayer I pray over my
family, and all people and ministries I oversee)

If we have posted our lives with the blood of Jesus, then everything
about us is off limits to the trespasser. (Have a red cloth to drape
over my shoulders)

If we live behind the bloodline, the enemy knows that we are off limits!
It is only at this point that we have a legal right to prosecute the
trespasser because the property has been posted. The posting of the
property with the Blood of Jesus says to the enemy "don't go there
because the owner means business!" (Tim Bowden)

## II. THE PROSECUTION OF THE PERPETRATOR

Before the perpetrator can be prosecuted, the owner of the property in
question must recognize the need for legal assistance and swear out a
warrant for his arrest.

What is the law used against trespassers? The Word of God! As a property
owner, you have the right to prosecute the trespasser according to every
Scripture contained in the Word. Job 22:28 (NKJV) "You will also
declare a thing, and it will be established for you; light will
shine on your ways."

Based on this Scripture, once we have caught the trespasser and have
decided to prosecute him, the court will not pass sentence on him until
we have declared our wishes concerning the penalty! (People who stole
copper out of a new build - The power of life and death is in the
tongue - Know the Book --Ignorance of the law -- my people perish for
lack of knowledge - Throw the book -- Believe it -- Speak it -- Stand on
it!)

The trespasser comes at us one way but when God arises to confront him,
he flees before us seven ways! Deuteronomy 28:7 (NKJV) "The
[Lord]{.smallcaps} will cause your enemies who rise against you to be
defeated before your face; [they shall come out against you one way and
flee before you seven ways]{.underline}."

Here is what happens:

- Our enemy is His enemy!

- Our battle becomes His battle!

- His victory is our victory!

Trust me saints, when we start doing things through the Blood and by the
Word, we are going to be "Over It!"

## III. THE PAYMENT BY THE PERPETRATOR

Three things are essential for us to understand:

- What we have the right to do!

- What the perpetrator will be required to do!

- What God will do on our behalf!

What we have the right to do

It is not God's will for us to discover that the enemy has trespassed
against us, and then strike a compromise that would allow us to settle
for him vacating the premises!

Our victory over the enemy is to be so complete that every time he stirs
up a conflict or declares war, when the dust settles, he will discover
that he has lost ground instead of gaining ground! Daniel 11:32 (NKJV)
"... the people who know their God shall be strong, and carry out
great exploits." The word "exploits" means "an act remarkable
for brilliance; daring or bold deed!" For a child of God to do exploits
means to finish the job in such a way that the enemy would not know what
hit him - and others would stand back and say Wow!!

(Blood of Jesus -- The Word of God - And the power of the Holy Spirit --
A triple whammy to help us to get "Over it!") (Insect repellant)

What the perpetrator will be required to do

Those who want to be "Over It" will start making the trespasser pay
the ultimate price!

How much should we expect the perpetrator to return?

- The book of Job say's double

- In 2 Samuel said four times the damage would be right

- Proverbs says seven-fold seems justified

For example: If he has stolen your children, don't just believe for them
to come back, believe they will come back with 6 of their friends.

What God will do on our behalf?

Once we have posted our property, demanded the prosecution of the
perpetrator, and notified the court of our desires about the payment
assessed against the perpetrator, THEN we can expect God to get involved
in a very substantial way.

I want to call your attention to an absolutely awesome Scripture, Romans
16:20 (NKJV) "And the God of peace will crush Satan under your feet
shortly. The grace of our Lord Jesus Christ be with you. Amen." The
word "bruise" as it is used here comes from the Greek word
"suntribo" (soon-tree-bo), which is a verb meaning "to crush
completely, to shatter, to break in pieces, to pulverize, to
disintegrate, to torpedo, to annihilate or to break into shivers."

The word "shortly" in the original is an expression of two Greek words
and is defined as "in haste, as soon as possible, without delay,
suddenly, swiftly, with all speed."

This Scripture leaves us with the image of all the works of the enemy
being thoroughly taken apart. Ladies and Gentlemen, that is exactly what
God has in mind. Isaiah 54:17 (NKJV) "No weapon formed against you
shall prosper, And every tongue which rises against you in judgment You
shall condemn. This is the heritage of the servants of the Lord, And
their righteousness is from Me," Says the Lord." What a promise from
God to us!

When he ends the verse with the words "Says the Lord" He is saying
"Signed God!" When God gets involved in our legal fight against
the trespasser, He will [see to it]{.underline} that we get "Over It!"
