on the earth is the Word of God! Hebrews 4:12 (NKJV) "For the word of
God is living and powerful, and sharper than any two-edged sword,
piercing even to the division of soul and spirit, and of joints and
marrow, and is a discerner of the thoughts and intents of the heart."

- Is a life-giving force... Ezekiel 37:7

- Is a saving power... Romans 1:16

No matter what else changes, the Word of God will remain the most
powerful force on the earth.

PREDICTION \# 2 - PRAYER WILL CONTINUE TO BE THE MOST EFFECTIVE THING
A CHRISTIAN CAN DO...

topics. The Christian is never as powerful as they are when they are
praying! Praying Christians accomplish far more over a lifetime than do
their non-praying counterparts!

A.J. Gordon said, "You can do more than pray after you have prayed;
but you can never do more than pray until you have prayed."  

Prayer is: (PLACE ON THE SCREEN ONE AT A TIME)

- Communication with God...

- A deterrent to sinful behavior...

- Filled with endless opportunity...

- A sure path to personal humility...

- A voltage regulator on the power gift...

- The only path to the throne room of God...

AT A TIME)

- Faith... Mark 11:24

- Contrition... 2 Chronicles 7:14

- Obedience... 1 John 3:22

- Righteousness... James 5:16

- Whole-heartedness... Jeremiah 29:13

PREDICTION \# 6 - THE GRACE OF GOD WILL CONTINUE TO BE EXTENDED TO HIS

Mercy is not getting what I do deserve; punishment for sin committed!
Grace is getting what I do not deserve; the blessings of God!

The word grace is mentioned one hundred seventy times in the King James
Version of the Bible. This is a very, very important subject!

The greatest gift available to mankind is the grace of God! Dwight Moody
said, "Grace isn\'t a little prayer you chant before receiving a meal.
It\'s a way to live." The law tells me how crooked I am. Grace comes
along and straightens me out."

Until Christ finished His work on the cross grace was only something
which was a figment of man's wildest imagination! Once His work was
finished on the cross, grace became a reality to all humankind!

Think of grace in this way: (PLACE ON THE SCREEN ONE AT A TIME)

- Grace releases me from my fiercest bondages...

- Grace opens up doors for me which was permanently shut...

- Grace provides a way of escaping judgment...

- Grace intervenes when sin is present...

- Grace delivers when the enemy persists...

- Grace proves my faith and salvation...

- Grace allows me the chance at heaven...

In our culture we are conditioned to believe "You get what you pay
for." The thing about grace is: (PLACE ON THE SCREEN ONE AT A TIME)

- We cannot earn it...

- We cannot purchase it...

- We cannot be good enough to deserve it...

- We cannot work hard enough to be worthy of it...

- We cannot be acquainted with enough people to negotiate it...

PREDICTION \# 7 -- THE PROVISION OF GOD WILL CONTINUE TO BE AVAILABLE
TO HIS PEOPLE...

The provision of God is something that is clearly seen throughout the
Word of God. It is amazing to see how God provided for His people in
times which appeared to be impossible. When all help had been ruled out,
God came through! Someone said, "God never comes too late or with too
little, He is always right on time with more than enough!"

God provides in at least three distinct ways:

- Overtly -- This is an unmistakable, undeniable miracle...

- Covertly -- This occurs by providence or outside influence...

- Mysteriously -- This is a deeper level of intervention than our minds
 can comprehend. At this level of provision He has been at work behind
 the scenes arranging hundreds of details and schedules to bring about
 Mysterious intervention at just the right moment!

God provided for: (PLACE ON THE SCREEN ONE AT A TIME)

- Abraham... (Covertly) As he traveled around "Looking for a city
 which hath foundations..."

- Elijah in a time of famine... (Overtly) The brook Cherith; ravens fed
 him!

- Widow of Zarephath... (Overtly) Made a cake for Elijah and the meal
 never wasted from the barrel!

- Four leprous men... (Mysteriously) At the gate of Samaria!

- Feeding of five thousand... (Overtly) 5,000 men plus the women and
 children!

God's provision during the wilderness wandering of the Children of
Israel is a strange combination of the overt, covert and mysterious
provision of God! God performed the miracle of provision so consistently
that they assumed this was a normal way of life. When we read the story
of Jehovah's dealings with the Children of Israel we take for granted
the miracle of His provision! Look at the daily miracle of provision:

- The camp of Israel was two thirds the size of Rhode Island and covered
 750 square miles...

- God provided approximately 4,000 tons of food per day...

- Transporting the food would require a freight train 2 miles long each
 day...

- The cost of the food would be approximately \$6,000,000.00 per day...

- An adequate water supply would equate to 11,000,000 gallons per day...

- The cost of water would be \$60,000.00 per day...

- Transporting the water would require a freight train 1,800 miles long
 each day...

God provided for them for 40 years or 14,600 days! This required:

- 58,400,000 tons of food at a cost of \$87,600,000,000.00...

- 160,600,000,000 gallons of water at a cost of \$876,000,000.00...

- Total cost of food and water would be \$88,476,000,000.00...

- Transportation for food and water would require a freight train
 26,309,200 miles long and would reach around the earth 106 times...

Charles Spurgeon once quipped, "We believe in the providence of God,
but we do not believe half enough in it."

While we do believe in the providence of God, we have a most difficult
time waiting for the promise! Hebrews 10:36 (KJV) "For ye have need
of patience, that, after ye have done the will of God, [ye might receive
the promise]{.underline}." Vance Havner once said, "God marks across
some of our days, 'Will explain later.'"

Some folks would say that they cannot receive the provision of God
because they have nothing for Him to work with! Is that right? You have
nothing? Nothing is exactly what God made the world of!

- God came from nowhere because there was nowhere to come from...

- God stood on nothing at creation because there was nothing to stand
 on...

<!-- -->

- God reached out where there was nowhere to reach and hung something
 upon nothing, spun the earth into orbit and told it to stay there and
 it did...

I predict God will provide for us in 2010!

PREDICTION \# 9 - GOD WILL CONTINUE TO LIVE IN THE PRAISES OF HIS
PEOPLE...

Psalms 22:3 (NKJV) "You are holy, [Enthroned in the praises of
Israel]{.underline}." The saints of God are spiritual Israel having
been grafted into the vine through the sacrifice of Christ. Ephesians
2:11-22 (NKJV) "Remember that you, once Gentiles in the flesh---who
are called Uncircumcision by what is called the Circumcision made in the
flesh by hands--- that at that time you were without Christ, being
aliens from the commonwealth of Israel and strangers from the covenants
of promise, having no hope and without God in the world. But now [in
Christ Jesus you who once were far off have been brought near by the
blood of Christ]{.underline}. For He Himself is our peace, who has
made both one, and has broken down the middle wall of separation,
having abolished in His flesh the enmity, that is, the law of
commandments contained in ordinances, so as to create in Himself one new
man from the two, [thus making peace]{.underline}, and that He might
reconcile them both to God in one body through the cross, thereby
putting to death the enmity. And He came and preached peace to you
who were afar off and to those who were near. For through Him we
both have access by one Spirit to the Father. Now, therefore, you
are no longer strangers and foreigners, but [fellow citizens with the
saints and members of the household of God]{.underline}, having been
built on the foundation of the apostles and prophets, Jesus Christ
Himself being the chief cornerstone, in whom the whole building,
being joined together, grows into a holy temple in the Lord, in whom
you also are being built together for a dwelling place of God in the
Spirit." According to this scripture, through the sacrifice of Jesus,
we have the same standing in the presence of God as our Jewish
counterparts. If we have the same standing, our praise also produces the
same effect as the praises of the Jewish people; they take us into the
Holy Place where God is enthroned in the praises of His people!

Praise always precedes victory! (LEAVE ON THE SCREEN UNTIL THE NEXT
SLIDE) In Second Chronicles chapter 20 Jehoshaphat called a counsel of
war and the decision was made, the battle strategy against the enemy
would be to appoint singers to go out ahead of the army singing praise
to God! (There was no fussing over who was going to be on the praise
team that day!) 2 Chronicles 20:21 (NKJV) "When he had consulted with
the people, he appointed those who should sing to the
[Lord,]{.smallcaps} and who should praise the beauty of holiness, as
they went out before the army and were saying: "[Praise the
[Lord,]{.smallcaps} For His mercy endures forever]{.underline}."

This strange battle strategy accomplished four distinct things: (PLACE
ON THE SCREEN ONE AT A TIME)

- Built the faith of the people... Fear dissipated as praise took
 precedence! Their praise helped them believe their mission would be
 successful!

- Mystified the enemy... The praise of the people caused confusion in
 the unholy alliance of the Ammonites, Moabites, and the Children of
 Mt. Seir! Ammon and Moab attacked and destroyed the inhabitants of Mt.
 Seir! Once the Children of Mt. Seir were dead, Ammon and Moab took
 turns destroying each other! Praise is God's jamming device to confuse
 the communication channels of the demonic spirits of Hell, who are
 bent on the destruction of the people of God! This interruption in the
 ability of the enemy to properly communicate with his cronies brought
 about their destruction! If you want to win your battle against the
 enemy get busy praising God!

- Caused God to get involved in the battle... The praise caused God to
 show up and it was His presence that turned the enemy against each
 other! Not only did the enemy kill each other the Bible say's "[The
 [Lord]{.smallcaps} set ambushes]{.underline} against the people of
 Ammon, Moab, and Mount Seir, who had come against Judah; and they were
 defeated." I can assure you there was an angel with a sword in hand
 behind every bush! Praise activates the indispensable element of the
 Heavenly host fighting for the people who are praising God! We are
 Gods children and when we choose to focus on praising Him rather than
 focusing on the difficulty, God shows up and He shows out! The Word of
 God makes this clear when it states, "Let God arise and His enemies
 be scattered!" My personal interpretation of that verse is "When
 God get's up, His enemies are gonna get it!!!" (My Dad used to say,
 "Don't make me get up from here!") Our praise brings God to His
 feet; Jesus to our rescue; the Holy Spirit to our side; Heaven to
 attention; and the angels camp out around us until the battle is
 complete and the spoils have been gathered!

- Created bountiful blessings for Judah... "When Jehoshaphat and his
 people came to take away their spoil, they found among them an
 abundance of valuables on the dead bodies, and precious jewelry, which
 they stripped off for themselves, more than they could carry away; and
 [they were three days gathering the spoil because there was so
 much]{.underline}." The will of God is far greater than for His
 people to win a battle; His will is for us to end the battle on a
 victorious note and then gather the spoils! How is this possible?
 Praise Him before the battle starts; praise Him while the battle
 rages; praise Him when the battle is complete; praise Him in all
 things and in every place, never quit praising God!

I consider myself a true worshipper! I have a heart of worship, but I
want us to understand what all is riding on our praise. For the child of
God, praise is the difference between success and failure; Victory and
defeat; life and death! Choose life, victory and success by choosing to
praise God!

God created humankind for the purpose of praise! Isaiah 43:21 states
that God has formed for himself a people "...That they may proclaim
My (God\'s) praise." The verse does not stop there, it goes on to say
"And they shall do it."

In giving oneself to praise, the worshiper declares his or her total
alignment with God\'s purposes. (LEAVE ON THE SCREEN UNTIL THE NEXT
SLIDE) Praise is the only thing fit for God! Praise is both a duty and
a delight. Psalms 63:3-8 (NKJV) "Because Your lovingkindness is
better than life, [My lips shall praise You]{.underline}. Thus [I
will bless You]{.underline} while I live; [I will lift up my
hands]{.underline} in Your name. My soul shall be satisfied as with
marrow and fatness,

And my mouth [shall praise You]{.underline} with joyful lips. When
I remember You on my bed, I meditate on You in the night watches.
Because You have been my help, Therefore in the shadow of Your wings I
will rejoice.

 My soul follows close behind You; Your right hand upholds me."

I want to be the kind of person who lives a life of praise to the point
there is a Holy overflow from my heart and spirit toward my Father,
because of who He is! I don't just want this to be an internal thing, I
want it to be such an expression that others will come to know me as a
worshipper!

Whenever and wherever God's people praise Him, He reigns among them and
does miraculous things on their behalf. I trust this will continue to be
an identifying mark of CT Church! I hope we will not settle down, I want
our praise and worship to intensify every time we come together!

Be warned, if one decides not to be a person of praise and a worshipper
of God that person will not take away from His Glory, they will only
shut themselves out from His presence, protection and provision!

## C.S. Lewis said, "A man can no more diminish God\'s glory by refusing
to worship Him than a lunatic can put out the sun by scribbling the
word, \'darkness\' on the walls of his cell."

I predict that if we want to find God in 2010, all we must do is to
start praising Him and He will be there!

PREDICTION \# 10 - THE COMING OF THE LORD WILL CONTINUE TO BE THE MOST
ANTICIPATED EVENT OF THE CHURCH...

John 14:1-3 (KJV) "Let not your heart be troubled: ye believe in God,
believe also in me. In my Father's house are many mansions: if it
were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for you.
And if I go and prepare a place for you, [I will come
again]{.underline}, and receive you unto myself; that where I am, there
ye may be also." Here in John chapter fourteen Jesus is conditioning
His Disciples to the fact that He will be leaving them very soon. One of
the things He says to them which were specifically designed to bring
cheer to their hearts and smiles to their faces is the promise, "[I
will come again]{.underline}." More than twenty centuries have come
and gone and when I read those words it develops hope in my heart and
causes a smile to break out on my face!

This world is in big trouble but let me explain to you that everything
is working according to Gods calendar! A lot of people want to know what
is about to happen and I want to take a few minutes to tell you exactly
what is about to transpire!

A little over 2,000 years ago, Jesus stood on a mountain over-looking
that wonderful city of Jerusalem and said to his beloved disciples, John
14:1-3 (NKJV) "Let not your heart be troubled; you believe in God,
believe also in Me. "In My Father's house are many mansions; if it
were not so, I would have told you. I go to prepare a place for you.
"And if I go and prepare a place for you, [I will come again and
receive you to Myself; that where I am, there you may be
also]{.underline}."

Take heart weary pilgrim, Jesus left a promise to "Come again!" We
cannot afford to: (PLACE ON THE SCREEN ONE AT A TIME)

- Quit...

- Give up...

- Become discouraged...

- Think of turning back...

ILLUSTRATION: One of the great heroes of American history is a man
who was born and raised in Little Rock, Arkansas; his name is General
Douglas McArthur. During World War II, his leadership ability came to
the forefront as he was placed in charge of the South Pacific Theatre
and was stationed in the Philippines.

During the later days of the war, General McArthur's men had taken a
beating so he withdrew them and moved them to Australia to recover. As
he boarded the DC 9 in the Philippines, it was obvious to General
McArthur that the Philippine population was anxious about their future.

Standing there noticing the fear and anxiousness on the faces of the
Philippine people he spoke three words that have been immortalized in
American history, "I shall return!"

Several months of war continued but one day the Philippine skies were
filled with the hum of military planes as General McArthur made good on
his promise to return to the Philippines!

We must hold to Gods unchanging hand! His promises are absolutely
accurate! He is coming again!
