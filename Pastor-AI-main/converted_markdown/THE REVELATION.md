# THE REVELATION

(Sermon for January 9, 2022)

TEXT: Romans 8:18-19 (NKJV) "^ ^For I consider that the
sufferings of this present time are not worthy to be compared with the
glory which shall be revealed in us. ^19 ^For the earnest expectation of
the creation eagerly waits for the revealing of the sons of God."

INTRO: We are all aware of the fact that every "jot, line and
tittle" of the Word of God is divinely inspired, or God breathed.
Therefore, every word and punctuation are extremely important to our
natural and spiritual lives.

Romans eight is in my opinion, a standout passage in the Word of God. In
this passage:

- We are brought to consciousness of the freedom we have from our
 past... ("There is therefore now no condemnation to those who are
 in Christ Jesus...")

- We are made aware of our struggle against sin... ("The carnal mind
 is the enemy of God -- And cannot be subject to the law of God...")

- We are alerted to our Sonship through the spirit... (This is adoption
 -- "The spirit Himself bears witness that we are the children of
 God -- If children then heirs of God and joint heirs of Jesus.")

- We are made cognizant of how God puts His foot on the scales for our
 benefit... ("If God is for us, who can be against us?" -- "We
 are made more than conquerors through Christ that has loved us."

Where I really want us to focus today is verse 19, "For the earnest
expectation of the creation eagerly waits for the revealing of the sons
and daughters of God."

I want to talk about three aspects of Revelation:

- The Revelation of who He is...

- The Revelation of who we are...

- The Revelation of His purpose for us...

THE REVELATION OF WHO HE IS...

Throughout the Bible, people have given many names to, or for God.
Sometimes they were given in response to something God had done, other
times they were given to describe who He is. Let\'s take a look at some
of the most popular names of God.

When we talk about the names of God, we are talking about names for
Jehovah, which is a variant
of [Yahweh](https://www.christianity.com/wiki/bible/was-yahweh-originally-an-edomite-or-canaanite-god.html),
which is translated into English
as [LORD](https://www.christianity.com/wiki/god/why-is-lord-capitalized-in-some-bibles.html).
There are well over one hundred names assigned to God in Scripture but
the seven most popular names are:

- Jehovah Jireh: The LORD our provider... ([Genesis
 22:14](http://www.christianity.com/bible/search/?ver=niv&q=genesis+22:14))

- Jehovah Rapha: The LORD our healer... ([Exodus
 15:26](http://www.christianity.com/bible/search/?ver=niv&q=exodus+15:26))

- Jehovah Nissi: The LORD our banner... ([Exodus
 17:15](http://www.christianity.com/bible/search/?ver=niv&q=exodus+17:15))

- Jehovah Shalom: The LORD our peace... ([Judges
 6:24](http://www.christianity.com/bible/search/?ver=niv&q=judges+6:24))

- Jehovah Raah: The LORD our shepherd... ([Psalms
 23:1](http://www.christianity.com/bible/search/?ver=niv&q=psalms+23:1))

- Jehovah Tsidkenu: The LORD our righteousness... ([Jeremiah
 23:6](http://www.christianity.com/bible/search/?ver=niv&q=jeremiah+23:6))

- Jehovah Shammah: The LORD is here... ([Ezekiel
 48:35](http://www.christianity.com/bible/search/?ver=niv&q=ezekiel+48:35))

We need a Revelation of the LORD as provider, healer, banner, peace,
shepherd, righteousness and peace! That is who He is!

It is my prayer that the LORD will be revealed for who He is, in this
Church, this year!

THE REVELATION OF WHO WE ARE...

Not only do we need a Revelation of who God is, we need a Revelation of
who we are!

Most believers have a dim view of their spiritual paternity. We struggle
with a lack of confidence, and therefore, underperform in our duties as
sons and daughters of God. For this reason, one translation of Romans
eight says, "The earth is GROANING for the REVELATION of the sons and
daughters of God!" Why is "The earth GROANING for the REVELATION
of the sons and daughters of God?" Surprisingly, the earth seems to
know more about the ability of the sons and daughters of God to make a
difference in the world!

Who are we?

- Kings...

- Priests...

- Disciples...

- Stewards...

- Redeemed...

- Ambassadors...

- Elect ones...

- The ecclesia...

- Heirs of God...

- Temples of God...

- Joint heirs of Jesus...

- Citizens of Heaven...

- Pilgrims and strangers...

Ladies and gentlemen, the earth is actually groaning for the Revelation
of the sons and daughters of God, because of what they can bring to the
table.

When we recognize our authority as believers, we have the authority and
power to bring Heaven to Earth!

It is my intention to walk this church down a path this year, of
stepping up to the plate and taking our rightful place in the earth!

When the church takes its rightful place in the earth "The Glory of
the Lord will be Revealed in us." When this begins to occur:

- People will come to Christ...

- Darkness will be dispelled...

- Light will spring forth in the earth...

- Culture will begin to shift beneath our feet...

- Righteousness will begin to come to the forefront...

- Evil will suffer a death blow...

- Heaven will come to earth...

No wonder "The earth is GROANING for the Revelation of the sons and
daughters of God!"
