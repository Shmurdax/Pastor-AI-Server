THREE R\'s OF RENEWAL

TEXT: 2 Samuel 5:17-25

INTRO: The worst thing that can happen to any organization is a lack
of introspection and a failure to [adjust]{.underline} to the times.

When renewal does not occur the following things happen:

- [Decay]{.underline}

- [Disrepair]{.underline}

- [Decline]{.underline}

- Lack of [motivation]{.underline}

- Loss of [interest]{.underline}

- [Reversal]{.underline}

If renewal does not occur pretty often, the leader will burnout and the
organization will [crash]{.underline} and [burn]{.underline}!

The following are three R's of renewal.

# I. REINVENT...

Reinvent [Programs]{.underline}...

Reinvent [People]{.underline}...

Reinvent [Ourselves]{.underline}...

## II. REAFFIRM...

Reaffirm [Programs]{.underline} - If a ministry has grown obsolete or
ineffective do one of three things:

- [Adjust it!]{.underline}

- [Repackage]{.underline} it!

- [Kill]{.underline} it!

Reaffirm [People]{.underline}. People need to be reaffirmed in the
following ways:

- [Cards]{.underline}...

- [Calls]{.underline}...

- A [pat]{.underline} on the back...

- Public [recognition]{.underline}...

Reaffirm [Yourself]{.underline}

Things we must do to reaffirm ourselves:

- Ongoing [evaluation]{.underline}...

- Ongoing [education]{.underline} ...

- Work on our [strengths]{.underline}...

- Overcome our [weaknesses]{.underline}...

## III. RELEASE...

When we release a thing, we must allow for the task to be accomplished
with the [personality]{.underline} of the leader being
[dominant]{.underline}.

We must:

- Release [Programs]{.underline}...

- Release [People]{.underline}...

- Release [Ourselves]{.underline}...
