The make-up of man: (Put up Power point - Body, Soul, and Spirit)

- Body

- Soul

- Spirit

The body is our earth suit - It is temporal not eternal. In the body we
have our senses - see, feel, taste, touch, hear, and smell.

The soul is our mind, will, and emotions. The soul is teat of the mental
realm - emotions, intellect, feelings, inhibitions, personality, and
reasoning.

The spirit is the God conscious part of man. This is the part of us that
is capable of knowing God and developing a relationship with him. It is
through our spirits that God speaks to us - It is in our spirit that we
worship God.

A true description of the way you are; would be: You are a spirit, you
have a soul, and you live in a body.

When you die the body goes back to the ground but the soul and spirit
lives on for an eternal day! If that be the case, what should we spend
the most time working on?

tonight...
