# CHRISTMAS LESSONS OF WORSHIP

TEXT: Matthew 2:1-12 "Now after Jesus was born in Bethlehem of
Judea in the days of Herod the king, behold, wise men from the East came
to Jerusalem, (2) saying, "Where is He who has been born King of the
Jews? For we have seen His star in the East and have come to worship
Him." (3) When Herod the king heard this, he was troubled, and all
Jerusalem with him. (4) And when he had gathered all the chief priests
and scribes of the people together, he inquired of them where the Christ
was to be born. (5) So they said to him, "In Bethlehem of Judea, for
thus it is written by the prophet: (6) 'But you, Bethlehem, in the land
of Judah, are not the least among the rulers of Judah; For out of you
shall come a Ruler who will shepherd My people Israel.' " (7) Then
Herod, when he had secretly called the wise men, determined from them
what time the star appeared. (8) And he sent them to Bethlehem and said,
"Go and search carefully for the young Child, and when you have found
Him, bring back word to me, that I may come and worship Him also." (9)
When they heard the king, they departed; and behold, the star which they
had seen in the East went before them, till it came and stood over where
the young Child was. (10) When they saw the star, they rejoiced with
exceedingly great joy. (11) And when they had come into the house, they
saw the young Child with Mary His mother, and fell down and worshiped
Him. And when they had opened their treasures, they presented gifts to
Him: gold, frankincense, and myrrh. (12) Then, being divinely warned in
a dream that they should not return to Herod, they departed for their
own country another way."

INTRO: (Relate the Christmas story, and explain that there are at
least five hidden lessons of "WORSHIP" that we can learn from the
historical account of this first Christmas ever!)

Let us look briefly at these five lessons:

WORSHIP WILL TAKE US ON A SPIRITUAL JOURNEY!

Matthew 2:1 "Now after Jesus was born in Bethlehem of Judea in the
days of Herod the king, behold, wise men from the East came to
Jerusalem."

We are not sure how far these wise men had to travel to get to
Bethlehem, but we must assume that it was quite a distance:

The "WORSHIP" of the wise men caused them to embark upon a
"JOURNEY!"

When one enters into true "WORSHIP" it will take us on a
"JOURNEY!"

Our "WORSHIP" of GOD should take us on a "JOURNEY" rather than
to leave us sitting still!

"WORSHIP" will cause us to go from death to life!

If we embark on a spiritual journey, it will be our "WORSHIP" that
transports us from where we are, to where GOD wants us to be!

WORSHIP WILL TAKE US OUT OF FAMILIAR SURROUNDINGS!

Matthew 2: 1-8 tells us that neither the wise men nor Herod knew exactly
where the Christ Child was!

Herod wanted to find Him to kill him, the wise men wanted to find Him to
worship Him. It was true then as it is now, people come to worship him
with different motives.

The desire to "WORSHIP" the Christ, brought the wise men from the
kingdoms of the east to Jerusalem!

The wise men were out of "FAMILIAR SURROUNDINGS" just getting to
Jerusalem! Now they were being required to go to Bethlehem!

Their "WORSHIP" removed them from their comfortable "FAMILIAR
SURROUNDINGS"!

Our "WORSHIP" will cause us to sit together in heavenly places in
Christ Jesus!

WORSHIP WILL BRING US TO OUR KNEES AT THE FEET OF JESUS!

Matthew 2:11 "And when they had come into the house, they saw the
young Child with Mary His mother, and fell down and worshipped Him. And
when they had opened their treasures, they presented gifts to Him: gold,
frankincense, and myrrh."

Please understand that these wise men, who were accustomed to being
called for by a king, for advice, fell at the feet of baby Jesus and
"WORSHIPPED"!

(Legion could identify with them!)

When we get a true vision of Him we will do the same!

(Someone asked - "How many wise men came to WORSHIP Jesus?" The
answer - "All wise people worship Jesus!")

WORSHIP WILL CAUSE US TO GIVE OUR VERY BEST!

Matthew 2:11 "And when they had come into the house, they saw the
young Child with Mary His mother and fell down and worshiped Him. And
when they had opened their treasures, they presented gifts to Him: gold,
frankincense, and myrrh."

When we really begin to "WORSHIP" Him, we will have a desire to give
up everything for him! Romans 12:1-2 "I beseech you therefore,
brethren, by the mercies of God, that you present your bodies a living
sacrifice, holy, acceptable to God, which is your reasonable service.
(2) And do not be conformed to this world, but be transformed by the
renewing of your mind, that you may prove what is that good and
acceptable and perfect will of God."

(Live Dead)

WORSHIP WILL CAUSE US TO LEAVE ANOTHER WAY!

Matthew 2:12 "Then, being divinely warned in a dream that they should
not return to Herod, they departed for their own country another
way."

When we come into His presence to "WORSHIP," we will most certainly
leave another way!

We cannot come into His presence and "WORSHIP" without leaving
another way!

- "WORSHIP" creates abandonment!

- When we truly "WORSHIP" we abandon ourselves...

- When we truly "WORSHIP" we abandon our sin...

- When we truly "WORSHIP" we abandon destructive relationships...

- When we truly "WORSHIP" we abandon bad habits and vices...

- When we truly "WORSHIP" abandon our path and follow His... "If
 any man will come after me..." "I am crucified with Christ,
 nevertheless I live..."
