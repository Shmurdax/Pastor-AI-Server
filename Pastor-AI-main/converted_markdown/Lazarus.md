Text: John 11:14-15 "Then said Jesus unto them plainly, [Lazarus
is dead. And I am glad]{.underline} for your sakes that I was not
there, to the intent ye may believe; nevertheless let us go unto him."

INTRO: We all know the story. . .

1.) Lazarus is sick

2.) Jesus is late (Just four day's!)

3.) Mary and Martha are mad. . . (After all, don't they deserve a
little

special treatment?)

4.) Lazarus is dead. . . (Things are as bad as they can get. This is
going

to turn out to be Jesus' fault!)

Up until this last turn of events, all Mary Martha and Lazarus needed
was a healing, now it was down to a funeral service or a resurrection!

But when you stop and think about it, would it be any more difficult for
Jesus to raise the dead than to heal the sick?

The most important event in the history of the world was the
RESURRECTION OF JESUS! 1 Cor. 15:17 "And [if Christ be not
raised,]{.underline} your faith is vain; ye are yet in your sins."

Mary and Martha and those around Jesus have witnessed the sick being
healed, but they are about to be taken to THE NEXT LEVEL. . . Our
churches need to be taken to THE NEXT LEVEL!!!

The reason Jesus tarried two more days after hearing the message that
Lazarus was sick, is because there was a need for those around him to be
taken to THE NEXT LEVEL!

They had known Jesus as...

1) A Friend...

2) A Savior...

3) The Master...

4) The Healer...

5) A Miracle worker...

Notice with me the words of Jesus amidst the mourners in this humble
house in Bethany. You would expect him to say something like, "I'm
sorry I did not come when you called for me, I hope this will not affect
our friendship!" Or you would expect him to say, "I'm sorry I was
out of town preaching a meeting, the least I could have done was to come
home for the funeral services, I hope you won't hold this against me at
the next Pastoral election!"

Notice what he said, "[LAZARUS IS DEAD. AND I AM GLAD]{.underline}!"
Now what kind of a thing is that for Jesus to say?

What he is saying is that "this whole ordeal is going to turn out for
the good, because you are about to see me in a light that you have never
seen me in before!"

Brother and sister don't ever forget that "[All things work together
for good,]{.underline} to those who love God and are called according to
his purposes!"

God is excellent at taking things that Satan meant for evil, and using
them to our eternal good!

1) Situation at Palestine!

2) Being too broke to pay my bills!

3) Being too sick to care for myself!

4) Fire in the Church at Fouke!

John 11:14-15 "Then said Jesus unto them plainly, Lazarus is dead.
And [I am glad for your sakes that I was not there, to the intent ye
may believe]{.underline};" The bottom line is, that if Jesus had not
delayed, Mary and Martha would not have gotten to know Him at the
LEVEL of Resurrection power for everyday living!

During the course of this message we have talked extensively about going
to THE NEXT LEVEL. If you and I are going to THE NEXT LEVEL in
God, there must of necessity, be certain things that happen in our
lives. These things will happen by the will of God! Let's look at them;

THE NEXT LEVEL!

A. As close as Mary, Martha, and Jesus were it took FAITH on
their part to believe Lazarus could live again! John 11:20-28
"Then Martha, as soon as she heard that Jesus was coming, went and met
him: but Mary sat still in the house. Then said Martha unto Jesus,
Lord, if thou hadst been here, my brother had not died. [But I know,
that even now, whatsoever thou wilt ask of God, God will give it
thee.]{.underline} Jesus saith unto her, [Thy brother shall rise

said unto her, I am the resurrection, and the life: he that believeth in

him, [Yea, Lord: I believe that thou art the Christ, the Son of God,
which should come into the world.]{.underline} And when she had so
said, she went her way, and called Mary her sister secretly, saying, The

B. The man with the withered hand. . . (Luke 6 tells us it was his
right hand). . . Jesus said. . . Luke 6:10 "And looking round
about upon them all, he said unto the man, Stretch forth thy hand. [And
he did so:]{.underline} and his hand was restored whole as the other."
It took FAITH for this man to stretch forth his hand!

He could have stretched forth his good hand. . . Jesus was wanting to
take him to a LEVEL where he had never been. . . LET'S QUIT
STRETCHING FORTH OUR GOOD HAND. . .

## C. If Mary and Martha would have reacted differently to Jesus there
brother might not have received his miracle! (They could have gotten
offended and quit the Church!) (They had a perfect opportunity for a
pity party!)

I found myself wondering as I prepared for this message, how many times
have I short circuited a miracle in my life because I reacted with
indifference? How many times have I missed the opportunity to go to
THE NEXT LEVEL because of a stubborn refusal to miss a grand
opportunity at a pity party?

If we are to go to THE NEXT LEVEL we must operate in FAITH not
in self pity!

[III. THERE COMES A TIME TO UNCORK THE CAVE IF WE ARE EVER GOING TO
THE NEXT LEVEL!]{.underline}

A. John 11:39 "Jesus said, [Take ye away the stone.]{.underline}
Martha, the sister of him that was dead, saith unto him, Lord, by this
time he stinketh: for he hath been dead four days."

THEY HAD PUT A LID ON THIS THING - We are always trying to keep a
lid on things. . .

We want to make sure that things don't get too far out of hand, because
sister strait jacket, and brother starched shirt might be embarrassed,
or worse yet offended!

B. Jesus comes to the mouth of the cave. Everybody follows Him.
There is an air of excitement. Jesus says GET READY! GET READY! GET
READY! TAKE YE AWAY THE STONE. . .

Martha, who has to have everything just right says, "HOLD EVERYTHING!
BY THIS TIME HE STINKETH!"

Now please understand, there is the possibility of a RESURRECTION
here, Martha is aware of that, and the only thing she can see is,
"We've got a stinking situation here. . ."

Now please understand, I know about stinking situations...

1) Dead dog under the office. . .

2) Counseling couples who are at each others throats, only to have
them

both to turn on you...

3) Help a friend get in business, only to get double crossed...

Don't try to tell me about stinking situations, I helped to edit the
book on it! But for every stinking Situation, there is always an
opportunity for a RESURRECTION!

If you ever hope to go to THE NEXT LEVEL, somewhere some time you
must become willing to UNCORK THE CAVE! ROLL THE STONE AWAY! TAKE THE
LID OFF!

ILL: Jesus is standing outside of your life, right in the big middle
of your stinking situation and he is urging you, UNCORK THE CAVE! ROLL
THE STONE AWAY! TAKE THE LID OFF! GET READY! GET READY! GET READY!
