# JESUS AS KING

INTRO: Over the past four weeks we have looked at:

- Jesus as the Word...

- The name of Jesus...

- The blood of Jesus...

- Jesus as the Lamb...

Today, on Easter Sunday we are going to look at Jesus as The King. What
better time to look at our resurrected Lord as The King than
resurrection Sunday!

In a world filled with political leaders, celebrities, influencers, and
self-made monarchs, we are reminded by Scripture that there is one true,
eternal ruler---Jesus Christ, the King of Kings. Even though we all know
He is the undisputed King of Kings, the real question is, "Is He your
King?"

As the resurrected King of Kings, He rules from "The right hand of
the throne of God, ever living to make intercession for us."

JESUS IS KING BY DIVINE APPOINTMENT

Philippians 2:9-11 "Therefore [God exalted Him]{.underline} to the
highest place and [gave Him the name]{.underline} that is [above every
name]{.underline}."

Look at this, Jesus is not a king elected by people or crowned by a
worldly system that can be reversed when the tide of public opinion
changes. He was exalted by God Himself, and "His kingdom shall never
end." Earthly Kingdoms are temporary:

- Sargon ruled the Akkadian Empire but lost power after 56 years. He met
 his final fate at age 75.

- Alexander the Great ruled the Macedonian Empire, but power slipped
 from his grip after 13 years. He died when he was only 32 years old.

- Cyrus ruled the Persian Empire but was only able to maintain power for
 30 years. He died at age 70.

- Caesar Augustus ruled the Roman Empire but found that power is
 temporary after 41 years. He died at 75 years old.

- Genghis Khan ruled the Mongol Empire but failed to maintain control
 after 21 years. He died at 65 years old.

What were the common denominators with these world rulers? They could
not hold power forever! The longest being 56 years -- All of them faced
the fate of death - The oldest living to age 75! Once dead, none of them
resurrected. King Jesus lived to the age, 33 ½ years but resurrected on
the third day to live forever!

Ladies and gentlemen, His Kingdom is not temporary; it is eternal and
divinely appointed. No one can depose Him or kill him!

In comparison, earthly kings wear a burger King Crown and pretend to be
in power! Jesus is the one and only true monarch wearing a crown made of
pure gold, surrounded by glory and honor, and building a universal
kingdom that shall never end!

Earthly kings rule at the pleasure of human permission. Jesus was raised
from the dead by His Heavenly Father and was "Highly exalted being
given a name above every name. that at the name of Jesus every knee
shall bow, and every tongue confess, that Jesus is Lord to the glory of
God the father."

If Jesus is King by divine authority, then we owe Him our highest
allegiance. This allegiance must supersede everything else. He will be
Lord of all or not Lord at all!

JESUS REIGNS WITH ABSOLUTE AUTHORITY

Jesus said in Matthew 28:18 "All authority in heaven and on earth has
been given to me."

King Jesus holds all authority. Kings of the earth have limited
influence. The authority assigned to Jesus is absolute and knows no
limitations.

When King Jesus speaks:

- Worlds are formed...

- Storms are calmed...

- The sick is healed...

- The dead are raised to life...

- Demons flee in shrieking terror...

- Those who are bound find freedom...

In World War Two, Winston Churchill had influence, but he couldn't end
the war by speaking a word. However, King Jesus calmed the sea by simply
saying, "Peace, be still." That's not just authority---that's the
Divine command of a true King.

If Jesus has all authority, we can trust Him to govern our lives.
Surrendering to Him is not weakness---it's wisdom. To fail to surrender
to the one and only true King and His Kingdom is the ultimate act of
insanity.

HIS KINGDOM WILL NEVER END Revelation 11:15 "The kingdom of the
world has become the kingdom of our Lord and of His Christ, and He shall
reign forever and ever."

Earthly empires rise and fall:

- The Akkadians...

- The Macedonians...

- The Persians...

- The Romans...

- The Mongolians...

They all rose to power and fell to defeat, then surrendered to death.
But Christ's Kingdom is eternal. His throne has no term limit, there is
no expiration date on the Kingdom of our Lord.

But two thousand years after Jesus walked the earth, His Kingdom is
gaining momentum lives are being changed and transformed by King Jesus
and His Kingdom.

Today there are 2.6 billion Christians in the world. 31% of the global
population claim to be Christian. (Experience in Corinth -- 97% of the
population of Corinth are Christians)

Ladies and Gentlemen, His kingdom is forever! There is no beginning to
His Kingdom, and there will be no ending! The resurrected Lord is
building His eternal Kingdom one conquest at a time!

CONCLUSION:

Jesus is not a ceremonial King or figurehead. He is King by Divine
Appointment, with Absolute Authority, and will reign for all Eternity.
The question is not, "Is Jesus King?" but rather, "Is He your King?"
