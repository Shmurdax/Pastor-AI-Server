This phrase "Exceeding abundantly" is a fun phrase in the original
language.  Paul uses a double compound word; in fact, he makes up his
own word phrase.  He is so caught up in the enormity of God that he
creates a new word phrase to make his point.  He stacks these words one
upon another in an attempt to say, God is not just big:

- He is big-uhmundo-huge-a-riffic

- He is fan-awe-stical-some

- He is magni-great-more-big-fully

- He is super-beyond-erful

- He is incredi-outrag-i-dible!"

 

In Ephesians 3:20 Paul is morphing words together to make his point!  He
is saying that God is:

Ably able...

Amazingly Amazing...

Awesomely awesome...

Extravagantly extravagant...

Fantastically fantastical...

Indescribably indescribable.

Infinitely infinite...

Incredibly incredible...

Outrageously outrageous... 

Bigly big...
