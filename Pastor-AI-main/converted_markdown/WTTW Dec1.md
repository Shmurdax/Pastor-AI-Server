1 Corinthians 11:10 ["]{dir="rtl"}For this cause ought the woman to
have power on her head because of the angels." This is a puzzling
verse, but when you look at the Greek word used for
["]{dir="rtl"}power," it comes alive to me. In the Greek the word power
is Exousia. Exousia means, "The ability or strength with which one is
endued, which they either possesses or exercises. The power of
authority (influence) and of right (privilege).

Exousia means:

Authority...

Domain...

Dominion...

Jurisdiction...

Liberty...

Power...

Right...

•

Ex = Out

Ousia = Essence

•

When Jesus called his 12 disciples, "He gave them POWER (Exousia )
over unclean spirits and to heal the sick and disease." Matthew
10:1

•

2,000 years ago, women were not even considered citizens. But Jesus
destroyed that way of thinking. He talked with women publicly. He
reached out to them compassionately.

The woman at the well...

The woman caught in the act of adultery...

The woman with the issue of blood...

He made women a key part of His ministry...

The first person He appeared to after His resurrection was a woman...
(See Day 324 post)

•

So, historically, Jesus freed women, regarding them in society as real
people, with gifts and callings on their life just like everyone else.
That[']{dir="rtl"}s what this verse says to me!

•

Notice the interesting last line of this verse, ["]{dir="rtl"}Because
of the angels." Meaning...The angels are watching the conduct of
women, which serves as an example to them of the right thing to do. The
angels, who are in perfect and total submission expect men and women to
perfectly and totally submit to Christ in the same way!

•

How are we submitting our exousia --- our essence, or our gifts and
callings? Angels are watching how we worship with our gifts and
callings. They are watching as we respond to who God made us to be.

•

Angels serve as mediators []{dir="rtl"}through whom messages, prophesy,
and prayers are sent. Improper use of our gifts or even the lack of
recognition of the gifts that are on our lives, offend the angels so
much so they will leave and not take our messages to God. But when
proper recognition and acknowledgement is given over the lives of
everyone, it gives them reason to remain and deliver God[']{dir="rtl"}s
messages to us.

•

Do we recognize the anointing, gifts, and callings on everyone? Men and
women alike?

•

It matters how we see and treat each other!

the Word of God?

Our mind is the battlefield. Our thought life is the command post.

•

In the armed services, all strategies, battle plans and orders must
first run through ["]{dir="rtl"}Intelligence." In reality, most
campaigns are won or lost right here (in our thoughts) -- long before
they actually are acted out on the battlefield. It is said in spiritual
warfare, ["]{dir="rtl"}Whoever gets the mind -- gets the person!"

•

Because out of our thought processes, decisions are made, and from our
decisions, behavior is practiced. In other words, what we think about -
we bring about!

•

Our lives are like a HOUSE:

Our mind is the door - Everything that shapes our character or
personality comes through it.

Our Will is the KEY - It decides what is allowed to enter.

and other sense as the means by which His light can shine.

The Conscience is the WATCHDOG -- It keeps away evil.

•

The five physical senses are the GATEWAY TO THE SOUL! They are the
pathway in!

•

A great deal of sin comes through the EYES....

Television

Reading material

Internet activity

Billboards

All of these involve SIGHT and influence what we think!

we begin to imagine (image). We are looking further with our minds that
we can see with our eyes.

•

We process a lot of information by what we SMELL -- The nose gate
involves the conscience. Through smelling we become conscience of
things. We detect the presence of good and evil through what we smell.
What we smell floods the mind with pleasant or unpleasant experiences.
(Perfume, flowers, marijuana, alcohol, food, smoke, etc.)

memory. What we hear recalls things of the past and stirs the memory.
Through the ear gate the mind is taken captive and thoughts begin to
take on imagination. (Music, songs, poems, etc.)

•

We process information by what we TASTE. This is the mouth gate and has
to do with reason. Taste opens the gate to reason. It permits the soul
to compare facts as the taste compares food.

•

We make decisions by what we TOUCH. This is the feel gate and has to do
with our affections. Touch stirs the affections as the hand which
touches the one it loves or even the one that hurts him/her. Many people
cannot stand to be hugged, or touched, others crave the touch of
another.

The Lord has provided a path to victory over whatever we are called upon
to go through or process.
