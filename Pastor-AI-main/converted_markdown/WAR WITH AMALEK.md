INTRO: Amalek was the grandson of Esau. Genesis 36:12 (NKJV)
"Now Timna was the concubine of Eliphaz, Esau's son, and she bore
Amalek to Eliphaz. These were the sons of Adah, Esau's wife."

Let me remind you of the family history behind our story:

- Jacob and Esau were the twin sons of Isaac and Rebekah

- While Rebekah was pregnant a prophesy was delivered to her that within
 her womb were two nations and the younger would be stronger and rule
 the older

- Esau was the first born and Jacob was the second born

- When the twins were born Jacob\'s hand was holding to Esau\'s heel

- Esau was a rugged man who loved the outdoors, hunting, fishing etc.

- Jacob was a peace loving guy who enjoyed gardening and hunting with a
 digital camera

- Isaac loved Esau while Rebekah favored Jacob

- Esau sold his birthright to Jacob for a bowl of soup

- Rebekah helped Jacob deceive his father into blessing him with the
 right hand of blessing

These events set into motion a rivalry that would grow worse and worse
as the centuries would come and go.

Amalek was the only group of people that God ever identified as His
constant enemy. From the time of Esau to the death of Haman the
Amalekites were a thorn in the flesh of God\'s chosen people. Just about
the time things appeared to be going in the right direction and peace
was prevailing Amalek would begin to cause problems again.

Following forty plus years of wilderness wandering Moses is restating
the Law to Israel when Amalek begins to raise sand once again. Moses
Instructs Joshua to gather the necessary man power to fight against
Amalek. It is here that we discover our Scripture setting for this
message.

## I. AMALEK IS THE FLESH!

A. Saul was told to completely destroy Amalek but decided to stop
short of carrying out the plan of God. (Saved King Agag alive, etc.)
Because he failed to carry out the plan of God against Amalek his
kingdom was taken from him. If you and I fail to wage war against the
flesh until it is completely destroyed, it will sooner or later get the
upper hand and destroy God\'s plans and our plans for our lives. (Woman
who raised a Boa Constrictor)

B. While I am fully aware that the descendants of Amalek cannot be
found on the earth following the death of Haman in the book of Esther, I
am here to tell you that the spirit of Amalek lived longer that the
patriarchs, Exodus, and judges; Past the prophets, priests, and kings;
the spirit of Amalek lived beyond the disciples, the cross and
Pentecost; it is alive in the world right now and is prevalent in this
room today!

## C. Amalek is the flesh, so what do we do about the flesh? Declare
perpetual war on it!
