[\~\~ THE 23rd Psalm \~\~]{.underline} 

This is an eye opener; some probably never thought nor looked at this
Psalm in this way, even though they say it over and over again.\
\
  The Lord is my Shepherd\
  [That\'s Relationship!\]{.underline}
\
  I shall  not want\
  [That\'s Supply!\]{.underline}
\
  He maketh me to lie down in green     \
\
  pastures\
\
 [  That\'s Rest!\]{.underline}
\
  He leadeth me beside the still waters\
[  That\'s Refreshment!\]{.underline}
\
  He restoreth my soul\
[  That\'s Healing!\]{.underline}
\
  He leadeth me in the paths of righteousness\
[  That\'s Guidance!\]{.underline}
\
  For His name sake\
[  That\'s Purpose!\]{.underline}
\
  Yea, though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death \
  [That\'s Testing!\]{.underline}
\
  I will fear no evil\
[  That\'s Protection!]{.underline}\
\
  For Thou art with me\
[  That\'s Faithfulness!]{.underline}\
\
  Thy rod and Thy staff they comfort me\
[  That\'s Discipline!\]{.underline}
\
  Thou preparest a table before me in the presence of mine enemies\
[  That\'s Hope!]{.underline}\
\
  Thou annointest my head with oil\
[  That\'s Consecration!\]{.underline}
\
   My cup runneth over\
   [That\'s Abundance!]{.underline}\
\
  Surely goodness and mercy shall follow me all the days of my life\
  [That\'s Blessing!]{.underline}\
\
  And I will dwell in the house of the Lord\
  [That\'s Security!]{.underline}\
\
  Forever\
[  That\'s Eternity!\]{.underline}
\
  Face it, the Lord is crazy about you. Send this to the people you are
crazy about.\
\
  I thought this was pretty special, just like [YOU !]{.underline} What
is most valuable, is not  [what]{.underline} we\
  have in our lives, but [WHO]{.underline} we have in our lives!\
