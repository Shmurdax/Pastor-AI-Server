INTRO: In the last quarter of each year I always look at the Jewish
Calendar to see what the meaning of the Jewish New Year (Which began in
September) will be. For as long as I have been doing this there has been
a correlation between what the Lord is saying to me for CT regarding the
New Year and the meaning of the Jewish New Year.

2020 is the year 5780 on the Jewish calendar. This is not only a new
year but a new decade.

The last decade was 5770 -- 5779 and is known as the AYIN decade. AYIN
means eyes or sight and so throughout this past decade, God was
revealing many things to us through our vision. 

While the previous decade was the Ayin (Seeing) decade, this new decade
from 5780 -- 5789 is the Pey (80) decade.  The numerical value of Pey is

80. Pey means mouth, word, voice, speech, breath or vocalization.
(PLACE THE SYMBOL OF THE MOUTH ON THE SCREEN)

On the gregorian calendar the number 20 is the word kaph (Cough), and
means open palm. So 2020 is two open palms:

- The offer is being made...

- It is here for the taking...

- Double provision...

- Double portion...

So, beginning now, we are going to be moved from seeing to speaking and
receiving.  

Because we are embarking on a time where we will be receiving what we
are speaking we don't want to be speaking out of order on the things we
have not seen or don't understand. It becomes extremely important for us
to see and understand before we open our mouth's and speak.

I believe God has placed a special mark on this year 5780:

- 5 is the number of grace...

 presence...

- 8 is the number of new beginnings...

What does this have to do with 2020? 5+7+8=20

We are going to speak these prophecies together. This is called
entrainment. Entrainment is a military term meaning in sync; in rhythm;
on beat; or to make the same sound.

This is what troops do when they march... Left, right, left, right,
left... When troops march across a bridge they cannot be in step because
if the sound of their rhythm matches the sound of the rhythm of the
bridge, the bridge will collapse. When we speak the same thing, we are
together in entrainment, and this kind of agreement transfers things
from the heavenly realm to the earthly realm. "If as many as two of
you shall agree as touching any one thing..."
