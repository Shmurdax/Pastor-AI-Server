# 3-DIMENSIONAL RESURRECTION

INTRO: Today we are going to talk about resurrection in a historic
sense (2,000 plus years ago), what resurrection looks like in the 21^st^
century, and what it will look like in a futuristic sense.

Most Christians have the resurrection tucked away in a nice, neat,
little historical package that they can reflect on once a year, and then
move on to more pressing and significant issues.

It is my hope that when we walk out of this place today, we will never
view resurrection in the same way! I pray that the lights will come on
for all of us and that we will not only believe in the resurrection but
walk in its enormous power every day, for the rest of our lives.

Luke 24:1-9 (NKJV) "Now on the first day of the week, very early in
the morning, they, and certain other women with them, came to the tomb
bringing the spices which they had prepared. 2 But they found the stone
rolled away from the tomb. 3 Then they went in and did not find the body
of the Lord Jesus. 4 And it happened, as they were greatly perplexed
about this, that behold, two men stood by them in shining garments.
5 Then, as they were afraid and bowed their faces to the earth, they
said to them, "Why do you seek the living among the dead? 6 He is not
here, but is risen! Remember how He spoke to you when He was still in
Galilee, 7 saying, 'The Son of Man must be delivered into the hands of
sinful men, and be crucified, and the third day rise again.'" 8 And they
remembered His words. 9 Then they returned from the tomb and told all
these things to the eleven and to all the rest."

DIMENSION \# 1

RESURRECTION IN HISTORY

Resurrection is:

- The most significant event in the history of the world... 1
 Corinthians 15:17 (CEV) "Unless Christ was raised to life, your
 faith is useless, and you are still living in your sins."

- The most misunderstood topic in the current culture...

- The most meaningful word in the Christian vocabulary...

The resurrection of Christ from the dead is the most important event in
the history of the world. This is true because the resurrection of
Christ from the dead validates:

- His virgin birth...

- His sinless life...

- His brutal death...

- His claim to be the Messiah...

If Christ had not been raised from the dead, His death would have been
nothing more than another (among many) brutal executions via
crucifixion. The good news is that He did in fact rise from the dead on
the third day!

DIMENSION #2

RESURRECTION IN REAL TIME

According to the resurrection chapter, 1 Corinthians 15:

- Our faith is predicated on the resurrection... (Without the
 resurrection -- "Our faith is in vain")

<!-- -->

- The resurrection is the bedrock of what we preach... (No resurrection
 -- "We are false witnesses")

- The forgiveness of sin is tied to the resurrection... (No resurrection
 -- "We are yet in our sin")

- Our contentment and joy stems from the resurrection... (No
 resurrection -- "We are of all men most miserable")

DIMENSION #3

RESSURRECTION IN THE FUTURE

- The physical resurrection of the dead in Christ depends on the
 resurrection of Jesus... (No resurrection -- "The dead rise
 not")

- The resurrection furnishes the impetus for the final defeat of death
 as an enemy... (Because of the resurrection -- "The last enemy to
 be destroyed - death")

- The second coming of the Lord is guaranteed because His first coming
 ended in a resurrection... (Because of the resurrection -- "I go to
 prepare a place for you..." "For the lord Himself shall descend..." "I
 show you a mystery...")

CONCLUSION:

We may be able to bury Jesus in a compartment of our lives but sooner or
later He is going to resurrect, and we will have to deal with Him.

We can:

- Banish Him from our lifestyles - He will eventually challenge how we
 are choosing to live...

- Banish Him from our relationships -- Sooner or later He will be the
 last one standing, and we must answer for who He is...

- Banish Him from our finances -- In time, we will have to admit "All
 good and perfect things come from above."

- Banish Him from our homes -- Sooner or later we will discover He is
 the only real source of peace...

- Banish Him from our eternity -- "Every knee shall bow..."
