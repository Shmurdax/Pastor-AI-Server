INTRO: Job 22:28 "You will also decide and decree a thing, and it
will be established for you; And the light \[of God's favor\] will shine

The words of Kingdom people change things in the atmosphere. Our words
move things from the heavenly realm to the earthly realm. We need to
stop seeing our words as conversation and start viewing them as
legislation! What are we making law, by the words we speak?

If the words we speak line up with the Word and will of God, they become
legislation for the good of the Kingdom and devastation to the kingdom

So, I am saying that It Is Time for all of us to Declare Kingdom
Principles over our lives and ministries. What does it mean to declare
Kingdom?

AS A CITIZEN OF THE KINGDOM, I DECLARE THE FAVOR OF GOD IS ON ME

Psalm 5:12 (NKJV) "For You, O Lord, will bless the righteous; With
favor You will surround him as with a shield."

What does it mean to have favor? According to dictionary.com,
"Something done or granted out of goodwill, rather than from justice or
for remuneration; a kind act: Excessive kindness or unfair partiality;
preferential treatment."

- Favor is granted rather than earned... ("For by grace are you saved
 through faith..." Kingdom people need to STOP thinking we only get
 what we earn -- Mercy = Not getting what I do deserve -- Grace =
 Getting what I do not deserve -- When we receive favor, God is not
 paying us back, however I do believe what enacts the favor of God is
 the heart and motives of the individual)

- Favor has nothing to do with the worthiness of the recipient and
 everything to do with the goodwill of the grantor... (Abraham was a
 liar but God decided to make him the father of nations -- Jacob was a
 supplanter but wrestled with Jesus and received the blessing - Moses
 was a stuttering hothead but became the greatest pastor ever -- Jonah,
 the world's worst preacher conducted the world's greatest revival -
 David was a cheat but became a man after Gods own heart -- John
 Baptist was indecisive but was the forerunner of Christ - Peter was a
 cursing, short tempered, disloyal traitor but became a prolific
 Apostle in the New Testament Church - Paul was a murdering maniac but
 wrote much of the New Testament)

- Favor is an overt act of excessive kindness... (Mephibosheth, the son
 of Jonathan and the grandson of Saul was shown the kindness of God for
 Jonathan's sake -- We receive excessive kindness for Jesus sake)

- Unfair partiality... (We are Children of the King -- Heirs of God -
 Joint Heirs with Jesus -- A holy nation -- A royal priesthood -- A
 chosen generation -- I believe this will be the generation that sees
 the Kingdom come -- In Houston as it is in Heaven)

- Preferential treatment... (Favor ain't fair -- Favor is not achieved,
 it is received)

Not only are we favored by the Lord, as citizens of the Kingdom, we are
"Surrounded as with a shield", with that very favor! In other
words, if we declare Kingdom favor over our lives, businesses, and
ministries, we will experience "unfair partiality and preferential
treatment" in literally everything we do!!! I want to declare a FOG
(Favor of God) alert over CT and all of its members and adherents
beginning now and continuing throughout this year!

Max Lucado said, "When grace happens, we receive not a nice compliment
from God, but a new heart. Give your heart to God and He will return the
favor."

As citizens of the Kingdom we can declare unfair partiality and
preferential treatment:

- In our marriages...

- In raising our children...

- In the furtherance of our careers...

- In all our business dealings and transactions...

- With our health...

- With our wealth...

We need to be declaring Kingdom favor over these things each day of our
lives. If we declare it, they will arise and come onto the stage of
action!

Joyce Meyer said, "God will give favor to anyone who will believe in
Him. Every day we should confess that we have favor wherever we go. When
we do, God will begin to open doors that we will not believe."

Deuteronomy 28:8 (NKJV) "The Lord will [command]{.underline}
the blessing on you in your storehouses and in all to which you set your
hand, and He will bless you in the land which the Lord your God is
giving you."

blessing" = (Ber-aw-kaw) The present... "In your storehouses" =
(Aw-sawm) Your barns = (Aw-sawm) Storage for overflow... "He will
bless" = (Baw-rak) To congratulate abundantly, praise, or to
salute... "In the land" = (Eh-rets) The world, the wilderness, the
field, the country or the world!

present to fill up your barns and storage overflow. He will reward
everything you set your hand to do with success. He will congratulate,
praise and salute you in the wilderness, the field, the country and the
world! The reason he will salute you in these places is because you are
possessing territory that He has already given you!"

ATTITUDE

The ability to transition from poverty to prosperity is at least in part
driven by our attitude toward money and things.

George Brazell in his book, "This is Stewardship" makes the
following statement: "Stewardship is the sum total of man\'s attitude
and reaction toward the Divine Creator and His creation. God is the
owner of all creation, and man is the responsible servant entrusted
with, and commissioned to, rightly handle for God His program on
earth." Our attitudes toward money and things, regulate whether we are
going to live our lives in poverty or prosperity.

If we are going to DECLARE NO MORE LACK, and make it stick, we must
change our attitude toward things! Everything I have belongs to God,
therefore He can have it when He wants it, and I don't have to worry
about it, because there is more where that came from!

value, plus 20 percent. 32 The [Lord]{.smallcaps} also owns [every tenth

Tithing is not a suggestion for a Christian; it is a commandment to
honor God with our finances. This commandment is not because He needs

giving and generosity that opens up the heavens.

We are either obedient or disobedient! Isaiah 1:19-20 (NKJV) "If you
are willing and obedient, You shall eat the good of the land; 20 But if

of the [Lord]{.smallcaps} has spoken."

TRUST

Tithing does not require trust and therefore does not necessarily bring
with it the blessing of multiplication. First-fruit requires trust and
enacts the blessing of multiplication upon our resources. If I give the
first of what I have believing for at least nine more - That requires
trust and trust is a big deal to God. Proverbs 3:5-6 (NKJV) "Trust in
the [Lord]{.smallcaps} with all your heart, And lean not on your own
understanding;^6 ^In all your ways acknowledge Him, And He shall direct
your paths." Let me paraphrase this -- "Be bold, confident and
careless when it comes to the things of the Lord by refusing to lean on
your own understanding. Acknowledge God first with the way you live your
life and He will make the traveling path of your troop or caravan,
pleasant, prosperous, and good." (ILLUSTRATION WITH 2 \$100 BILLS --
HAVE EVERYONE TO HOLD UP A \$5 BILL)

We must be willing to give up what we have in our hand to get what He
has in His!

GENEROSITY

If we are going to DECLARE NO MORE LACK, we must be ready to enact
the principle of generosity.

A lot of people want to be compliant, but if we want to truly walk in -
NO MORE LACK - We must become one with generosity, in every area of
our lives.

Generosity is the defining test of all would-be-stewards and is
essential in breaking the power of lack. Matthew 25:29 (NLT) "To
those who [use well what they are given, even more will be
given]{.underline}, and [they will have an abundance]{.underline}. But
from [those who are unfaithful, even what little they have will be taken
away]{.underline}." (BRING 3 PEOPLE UP TO DO TRIP ILLUSTRATION)

If we cannot pass the generosity test with a good attitude, we can
forget being in possession of the many other blessings of God. Proverbs
11:24 (NKJV) "There is one who scatters, yet increases more; And
there is one who withholds more than is right, But
it leads to poverty." My personal paraphrase: "The path of the
generous leads to prosperity; The path of the stingy leads to
poverty."

Generosity is the first step toward multiplication. The Bible is full of
instances of the miracle of multiplication:

- The strength of Israelite soldiers was multiplied on many occasions
 when they were hopelessly outnumbered...

- The meal and oil of the widow and her son was multiplied...

- The multiplication of a little boy's lunch...

Multiplication is the ground that grows the ability to DECLARE NO MORE
LACK!
