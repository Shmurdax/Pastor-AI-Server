The message of the cross cost of the disciples, in fact it cost them
dearly.

Andrew died on the cross.

Simon was crucified.

Batholomew was flayed alive.

James the son of Zebedee was beheaded.

James the son of Alphaeus was beaten to death.

Thomas was run through with a lance.

Mathias was stoned and then beheaded.

Matthew was slain by the sword.

Peter was crucified upside down.

Thaddeus was shot to death with arrows.

Philip was hanged.

Only John made it through alive but he was exiled to a small island in
the middle of the Mediterranean Sea.
