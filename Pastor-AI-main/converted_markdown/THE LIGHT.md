# THE LIGHT

(Tom Bodette Motel 6)

TEXT: John 1:1-14 (NKJV) "In the beginning was the Word, and
the Word was with God, and the Word was God. ^2 ^He was in the beginning
with God. ^3 ^All things were made through Him, and without Him nothing
was made that was made. ^4 ^In Him was life, and the life was the light
of men. ^5 ^And the light shines in the darkness, and the darkness did
not comprehend it. ^6 ^There was a man sent from God, whose
name was John. ^7 ^This man came for a witness, to bear witness of the
Light, that all through him might believe. ^8 ^He was not that Light,
but was sent to bear witness of that Light. ^9 ^That was the true Light
which gives light to every man coming into the world.

^10 ^He was in the world, and the world was made through Him, and the
world did not know Him. ^11 ^He came to His own, and His own did not
receive Him. ^12 ^But as many as received Him, to them He gave the right
to become children of God, to those who believe in His name: ^13 ^who
were born, not of blood, nor of the will of the flesh, nor of the will
of man, but of God. ^14 ^And the Word became flesh and dwelt among us,
and we beheld His glory, the glory as of the only begotten of the
Father, full of grace and truth."

INTRO:

When Jesus was crucified the lights went out... It was like midnight in
the middle of the day... The earth shook, rattled and rolled...

Look at this:

- Vs. 4 "In Him was life, and the life was the light of men." They
 killed the light... They should have expected darkness... Anytime you
 kill the light you should expect darkness... (Cut the lights on... Cut
 the lights off...)

- Vs. 5 "And the light shines in the darkness, and the darkness did
 not comprehend it." Only people who are totally blind fail to
 distinguish between light and darkness. The sin of this world can have
 such a blinding effect that those who are involved in it fail to
 realize the light has been turned on or off... That was precisely the
 condition of the world at the crucifixion of Jesus... It is the
 condition of the world today... The world calls good bad and bad
 good...

- Vs. 6-8 Tells us that john the Baptist was sent to bear witness that
 Jesus would come into the world to provide true light... This is not
 the motel 6 kind of light... This is eternal light... No more night...
 No more darkness... "In the city where the Lamb is the light. In the
 city where there cometh no night, I've a mansion over ther,e and when
 freed from toil and care, I am going where the Lamb is the light."

- Vs. 9 "That was the true Light which gives light to every man
 coming into the world."

When Jesus died He went to hell for three days and conquered the keys of
death, hell and the grave. The Bible is plain to tell us that Jesus'
soul did not experience the torment of Hell. God did for Jesus exactly
what He did for the Three Hebrew Children!

When He rose on the third day He turned the lights on:

- He turned the light of salvation on... No more dead animals.

- He turned the light of relationship on... No more need for a man to go
 between God and man.

- He turned the light of Hope on... Hope sprang out of hopelessness --
 Hope is the anchor of the soul.  

- He turned the light of Peace on... He was chastised for our peace --
 Do you really think he would take a beating that would have killed six
 men just like him only to have us to be devoid of peace?

- He turned the light of eternity on... So great was His resurrection
 that the saints of the ages were seen walking around on the streets of
 Jerusalem.  

He didn't just turn the lights on, He left them on and removed the
switch so no one could turn the lights out!

He went into Heaven and left the door (Light) standing open (On).
Revelation 4:1 (NKJV) "After these things I looked, and behold, a
door standing open in heaven. And the first voice which I heard was like
a trumpet speaking with me, saying, "Come up here, and I will show you
things which must take place after this."

 

- His salvation is profound... I can't quite get my head around why He
 would do such a thing for me! The songwriter said, "I don't know why
 Jesus loved me... But I'm so glad that He did..."

- His salvation is inclusive... Whosoever will - Till all know... 

- His salvation is extensive... Multi ethnic (Black, white, red,
 yellow, purple with pink polka dots) - Multi cultural - Multi
 generational -- Multi economic...

- His Salvation is eternal... Forever - Without beginning and without
 end -- The songwriter said, "Ten thousand years we will just be
 starting, ten thousand years we've just begun..." By the way -- Lost
 lasts forever too!

CONCLUSION:

The lights went out when Jesus died, but when He got up from the grave
on the third day, He decided to turn the lights on:

- In the dark tomb...

- In the dark cemetery... In the darkness of the twilight the women came
 to the garden tomb! (the Lord spoke to my dad and said, "Why are you
 seeking the living among the dead?"

- In the world... Jesus is the light -- We are the light -- You can be
 the light!

- In Heaven -- John 14 "Where I go you know, and the way you know.
 Thomas said, 'Lord, we don't know where you are going so how can e
 know the way?' Jesus said, 'I am the way the truth and the life. No
 man comes unto the father except by me'"

We can have light without Tom Bodette, but we will never have light
without Jesus.

We can have life without a lot of things that are considered by
Americans to be essential, but we can't have life without Jesus!

The culmination of the light in our lives is this... "Ain't no grave
gonna hold my body down..."
