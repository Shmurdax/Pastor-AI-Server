# WHEN A NATION FORGETS GOD

INTRO: Today we are celebrating Pentecost Sunday, which is the
birthday of the church. Pentecost Sunday is my second favorite Sunday on
the Church Calendar, after Easter Sunday. Every Pentecost Sunday in the
last forty-two years of Pastoring I have preached on the Pentecostal
experience. I am not going to do that this morning -- We will celebrate
the birthday of the church tonight at 6, with the rest of CT Nation.

This past Monday we celebrated Memorial Day -- We remember those who
have made the ultimate sacrifice for our freedom -- Amid the family
gatherings, cookouts, hamburgers and hotdogs, may we not forget the
sacrifices that have been made for our freedom.

Just prior to Memorial Day we were made painfully aware of the depravity
of man, and just how far America has drifted from the values upon which
it was founded with the killing of innocent children and adults inside a
public school in Uvalde Texas. While these things take us by surprise,
they shouldn't because...

A NATION THAT FORGETS GOD, CAN EXPECT A DOWNWARD SPIRAL

As we sit here this morning -- SADLY -- America is a fading shadow of
what it one time was -- I do not mean to be a negative Nancy but it is
time for the Spiritual and public leaders of this nation to speak up and
boldly, declare that this nation has lost its way -- BECAUSE it has
indeed, forgotten God!

We have:

- Taken prayer out of schools...

- Taken the Bible and Ten Commandments out of the public square...

- Murdered more than 61 million unborn children... (We have
 intentionally killed more than 20% of our national population -- Not
 counting the children, grandchildren, and great grandchildren they
 would have produced)

- Made fun of God and expelled Him from public policy, and even public
 conversation...

- Shook our fists in the face of God, believing our politicians know
 better than God, how to run a country... (How is that working for
 you?)

- Removed the pledge of allegiance from schools and sporting events...

- Excused athletes that refuse to stand for the national anthem... (Try
 explaining why this should be tolerated to a Gold Star family)

At one time during the history of the USA, there was a very healthy fear
of the Lord. In this culture, social/secular life has become separate
from spiritual life, and many have just completely moved away from
spirituality altogether.

There was a time when we needed God so much that we walked with Him like
children do with their fathers, when they are in a strange place. As God
began to bless, we began to trust in those blessings more than in the
One who gave them. For a while we still continued to give lip service to
Him, yet our hearts were moving farther and farther away from Him. We
have reached the point now that many are just in survival mode, and do
not have time for God or spiritual things.

Listen to what the Bible predicts for a nation that reaches the point of
Forgetting God: Psalm 9:15-17 (NKJV) "The nations have sunk down in
[the pit which they made]{.underline}; In [the net which they hid, their
own foot is caught]{.underline}.16 The Lord is known by the judgment He
executes; [The wicked is snared in the work of his own
hands]{.underline}. Selah. ^17 ^[The wicked shall be turned into hell,
And all the nations that forget God]{.underline}."

- The pit we are in is of our own making... "The nations have sunk
 down in [the pit which THEY have made]{.underline}..." (We cannot
 blame God -- We are to blame)

- Our public policy has created a net that has entrapped us... "[The
 wicked is snared in the work of HIS OWN HANDS]{.underline}. Selah."
 (Selah = Pause and think about that)

- Our wickedness turns our very existence into a living hell... "[The
 wicked shall be turned into hell, And all the nations that forget
 God]{.underline}." (The person who forgets God can expect their
 lives to be turned into a living hell -- Not because God is
 vindictive, but because they turn from God to Satan, and he is bent on
 destroying everything he touches -- The same goes for a nation -- A
 nation can't forget God and expect His blessing -- They should expect
 the destruction of the enemy, because they have opened the door to his
 diabolical schemes -- Representative Jerry Nadler, Democrat from New
 York said, "What any religious tradition describes as God's will is
 no concern of this Congress.") 

WHEN A NATION FORGETS GOD, IT CREATES A DATE WITH DISASTER

Romans 1:18-32 (NKJV) "For the wrath of God is revealed from heaven

truth in unrighteousness, ^19 ^because what may be known of God

Look at this:

- The depravity of man (Not the wrath of God), is creating a date with
 disaster in the earth...

- Our current problems have more to do with God allowing man to date the
 devil, than His wrath being poured out... (Make no mistake about it,
 His wrath is going to be poured out, but I do not believe that is what
 we are seeing right now -- The wrath of God will be unmistakable)

- We are seeing the results of man's stubborn refusal to "Retain God
 in our knowledge." (We are shocked by an 18 year-old child killing
 19 people in Texas -- It is a national tragedy and a blight on America
 -- But I am not sure it should come as much of a surprise to us -- We
 have set the stage for these things because we have "Forgotten
 God" - We are now beginning to reap the grim consequences of our
 actions)

expect when they steadfastly refuse to acknowledge God in their public

- Fornication = Unhealthy lust of either sex

- Wickedness = To plot sin

- Covetousness = Fraudulent extortion, greed, or eager for gain

- Maliciousness = Bad, evil, harmful things

- Envy = Jealousy, spite, defilement, ruin, self-corruption

- Murder = To be slain -- Slaughter

- Debate = Quarrels that are very contentious, strife, variance

- Deceit = Create a decoy -- Filled with guile

- Malignity = Bad character that is filled with maliciousness

- Backbiters = Slanderers

- Haters of God = No respect or reverence for God

- Despiteful = Insults that lead to injury

- Proud = Taking a posture of being better than everyone else

- Boasters = Braggadocios -- Bragging when there is nothing to brag
 about

- Inventors of evil things = Inventing evil, harmful, bad things

- Disobedient = Stubborn, willful, disobedience to authority --
 Lawlessness

- Without Understanding = Foolishly proceeding without having
 understanding of the facts

- Covenant Breakers = Breaking promises and contracts as a matter of
 doing business

- Lacking Natural Affection = Being heartless -- Taking inhuman actions

- Implacable = Breaking truces without a second thought

- Unmerciful = Having no mercy -- Merciless

Every single one of these things are being played out on our television
screens every day, and being passed off as a normal way of doing things!

Unless this nation and world turns back to almighty God, it has a date
with disaster!

A NATION THAT FORGETS GOD, CAN TURN BACK TO GOD

2 Chronicles 7:13-14 (NKJV) "When I shut up heaven and there is no
rain, or command the locusts to devour the land, or send pestilence
among My people, ^14 ^if My people who are called by My name will humble
themselves, and pray and seek My face, and turn from their wicked
ways, then I will hear from heaven, and will forgive their sin and heal
their land." 

When a person or a nation gets to the point of incurring the wrath of
God, and we need God the most (Which is where we are), instead of
digging our pit deeper and deeper, we need to return to our Creator and
watch how He will put us back on the right path.

I will issue the following warning: If we do not come to our senses, we
will begin to see the wrath of God on our personal lives, upon our
country, and upon our world.

God oftentimes attempts to reach us by pressing us with the consequences
of our own sin. This is an attempt by God to drive us to our knees so
that we will return to Him, and receive His protection and blessings. I
call it "Last ditch mercy." Our Lord is so loving that even His
judgment is an attempt at mercy. "The destruction of the flesh is an
attempt to save the soul."

If we choose to ignore His warnings, we will most certainly reap the sad
consequences of our own rebellion and sinful ways.

Sooner or later sin will bring about its fruit in our lives, our nation,
and the world.

God is so good that He allows us to make our own choices, but He also
tells us what the right choices are, and what the wrong choices are.
While He allows us to make our own choices, He does not let us choose
our own consequences!

He is so merciful that He warns us ahead of time of the consequences of
our actions!

The prudent person weighs the decision, and the consequences, and
chooses to live Godly in this present world! Proverbs 22:3 (NKJV) "A
prudent man foresees evil and hides himself, But the simple pass on and
are punished."

We must listen and pay attention to the Word of God and what He is
trying to say to us in our day. If we do not pay attention to the Word
of God, we will suffer the grim consequences of our actions.
