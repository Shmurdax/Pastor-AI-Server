# HONOR & KINGDOM FINANCES\
\
HONOR

Romans 12 &13

Every sin is a sin of dishonor:

- If we sin against the Lord, it is because we have dishonored Him.

- If we sin against others, it is because we have dishonored them.

- If we sin against ourselves, it is because we have sinned against our
 own character.

- If we fail in life, we can trace it to a person we dishonored.

- If we succeed in life, we can trace it to a pattern of honor.

> Honor creates instant credibility. I have attempted to live a life of
> honor:

- I have honored my parents.

- I have honored my wife.

- I have honored my leaders.

- I have honored my elders.

- I have honored my government.

> Therefore, I have never had a difficult time having my voice heard,
> and I have never struggled to find people who are willing to walk with
> me.

For the most part, the people I lead do a great job of showing honor to
Susan and I. However, they sometimes struggle with shoeing honor to
people I have delegate the work of the ministry to.\
\
People call the office and talk ugly to our admins and then when I get
on the phone, they are sweet and honoring. If a person disrespects those
I have empowered, they in turn disrespect me.\
\
The opposite of honor is dishonor.

Dishonor expresses itself in criticism.

Dishonor and criticism stops our influence.

Dishonor and criticism is the love language of hell. So when we allow
ourselves to dishonor and criticize we are entering a partnership with
the devil. "Where there is strife there is every evil work."

Dishonor and criticism reduces our influence. If we are struggling with
creating influence, we may want to see if there is dishonor in our
lives.

When we criticize a person, they turn down the volume of our voice and
they stop listening to us. This results in a loss of momentum and
reduced influence.

Dishonor and criticism creates distance.

Honor creates access.\
\
In Romans 12:10 we are commanded to "Attempt to outdo one another in
showing honor. " 

KINGDOM FINANCES

The Kingdom of God has an economy all of its own. The Kingdom of God is
not bound by the principles of the kingdom of this earth. The economy of
this earthly kingdom is based on getting.

The economy of the Kingdom of God is not based on getting, it is based
on giving. "Let him that stole steal no more, but rather let him
labor with his hands, that he may have something to GIVE!"

We don't work for a living. If hard work would make you successful and
rich, there would be a lot more rich people in this room.

We work to get our hands on resources so we will have something to give.
Our income is not based on how hard we work, but on how much we give.

The baseline for lining in Kingdom blessing is 10% of our total family
income. (Net vs. Gross)

People say they are givers, but they aren't tithers. You can't give
anything UNTIL you have tithed. Tithing doesn't make one generous, it
makes one compliant.

Malachi say's, "If we do not give God at least 10%, we have ROBBED
God!" (Difference between a thief and a robber -- Aggravated robbery
-- God cannot bless that lifestyle)

Tithing positions us to enter the gateway of Kingdom finances and
Kingdom blessing. To fail to tithe enacts a self- inflicted curse upon
us and our families.

Giving above the tithe is where the life of multiplication is enacted.

Tithing requires no faith -- If I give 1 of 10 I am compliant.

First Fruit is where faith is enacted. What is First Fruit? First Fruit
is tithing on everything that comes into our hands BEFORE we spend any
of the other 90%. First Fruit makes the Kingdom a priority.

Cain & Able -- Cain -- "Offered his sacrifice in the process of
time." Able "Offered the First Fruit to the Lord."

Adventurous giving leads to adventurous living.

Tithing and First Fruit is all about trust. If you can tithe the First
Fruit, you express your trust in God and His Kingdom financial plan. If
you cannot tithe the First Fruit you do not trust God enough to enact
His Kingdom financial plan.

Today is your day to decide to go out on a limb and begin to enact His
Kingdom financial plan, or toe continue operating in this world's system
that tends to produce poverty and a spirit of lack.
