INTRO: (History of this scripture) David is the youngest of the
eight sons of Jesse. Nathan the prophet has already been to Jesse's
house to anoint a King. David has killed a bear and a lion without the
advantage of weapons!

David has three older brothers, Eliab, Abinadab, and Shamma, who are
soldiers in Sauls army. David's brothers were no doubt his hero's! He
probably dreamed of the day that he would grow up to be a brave soldier,
just like them!

The Philistines have attacked Israel and Jesse sends David to the war
zone to check on his brothers.

When David get's to the battlefield he see's Goliath defying the armies
of God! Goliath was 9'9" tall his armor weighed in at nearly 200 lb..

For the first time in his life David see's his brothers for what they
really are! COWARDS!!!

David begins inquiring as to what will happen to the man who kills the
giant! He is told that he will be enriched with great riches, his house
will be forever free in Israel, and he would be allowed to marry the
King's daughter!

David is discouraged by his brothers! (Who did you leave those FEW
SHEEP with?)
