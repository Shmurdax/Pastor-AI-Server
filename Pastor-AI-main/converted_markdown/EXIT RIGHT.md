# EXIT RIGHT

TEXT: Exodus 3:20-24 (NKJV) "I will give this people favor in the
sight of the Egyptians; and it shall be, when you go, that you shall not
go empty-handed. ^22 ^But every woman shall ask of her neighbor, namely,
of her who dwells near her house, articles of silver, articles of gold,
and clothing; and you shall put them on your sons and on your daughters.
So you shall plunder the Egyptians."

INTRO: This passage is the conclusion to the discourse between Moses
and God at the burning bush. This passage marks the call of God on the
life of Moses to provide a strategy to help the Children of Israel to
EXIT Egypt, and to do it the right way!

This is commonly known as the Exodus. In fact, 40 chapters, 1,213
verses, and 32,585 words are committed to allowing the reader to have a
peek into the happenings surrounding the Exodus of the Israelis from the
land of Egypt.

We know that all Scripture has a present meaning, something that is
going to happen right away, and also has an ongoing meaning, that is
apropos for the future life of the believer.

It is important to note that the EXIT or exodus of the Israelis from
Egypt would be a process, because they needed to leave RIGHT for many
reasons. (We will discuss some of them later) (My philosophy about
starting with YES and allowing the process to determine if I made the
right decision)

Susan said yesterday in her sermon. "All transformation requires some
kind of exodus."

Everyone in Project Hope and Saving Grace, theoretically is here to
experience some kind of transformation. Let me assure you, if you
receive your transformation it will require you to make some kind of
EXIT from your past life, and it is imperative that you do that RIGHT!

THE HISTORY OF THE EXIT

- Joseph sold into slavery

- Joseph placed in prison

- Joseph made Prime Minister

- Joseph's family came to Egypt during the famine

- Joseph's family overstayed the will of God, and became slaves

- Joseph's family were slaves for 400 years

- God prepared Moses in the palace (Moses was the only one among Israel
 that didn't have a slave mentality - Because God sheltered him in the
 palace)

- Moses was banished to the wilderness for 40 years (God was teaching
 him to survive the wilderness)

- God called Moses as a deliverer

- The plagues came upon Egypt (Water to blood -- Frogs -- Lice -- Flies
 -- Livestock became sick and died -- Boils -- Hail -- Locusts --
 Darkness -- The first born)

THERE IS PROCESS REQUIRED TO EXIT RIGHT

God had to kill the first born in Egypt to spring the Children of Israel
to freedom! What is God going to have to do to get you truly free?

The blood had to be applied to set them free! You are going to have to
apply the blood to your own life to experience an EXIT! I can't apply
the blood for you, if I could I would have hired a tanker truck full of
it this week, and brought a power nozzle, and none of you would be able
to escape!

After the death of the first born they were finally, free! But were
they? You are clean from drugs, alcohol, and life controlling problems!
But are you truly free?

Exodus 3:20 gives us a glimpse into the way God views our EXIT as
opposed to how we view our EXIT!

The Children of Israel wanted deliverance, but God wanted redemption!
Deliverance can happen in a few minutes; redemption is a process! 5
times God hardened Pharaoh's heart during the ensuing plagues! What? God
hardened Pharaoh's heart? Yes!!!

Pharaoh was ready to release the Israeli's and God hardened his heart 5
times, so that he wouldn't let them go! Moses must have been very
frustrated! Wouldn't we all be frustrated? Moses was doing what God told
him to do, and 5 times God sabotaged him!

We may not understand the process, but I can assure us, there God has
meaning behind the madness of His will!

God did this because he was preparing the hearts of the Egyptian people
to release their material possessions. God was going to reimburse --
Redeem Israel for 400 years of slavery! We have to be very careful not
to short circuit the process, and EXIT wrong instead of RIGHT -- God may
be trying to reimburse us through the process! If we short circuit the
process, we may miss the reimbursement! (Madia)

God told Moses, "When you go you will not go empty!" He also told
them to "Spoil" the Egyptians -- The Hebrew word here is
"Naw-tsal" and means, "To snatch it away from." (Illustrate
this)

When the firstborn died, the next morning the Egyptians welcomed the
Israelis into their homes to take whatever they wanted -- They didn't
get the cheap stuff -- They took enough stuff to build a
multi-million-dollar tabernacle and finance a 45-year-trip through the
wilderness.

God is not just trying to get you out -- He is trying to transform you
and reimburse you in your EXIT! In this season He wants to transform and
reimburse you:

- Spiritually

- Physically

- Emotionally

- Relationally

- Financially

Are you going to short-circuit the process or will you submit to the
EXIT strategy that allows for transformation and reimbursement?

THE MENTALITY OF THE EXIT

Once they left Egypt, these people were no longer geographically in
Egypt. They left and never returned! But they maintained a slave
mentality, which kept them entrapped by the wilderness for 45 years!

It was an 11-day-journey from Egypt to Canaan -- These people turned it
into a 45-year-trip, all because they did not change their thinking!

Not only did it take them 45 years to do what could have been done in 11
days, only 2 people who lived in Egypt ever lived in the promised land!
It is estimated that between 2-3 million people left Egypt, but only 2
of them eventually lived in Canaan Land! Not very good odds.

If we submit to the process we can expect transformation and
reimbursement -- If we don't we can expect relapse and death in the
wilderness.

CONCLUSION:

Some of you have had an EXIT from your old way of doing things but your
way of thinking hasn't changed. Unless you change your mentality, there
is a better than good chance that you will walk off or relapse!

Some of you are one relapse away from a funeral!

It is my prayer that this week will result in a mindset change! All
transformation requires an EXIT! Only a right EXIT will produce
transformation!
