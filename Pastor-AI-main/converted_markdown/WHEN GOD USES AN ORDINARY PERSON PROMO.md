# WHEN GOD USES AN ORDINARY PERSON...

In this series of sermons Pastor Nordin uses Matthew 1:20-21 to explore
the life of Joseph, the earthly father of Jesus. From this ordinary mans
life we learn how to become extraordinary for God. The following
principles served as a guide for the direction of Joseph\'s life:

- Joseph was a man of faith!

- Joseph was a man of prayer!

- Joseph was a man of obedience!

If you have a desire to make your life count for God this series of
messages will no doubt assist you in your pursuit of His purpose!
